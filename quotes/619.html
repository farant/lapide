Louis of Bavaria, captured by Albert, Margrave of Brandenburg, who demanded many things and added threats, said: "Whatever you could obtain from me as a free man, seek in the same manner from me as a captive. If you want anything more, my body is in your power; but my spirit you will find subject to me, not to you."

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XLIX — "Judah the Lion's Cub"
