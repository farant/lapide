His own prerogatives Jesus bestows on others. He is the Light: "You are the light of the world," He says. He is a Priest, and He makes priests. He is a Sheep, and He says: "Behold, I send you as sheep in the midst of wolves." He is a Rock, and He makes a rock. What is His own He bestows on His servants. For Christ is like a fountain perpetually flowing.

— Basil, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter I — "Let There Be Light"
