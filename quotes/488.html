Consider how nowhere are there those superfluous and useless things, nowhere the diabolical pomp, nowhere cymbals and flutes and dances and those satanic banquets and jests full of every obscenity; but all is dignity, all is wisdom, all is goodness.

— St. Chrysostom, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXIV — "The Mantle and the Bridal Veil"
