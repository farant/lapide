When Abba Pastor was asked, "What shall I do to be saved?" he replied: "When Abraham came to the land of promise, he acquired a tomb for himself, and through the sepulchre he received the land as an inheritance." And the brother said: "What is a sepulchre?" The elder replied: "A place of weeping and mourning."

— Abba Pastor, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXIII — "The Right of Burial"
