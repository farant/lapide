We read the Prophetic and Apostolic books for the commemoration of our faith, the consolation of our hope, and the exhortation of our charity, harmonizing with each other's voices; and with that harmony, as with a heavenly trumpet, both rousing us from the torpor of mortal life and stretching us toward the prize of the heavenly calling.

— Augustine, in Cornelius a Lapide, Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture — "The Old Testament Surpasses the New in Allegorical Richness"
