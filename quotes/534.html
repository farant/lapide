When injury is done to us, and we are meek, gentle, and patient, we enjoy greater and more abundant divine help. Therefore let us not struggle against those who oppress us and wish to slander us; but let us bear it nobly, knowing that the Lord of all will not despise us, provided we acknowledge His benevolence. For "Vengeance is Mine," He says, "and I will repay."

— St. John Chrysostom, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXXI — "See All the Spotted Males"
