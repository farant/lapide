Thus Macarius dwelt in the heavens with God, and used to say to himself: "You have Angels, Archangels, all the heavenly powers, Cherubim and Seraphim, God the maker of all these; dwell there, do not descend below the heavens, do not fall into worldly thoughts."

— St. Macarius, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter V — "Enoch Walked with God"
