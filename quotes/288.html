Adam did not keep watch to guard against the serpent's malice. He was simple; he was not shrewd against the devil. For he agreed with the devil who persuaded rather than with the Lord who threatened, and he lost the life he had, and received the death he did not know.

— Chrysostom, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter II — "Of the Tree of Knowledge Thou Shalt Not Eat"
