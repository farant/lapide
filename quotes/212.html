Silver is the brightness of speech or of wisdom; the veins are Sacred Scripture, as if to say plainly: He who prepares himself for the words of true preaching must draw the origins of his arguments from the sacred pages; so that he may trace back everything he says to the foundation of divine authority, and build the edifice of his speech firmly upon it.

— Gregory, in Cornelius a Lapide, Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture — Chapter I: "On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture"
