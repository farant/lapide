The cross of Christ is the key of paradise: it is the staff of the infirm, the rod of pastors, the guidance of those converting, the perfection of those advancing, the salvation of soul and body, the averting of all evils, the giver of all good things.

— St. John Damascene, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Exodus, Chapter XV — "He Showed Him a Piece of Wood"
