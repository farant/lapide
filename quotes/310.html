Today the Virgin ascends — not as smoke, but as light; not as a pillar of the desert, but as the queen of heaven. Today the Virgin is assumed — body and soul — into the glory of heaven. Today the Virgin is exalted above all the choirs of angels, and placed at the right hand of her Son. Today the Virgin enters into the joy of her Lord — the joy that was prepared for her from the beginning of the world.

— Adam of Perseigne, Mariale — Sermon V: On the Assumption
