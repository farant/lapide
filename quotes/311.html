He chose you as the way by which He would come to us; He ordained you as the path by which we would return home. O way free from roughness and harshness, free from ignorance and error! O common way of salvation, free from iniquity and malice, free from trouble and toil. You are wholly pleasant and clear, wholly sweet and straight, gentle and peaceful — you who bring to us the illumination of our hearts from the heart of the Father.

— Adam of Perseigne, Mariale — Fragment I
