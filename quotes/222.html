This true and genuine wisdom is not taught by reading, but by anointing; not by the letter, but by the spirit; not by erudition, but by practice in the commandments of the Lord. You are mistaken, you are mistaken, if you think to find among the masters of the world what only the disciples of Christ, that is, the despisers of the world, attain by the gift of God.

— Bernard, in Cornelius a Lapide, Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture — Chapter V: "On the Dispositions Required for This Study"
