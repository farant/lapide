God is what He is, that is, He is His own being and the being of all other things. If you call Him good, if great, if wise, in this word it is summed up: He Who Is. For this is for Him to be: to be all these things. If you say more, you have added nothing; if you have not said it, you have diminished nothing.

— St. Bernard, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Exodus, Chapter III — "I Am Who I Am"
