The eyes of the patriarch grow dim, the palate is deceived, the hand is deceived, the ear is not deceived. What wonder, if the ear perceives the truth, since faith comes from hearing, hearing through the word of God, and the word of God is truth? There is no truth in the eye, no wisdom; only hearing has the truth, which perceives the word.

— St. Bernard, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXVII — "Verse 22"
