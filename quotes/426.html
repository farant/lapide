We who remember that our mother is the Jerusalem which is above, we assert that those are the fathers who preceded us in merit of life and in order. There was Abel, the pious victim; there was the pious and holy Enoch; there was Noah: it is to these that the passage of Abraham is promised.

— St. Ambrose, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XV — "You Shall Go to Your Fathers in Peace"
