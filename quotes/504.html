If sickness befalls us, let us not be saddened, because it profits us for the destruction of bodily desires. If we lose our eyes, let us not bear it heavily, because we have lost the instrument of pride. If we have become deaf, let us not grieve, because we have lost vain hearing.

— St. Syncletica, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXVII — "Verse 1"
