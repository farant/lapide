You are the rich clusters from the vineyard of the Lord, and grapes already ripe in fruit, trampled by the assault of worldly oppression; you feel our winepress. In place of wine you pour forth blood; strong for the endurance of suffering, you willingly drain the cup of martyrdom.

— St. Cyprian, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Exodus, Chapter IV — "You Are a Bridegroom of Blood to Me"
