I left you in one state, O Adam, and find you in another. I had clothed you with glory; you walked gloriously before Me; now I see you naked and seeking hiding places. Where now are those magnificent promises of the serpent? Where is that first tranquility of your mind? Where the security of spirit? Where the peace and confidence of conscience? Where that entire possession of so many goods, and freedom from all evils?

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter III — "Where Are You?"
