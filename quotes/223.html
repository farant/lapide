Wondrous is the depth of Your words, Lord, whose surface, behold, is before us, charming to little ones, but wondrous is the depth, my God, wondrous depth; it is dreadful to gaze into it: a dread of honor, and a trembling of love.

— Augustine, in Cornelius a Lapide, Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture — "The Fathers' Defense of the Old Testament"
