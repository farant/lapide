Do you not know that even if you drive him into the most desolate wilderness, he will still have a powerful Lord who knows how to help him and make him far more illustrious? For nothing is stronger than to enjoy heavenly protection, just as nothing is weaker than to be deprived of it.

— St. Chrysostom, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXVI — "Depart from Us"
