When asked how he had lived so peacefully in the monastery and had learned to maintain silence and patience in every tribulation, Nesteros replied: "When I first entered the community, I said to my soul: You and the donkey are to be one. For just as a donkey is beaten and does not speak, endures injury and does not respond, so also you."

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XLIX — "The Prophecy and Blessing of Issachar"
