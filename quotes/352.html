No desire for any other thing rose in my heart except for God. God hid nothing of earthly things from me; I did not sleep during the day, nor rest at night, seeking God; I received every petition from God immediately. I often saw myriads attending upon God; I saw the choirs of the just. I saw the multitude of Martyrs; I saw the rule of life of monks; and the work of all praised God. I saw the just rejoicing for eternity.

— Anuph, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter V — "Enoch Walked with God"
