Does simplicity of character please you? Let Isaac come to mind, whom the tranquility of his life adorned in the eyes of Almighty God. Thus David avoided Saul who was pursuing him by flight; and when he could have harmed him, he would not: and so by David's kindness the envy of Saul was overcome. Thus Solomon was peaceable, and therefore deserved to build a temple for the Lord.

— St. Gregory, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXVI — "Nor Have We Done Anything That Would Harm You"
