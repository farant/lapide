O, what an ocean of divine goodness! "I cannot do anything until you enter there." But the sins of Sodom are exceedingly great. "I cannot do anything." But the crimes of the shameless are fulfilled. "I cannot do anything." But they cry out to heaven. "I cannot do anything" until Lot takes himself to the mountain.

— Prado, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XIX — "Until You Enter There"
