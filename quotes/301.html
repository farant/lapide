Your piety is infinite, your power is infinite. The outpouring of your magnificent liberality can not only never exhaust your treasures of glory, it cannot even diminish them. Give what you will — you have no less. You can enrich the poverty of each individual, yet your inexhaustible stores endure. For your fullness is He Who, since He is boundless, receives no increase; since He is simple, sustains no loss; and since He is eternal, knows no end.

— Adam of Perseigne, Mariale — Sermon I: On the Annunciation
