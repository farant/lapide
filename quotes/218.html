I entered the monastery at the age of seven, and there I devoted all my effort to meditating on the Scriptures for my whole life, and amid the observance of regular discipline and the daily care of singing in the church, I always found it sweet either to learn, or to teach, or to write.

— Bede, in Cornelius a Lapide, Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture — Chapter IV: "The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers"
