Six thousand years of our world have not yet been completed; and how many previous eternities, how many ages, how many origins of centuries must we suppose there were, in which the Angels, Thrones, Dominions, and all the other Virtues served God, and at God's command stood without the vicissitudes and measures of time?

— Jerome, in Pianciani, Appendix: On the Creation of Angels — "Angels Exist and Are Not Eternal"
