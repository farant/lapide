Let us follow the ways of St. Jacob, that we may arrive at those sufferings, at those encounters; let us arrive at patience, the mother of the faithful, and at the father Isaac, that is, one capable of joy, overflowing with gladness; for where there is patience, there is gladness.

— St. Ambrose, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXXV — "The City of Arbee"
