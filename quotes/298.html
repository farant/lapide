Mary, Mother of God and man, was ever an Anchor in tumult, a Harbor in shipwreck, a Support in tribulation, a Consolation in sorrow, a Help in oppression, an Aid in time of need, a Moderating force in prosperity, a Joy in expectation, a Refreshment in labor. To seek Mary, Queen of heaven, is Virtue; to find Her, Salvation; to lay hold of Her, Joy; to love Her, Praise and Honor; to hold Her fast and never let Her go, the highest Good.

— Adam of Perseigne, Mariale — Title Page
