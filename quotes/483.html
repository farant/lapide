Often, the allurement of a woman has deceived even the strongest husbands, and has made them depart from their religion. Therefore religion is sought first in marriage. Learn then what is sought in a wife: Abraham did not seek gold, nor silver, nor possessions, but the grace of a good character.

— St. Ambrose, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXIV — "Not from the Daughters of the Canaanites"
