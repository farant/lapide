The tradition of the Hebrews is that Abram was literally cast into fire by the Chaldeans, but was miraculously freed from it by God. The Church prays for the dying that God may free them from the anguish of death and from the fire of Gehenna, just as He freed Abraham from Ur, that is, from the fire of the Chaldeans.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XI — "Ur of the Chaldeans"
