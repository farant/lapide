Whenever God shall will it, even if we are in the wilderness and in the extremity of afflictions, and have no hope of salvation, we shall have need of nothing else, with divine grace supplying us all things. For if we have obtained His grace, no one shall prevail against us, but we shall be more powerful than all.

— St. John Chrysostom, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXI — "And She Wept"
