Saladin, king of Egypt and Syria, who took the Holy Land from the Christians, when about to die, ordered a standard with a funeral cloth to be carried through all his camps, and a herald to proclaim: "This is all that Saladin, ruler of Syria and Egypt, from all his empire, will now carry with him."

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter II — "Of the Tree of Knowledge Thou Shalt Not Eat"
