What do you do against temptations of the flesh? Juniper said: When I feel such thoughts, I immediately say: Away, away, for the inn is taken. To whom Giles said: I agree with you; for it is best to flee: for chastity is a clear mirror, which is obscured by a mere look and breath.

— Bl. Giles of Assisi, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XXXIV — "Moral Lesson on Flight from Men"
