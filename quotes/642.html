O wicked age, which is accustomed to bless only its own friends in such a way as to make them enemies of God! Haman found favor with King Ahasuerus; this favor produced for him a cross: Ahithophel found favor with Absalom; this brought him the gallows.

— St. Bernard, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Exodus, Chapter I — "A New King Arose"
