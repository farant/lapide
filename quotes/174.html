How greatly and how vehemently Jeremiah rebuked sinners and the wicked! Yet he was one among them, and when he entered the temple, he celebrated the same sacraments, he lived in the same congregation of wicked men, but by crying out he went out from there. This therefore is what it means to go out from there, this is not to touch the unclean thing: not to consent to their will, and not to spare with the mouth.

— Augustine, in Bonacursus Catholicus, The Life of the Heretics — Against the Arnaldists
