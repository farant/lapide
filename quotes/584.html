Even though I am not Joseph, I would nonetheless proclaim that those fat cows signified not only wantonness but also carelessness toward divine reverence. For of the wicked it is said: "Fat bulls have besieged me." And therefore that dream of worldly abundance could not be perpetual; but there would come a time when a harsh famine would succeed these things.

— St. Ambrose, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XLI — "Verse 4"
