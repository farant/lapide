Who are You, O Lord, and who am I? Let me know You, let me know myself. You are the abyss of wisdom, goodness, power, virtue, of all good, and of all being; I am the abyss of ignorance, malice, weakness, vices, and nothingness.

— St. Francis, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Exodus, Chapter III — "The Lord Appeared to Him in a Flame of Fire"
