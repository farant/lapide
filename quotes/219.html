You should fortify no other road to grasping and obtaining truth and sacred wisdom, than that which has been fortified by Him who, as God, sees the weakness of our steps. For the first thing is humility, the second is humility, the third is humility; and as often as you ask me, I would say the same thing.

— Augustine, in Cornelius a Lapide, Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture — Chapter V: "On the Dispositions Required for This Study"
