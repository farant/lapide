What was lacking to him, to whom God was present? Who was so powerful in his own home, as this man in a foreign one? Who was so abundant in prosperity, as this man in famine? Who was so strong in youth, as this man in old age? Who was so active in business, as this man in leisure? Who was so happy in the flower of adolescence, as this man on the threshold of death? Who was so rich in a kingdom, as this man in a foreign place?

— St. Ambrose, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XLVI — "Verse 4"
