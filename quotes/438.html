You love riches — you are not satisfied, because they are not Shaddai. You love honors — you are not fulfilled, because they are not Shaddai. O human heart — why do you run in vain after empty, trifling, brief, and deceptive goods? They cannot satisfy the hunger and thirst of your soul. Love your Shaddai: He alone can fill all the recesses of your soul.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Genesis, Chapter XVII — "El Shaddai"
