And if we cannot follow our leaders to the heights of divine contemplation, let us take anxious care that no temptation draw us away from the vicinity of the mountain of God; but according to our own measure let us persist in works of virtue, clinging with steadfast heart to the sacraments of our Redeemer with which we have been imbued, and taking care to preserve undefiled in ourselves the grace of His Spirit with which we have been sealed.

— Bede the Venerable, On the Tabernacle, Book I, Chapter I — "Moses Ascending the Mountain"
