The exemplary life of this man made the lives of many exemplary. His devotion instilled religion in those who lacked it, and increased it in those who had it. He watched over the salvation of others as over his own; he rejoiced in the correction of others as in his own. If anyone was converted, it was his conversion; if anyone did good works, it was his work.

— Deeds of the Bishops of Verdun — Continuation: Abbot Richard
