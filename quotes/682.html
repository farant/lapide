From our homeland we departed by being proud, by disobeying, by following visible things, by tasting forbidden food; but to it we must return by weeping, by obeying, by despising visible things, and by restraining the appetite of the flesh. Therefore by another way we return to our homeland.

— St. Gregory, in Cornelius a Lapide, Commentary on Exodus, Chapter XII — "For It Is the Phase, the Passover of the Lord"
