What is the logic that Priscian should be heeded, and Truth, which is God, should be despised? For if Terence, Vergil, Cicero, and other followers of liberal letters earned this in their writings — that their sayings should remain unshaken — how much more should the oracles of heavenly words persevere in their own rule!

— Berno of Reichenau, On the Various Modulation of Psalms and Chants — Section 8
