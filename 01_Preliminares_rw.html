KU MUKURU W'ICYUBAHIRO N'ICYAMAMARE CYINSHI
NYAKUBAHWA
HENRI FRANCOIS VAN DER BURCH,
UMWEPISKOPI MUKURU KANDI UMUDUKA WA CAMBRAI,
UMUTWARE W'INGOMA NTAGATIFU Y'ABAROMA, KONTE WA CAMBRAI.

Byagenze neza, Imana ibigennye ityo, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, ko mu gihe ubwo nyine wari utangizwa nk'Umwepiskopi Mukuru n'Umutware w'Ingoma Ntagatifu y'Abaroma i Cambrai, uyu Mose wanjye — wagukuriwe kuva akiri agashya kawe ka mbere kandi ugukwiriyeho ku mpamvu nyinshi — yasohorwaga.

Bose bazi ukuntu ubumwe bw'imitima yacu bwagumye imyaka myinshi, isimano yabyaye igushimisha ku miterere, ibyiyumvo bimwe n'inyigisho zimwe, ubumenyane bukabyongera, ubuntu bw'Imana bukabisimbuza kandi bukabisohoza mu buzima bwacu bombi busa cyane. Ni yo mpamvu, naguhamagariwe i Mechlin kujya ku Kiliziya Nkuru wari Umukuru w'Abadekani — nk'umwumvizi w'ibyaha ku minsi mikuru y'umwaka — nakoresheje neza kandi mu bwigenge ikiruhuko n'imeza yawe mu myaka myinshi, kugeza igihe Umuryango wacu washyiraga muri uwo mugi Icyiciro cy'abashya n'Ishuri.

Ariko ibyo Mutagatifu Yohani Batisita yavuze kuri Kristu — «Ni we ugomba kwiyongera, naho jye nkagabanuka» — ibyo navugiye kera ku Bwicyubahiro bwawe n'ubwanjye, nubwo ntari umuhanuzi; kandi twese tubona ko byashohojwe, kandi biraturejeje.

Koko rero, uyu Mose wanjye yabera nde neza kuruta Ubwicyubahiro bwawe Bukuru, wowe uyobora ubwoko bw'Imana nk'Umuduka w'idini n'uwa politiki, nk'Umwepiskopi n'Umutware icyarimwe — nk'uko Mose yashyizeho, yayoboye kandi akagira inama Kiliziya y'Abaheburayo nk'uko yabigiraga n'Igihugu cyabo, akabayobora abakuye muri Misiri anyuze mu mashyamba y'ubutayu n'abanzi badabarika, batsinzwe kandi batsinda, bagana igihugu cy'isezerano? Kuko yashyizeho kandi akayobora Kiliziya akoresheje amategeko y'imigirire y'idini y'Amategeko Cumi yakiriye ku Mana, Igihugu akoresheje amategeko y'imanza, kandi yombi akoresheje amategeko y'imico. Muri Mose rero, nk'uko byari muri Melkisedeki, Aburahamu, Isaka, Yakobo, n'abandi ba sekuruza ba kera, ubuyobozi bubiri bw'ikirenga — ubw'umutware n'ubw'umupadiri — bwari buhujwe, ku buryo yagenzuraga ibya Leta nk'umutware, n'ibitagatifu nk'umupadiri, umutambyi mukuru kandi umuyobozi w'idini; kugeza igihe yimuriye kimwe mu byo bikorwa, ari bwo ubusaserdoti, muri murumuna we Aroni, akamwimika Umutambyi Mukuru. Mose ni yo mpamvu yari umushumba — wa mbere w'intama, hanyuma w'abantu, abo yarokoye Farawo akoresheje inkoni ye y'ubushumba, igikoresho cy'ibitangaza byinshi, kandi akabayobora n'amategeko atagatifu cyane y'ubwami n'uby'idini; kuko umwami n'umutware nk'uko bimeze ku mupadiri n'umutambyi mukuru agomba kuba umushumba.

Homeri yita umwami umushumba w'amahanga, kuko agomba kuyagaburira, nk'uko umushumba agaburira intama, aho kubahumbya.

Nuko rero, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, ube Mose wacu w'Ibihugu by'Epfo; reba uyu Mose wacu, kandi, nk'uko usanzwe ubigira, ujye umushushanya mu buzima bwawe cyane cyane — ni ko uzayobora ubwoko bw'Imana utari mu gihugu cy'Abanyakanani cyasezeranyijwe Abayuda, ahubwo mu gihugu cy'abazima n'abatsinda mu ijuru; mbese uzabagezayo byuzuye, ibyo Mose ubwe yadashoboye.

Mutagatifu Bazili Mukuru yari Mose w'igihe cye, nk'uko umugenzi we Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi abivuga mu Ijambo ry'Icyubahiro kuri Mutagatifu Bazili, kandi yize gukora nka Mose abyigiye kuri Mose ubwe. Mutagatifu Bazili ubwe abyemera mu rwandiko rwa 140 rwandikiwe Libaniyusi Umusofi: «Twebwe mu by'ukuri, umugabo ukomeye, tuganira na Mose na Eliya n'abandi bantu b'umugisha bameze batyo, batubwira inyigisho zabo mu rurimi rw'amahanga; kandi ibyo twumvise kuri bo turabivuga — ari ukuri mu by'ibisobanuro, nubwo ari ibikaze mu magambo.» Ukuntu Mutagatifu Bazili yigiye Mose we biragaragazwa n'ibitabo by'Amezi Atandatu gusa — bya bikorwa yanditse abihataniye cyane nk'ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro ya Mose, ku buryo Mutagatifu Amburwazi yabihinduye akabigeza ku matwi y'Abalatinya atari igitekerezo cye bwite ahubwo igitekerezo cya Mutagatifu Bazili, mu gitabo cye Ku Mirimo y'Iminsi Itandatu.

Rufinusi ahamya ko Mutagatifu Bazili na Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi, bamaze kwiga ubuhanga bwo kuvuga n'ubuhanga bw'isi i Ateni, bashyize imyaka cumi n'itatu mu gusoma no kuzirikana Mose n'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu. Bose bazi, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, ukuntu ukunda Mose n'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu, ukuntu ubyishimira, iyo imirimo ibikwemereye, kujya ubisoma, ubisuzuma kandi ubitotonye. Uribuka ukuntu ikiganiro cyacu ku meza, iyo nari umushyitsi w'ikiruhuko cyawe, cyahuzuraga kubiganiraho; uribuka ko ku ifunguro rimwe twasomaga hamwe ibice cumi cyangwa cumi na bibiri by'Intangiriro, nawe ukanshyiriraho ibibazo byinshi bigoye birebana nabyo, nanjye nkabisubiza ako kanya uko uburyo n'ibuka byemereraga — ariko muri iki gitabo uzabibona biva ku ntangiriro, bisuzumwa neza, bisobanurwa byuzuye kandi bikurikiranywa mu mwanzuro umwe.

Mose yavutse mu muryango w'icyubahiro w'Abakurambere, kandi yari umuzukuruza wa Aburahamu. Kuko Aburahamu yabyaye Isaka, Isaka abyara Yakobo, Yakobo Levi, Levi Kaati, Kaati Amuramu, Amuramu na we Mose.

Mutagatifu Bazili na we yaturutse ku babyeyi b'icyubahiro ku bw'ukwizera ntarengwa n'ubwoko — Bazili na Emeliya — kandi nyina yakurikiye umuhungu we igihe yigunze mu butayu. Imilyango yawe, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, igaragara ku bw'ingeso nk'uko bimeze ku bw'amaraso, iyubahwa n'abaturage bawe. Sekuru yawe yari Perezida w'Inama y'i Flandre, warangije iryo banga abishimirwa cyane n'Igihugu. So wawe, umugabo w'ubwenge bw'ikirenga n'ubuhanga, yabaye Perezida wa mbere w'Inteko Nkuru y'i Mechlin, hanyuma y'Inama Ntimukanwa; yahagaraye adashobora guhinduka mu budahemuka ku Mutware we mu mitingito n'imivurungano y'ibitangaje kandi bikomeye by'ibi Bihugu by'Epfo, kandi ku bw'iyo mpamvu yakuriwe cyane n'Umwami Gatolika Filipo wa II, w'icyubahiro cy'urwibutso. Kandi nubwo yarangije ayo mabanga akomeye cyane mu myaka myinshi, ubwo yashoboraga guhuriza ubukungu bwinshi, ntiyongereye ubutunzi bw'umuryango, ahora yitaye ku nyungu rusange, ku buryo yabonekaga atitaye ku byo mu rugo rwe bwite.

Ibyo ni byo n'umuyobozi w'icyubahiro w'Ubwongereza n'umumaritiri, Intahemuka Tomasi Mori, yagezeho, wamaze imyaka mirongo itanu mu buzima bwa Leta afite imyanya y'ikirenga, ariko adashobora kongerera amafaranga ye y'umwaka kugeza ku mirongo irindwi y'izahabu. Ahubwo, so wawe yagabanije ibintu bye bwite kandi ahabwa ibyago bikomeye by'ubutunzi kubera ko yagumye ari indahemuka mu budahemuka ku Mutware we. Kuko mu mwaka wa 1572, igihe abanyamadini y'ikinyoma bafataga Mechlin bitunguranye, yajugunywe mu buroko bw'agasuzuguro, akababazwa n'ibyago byinshi, kandi akemera no gutakaza ubutunzi bwinshi; kandi iyo Umuduka w'i Alba aza atazanye ingabo ze buno nyine, yari amaze kugenerwa urupfu. Hanyuma mu mwaka wa 1580, igihe uwo mugi wongera gufatwa n'abanyamadini y'ikinyoma, inzu ye yongeye gusahurwa, ibintu bye byose bitwaliwe, ikindi kandi yagombye kwishyura ibihumbi n'ibihumbi by'amafaranga kugira ngo acungure umugore we, utashoboye kwikiza mu guhungira.

Mose ntiyadukiye ubuyobozi ako kanya, ahubwo yazamutse intambwe ku yindi. Mu myaka ya mbere mirongo ine yigishijwe mu ngoro ya Farawo ubuhanga bwose bw'Abanyamisiri, ariga gukora n'abakuru. Mu myaka mirongo ine ya kabiri, areze intama, yiyeguriye kuzirikana; hanyuma afite imyaka mirongo inani, yitwaje ubushumba n'ubuyobozi bw'ubwoko. Mutagatifu Bazili na we yabikoze, uwo Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi avugaho ati: «Amaze gusoma mbere ibitabo bitagatifu no kuba umubisesengura, yimiwe ubusaserdoti na Erumojeni, Umwepiskopi wa Sezareya,» n'ibindi.

Mu buryo bumwe, Mutagatifu Sipriyani ashima Mutagatifu Korneli, Umwepiskopi wa Roma, mu gitabo cya IV, urwandiko rwa 2 rwandikiwe Antoniyani: «Uyu muntu (Korneli), ni ko avuga, ntiyageze ku bwepiskopi buno nyine, ahubwo amaze kuzamurwa mu nzego zose za Kiliziya, kandi amaze kuboneka kenshi imbere y'Uhoraho mu bikorwa bitagatifu, yazamutse ku mpinga y'ikirenga y'ubusaserdoti anyuze mu ntambwe zose z'ubuzima bw'idini. Hanyuma ntiyisabye ubwepiskopi ubwe, ntiyabushaka, kandi ntiyabunyaze nk'uko abandi bakora, abo kwikuza n'ubwibone byababaza; ahubwo yari atekanye kandi yicisha bugufi, kandi nk'uko bameze abasanzwe batorwa n'Imana kuri iyi myanya, kubera isoni z'umutimanama we utagira icyaha, no kubera kwicisha bugufi kw'isoni yavukana kandi yitagatifuza, ntiyakoze nk'abandi ngo yikubite ku bwepiskopi, ahubwo we ubwe yababajwe ngo yemere ubwepiskopi.»

Mbese si ayo magambo nyine ashushanya Korneli Mutagatifu Sipriyani akwishushanyaho nawe, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, n'imico yawe itagira inenge? Wazamutse intambwe ku yindi kugera ku mpinga y'ubusaserdoti. Wa mbere warangije imirimo yo kuba kanonika n'umupadiri — utari mu bwishuke n'ubunebwe, ahubwo uha umuryango wawe imyigishirize y'idini, wiyeguriye kumva amabanga, wizihiza imyigishirize, ukurikirana amasengesho ya buri gihe, ufasha abishonje mu nama nk'uko wabigiraga mu gufasha, ukaguma mu mirimo y'umutima mwiza n'imbabazi. Ubwo buzima bw'inzirakarengane n'ubudahumanye, bwuzuyemo urukundo n'ishyaka nk'uko bwuzuye ingeso nziza, bwakweretse amajwi y'abose, ku buryo bagutoye ngo ube Umukuru w'Abadekani b'Ikiliziya Nkuru y'i Mechlin — kandi icyo wagezeho muri ubwo bwiru, abaririmbyi n'abagayita b'i Mechlin, ari bo ndorerwamo y'ingeso nziza n'idini mu Bihugu by'Epfo byose, baracyabivuga nta jambo ryanjye. Bidatinze watorewe kuba Umuvikari Mukuru n'Umwepiskopi Mukuru w'Icyubahiro w'i Mechlin; muri ubwo bwiru wagenzuye kandi uyobora ibikorwa byose bya Kiliziya, mu budahemuka, umwete, ubuntu n'ubuhanga, ku buryo aho hose wasubije, warushijeho kandi usimbuza disipuline ya Kiliziya — umwigishwa ukwiriye umwigisha akomeye nk'uwo. Kandi muri ibyo byari igitangaje cyane ko warangije izo nzego zombi neza cyane ku buryo abaririmbyi ntibigeze bakumbuza Umukuru w'Abadekani babo, kandi diyosezi na yo ntiyigeze ikumbura Umuvikari wayo. Wahora uri uwa mbere mu kuririmba, ndetse no mu gihe cy'imbeho ikaze, mu bukonje bukaze kuruta, ndetse niyo wabaga ugarutse i muhira unaniwe uvuye mu gusura abapadiri, udaha umubiri ikiruhuko. Iyo ntambwe yakuguye ku bwepiskopi bw'i Gand na Arkiduka wacu w'Icyubahiro, ukoresha ubwenge n'ubushishozi butandukanye mu gutoranya abayobozi b'idini, adaha ikintu icyifuzo cyangwa amaraso, ahubwo byose abiha ingeso nziza — muri ubwo bwiru rero wiyerekeje imbere ye no mu Bihugu by'Epfo byose ku buryo ubu utari gusa utumiriwe ku bwepiskopi bukuru, ahubwo ukaba wahatiwe kubuha.

Mose, ahamagariwe n'Imana kwitwaza ubuyobozi ubwa gatatu n'ubwa kane, yanze, yirinda kugeza ubwo yababaza Imana, yanga icyubahiro n'umutwaro icyarimwe. Mu Iyimukamisiri iv agira ati: «Ndagusaba, Nyagasani, sinzi kuvuga neza, si ejo si ejobundi kandi si kuva wabwiye umugaragu wawe; ahubwo mfite ururimi rutinda kandi rwibinda: ndagusaba, Nyagasani, ohereza uwo uzoherereza.» Mutagatifu Bazili na we yahunga ubwepiskopi bwa Neyo-Sezareya, nk'uko we ubwe abivuga mu rwandiko rwa 164. Mu buryo bumwe, amaze guhagarara ku nshuti ye Euzebiyusi, Umwepiskopi wa Sezareya, mu ndwara ye kugeza ku rupfu, Euzebiyusi amaze gupfa, Bazili yahise yihisha; abamubonyeho yigana uburwayi; kandi asumbirwa gusa, arwanya cyane, yabaye umwepiskopi.

Igihe wari Umuvikari, washakaga gukuraho umutwaro, kwikura no kubaho kuri wowe n'Imana; kandi wari kubigeza ku iherezo, iyo Padiri wacu w'Icyubahiro Mukuru wa Porovense — wabaye umwigisha wawe muri Filozofiya — atakugarura ku gitekerezo cyawe kandi akakwemeza gusubira kwicurika ku mutwaro w'idini.

Ikindi kandi, igihe Arkiduka w'Icyubahiro Mukuru yatekereza kukwimurira mu bwepiskopi bw'i Gand kandi akaba yarakutoye ngo ube Umwepiskopi Mukuru wa Cambrai, Mana yanjye! ukuntu wabyumvise nabi, igihe kingana ukibyanga, inzira zingahe z'uguhunga washakishije — kandi gusa igihe wahatiwe n'amasaba adacogora y'abantu benshi, n'imbabazi n'inshuro amaherezo, kugira ngo ureke kugaragara nk'urwanya Imana yaguhagarariye mu bimenyetso byinshi bityo, ni bwo wemeye utabipfuza ubwo bwiru.

Ibyo ni byo byakozwe mu kinyejana kibanziriza, isi ibibonye ishima, na Yohani Fisher, Umwepiskopi wa Rochester, umumaritiri w'icyubahiro w'Ubwongereza, wahawe ubwepiskopi bwa Rochester kubera ubuhanga bwe butagereranywa n'ubuzima bwe budasobanutse. Kandi igihe iryo banga ryaje kugaragara ko ari rito cyane ku iziranenge ry'umugabo akomeye nk'uwo, kandi Henri wa VIII akifuza kumuzamura ku kintu gikuru kurushaho, ntiyigeze ashobora kubemezwa gusiga umugore we — yari umutoya mu by'ukuri, ariko wa mbere bitewe n'itangira ry'Imana, kandi yamwitayeho nk'uko ashoboye mu myaka myinshi y'imirimo ye — mu buryo ari bwo bwose bwo kumusimbuza intebe y'ubutunzi. Yongereyeho ati: «azaba ahirwa cyane niba ashobora gusa gutanga ikibazo cyiza ku Munsi w'Uhoraho ku bw'iri tongo rito ryamugenewe, no ku bw'inyungu zidashimishije cyane zakiriye; kuko icyo gihe hazabazwa ikibazo kirushijeho ku bw'ubugingo bwitabweho neza no ku bw'amafaranga yakoreshejwe neza, kuruta uko abantu basanzwe batekereza cyangwa bitaho.»

Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu biha Mose iri shangwe: ko yari umunyakamuhaguruka w'abantu bose. Mutagatifu Bazili, Mose w'Abakristu, yatsinze abanzi be mu bwuzu budahwema, nk'uko Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi abivuga kuri we.

Ineza yawe, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, itangarirwa n'abose — ineza ukoresha mu kwakira bose neza, mu kubaramutsa mu cyubahiro, no mu kubagaragariza bose amaso ashimishije, ijambo rikwiriye n'umutima w'ingabire. Ubwo ni bwo buryo wakweretse imitima y'abaturage b'i Gand ngo bakwifuze, wagakuye ibishisha, usubiza disipuline ya Kiliziya, ugira neza cyangwa ukura abapadiri b'ubuzima bw'ubusembwa, ku buryo ubukayagirire bushya — mbese ikuzo — buramurikira mu Bubiligi bwose nk'icyerekezo gishya giturutse ku Kiliziya y'i Gand. Kuko nk'uko Ubibiligi ari imbuto y'isi, niko Gand ari imbuto ya Flandre n'Ubibiligi, izwi ku bw'uko ari aho Karoli wa V, Umwami Utsinzwe wavukiye. Ni byo byavuzwe mu ijwi ryo hasi n'abaturage igihe wanyuraga mu mihanda: «Dore, umumalayika aranyura. Dore, umumalayika wacu.» Iryo teganyirizo ry'ubwenge ry'Imana riganza isi yose mu buryo bw'Imana, nk'uko Umuhanga abyemeza, «rigera kuva ku mpera imwe kugeza ku yindi rifite ububasha, rikayobora ibintu byose mu bugwaneza.» Iri teganyirizo urishushanya: mu bugwaneza uroroshya kandi winjira mu bigoye, mu mbaraga ubitsinda. Nuko rero icyo ugennye cyose mu mutima wawe, ukigira neza kandi ukigeraho. Ni uko rero kimwe cyiza ibikwerekeye: Mu bugwaneza no mu mbaraga.

Mose yakundiye ubwoko bwe bw'umutima ukomeye urukundo rwa nyina, kandi yarukunze cyane ku buryo yasabye kuvanywa mu gitabo cy'ubuzima. Ni yo mpamvu, nk'umurerezi, yagaburiye ubwo bwoko mu myaka mirongo ine mu butayu ukoresheje umugati uva mu ijuru — ari yo manu; kandi yiyihanganiye cyane kwaka imitima yabo mu gutinya no gukunda Imana, nk'uko bigaragara mu Ivugururamategeko ryose. Rufinusi arongorera ku ishyaka n'ineza ya Mutagatifu Bazili ku bwoko bwe, Igitabo cya II, igice cya ix: «Bazili, ni ko avuga, anyuze mu migi n'imirima bya Pontu, yatangiye gukangura amagambo ye imitima ibogoboga y'ubwo bwoko — butagira impungenge ku byiringiro by'ejo hazaza — no kubacana ubucanyi bwe, no kubakuraho umusinga w'ubunebwe bw'igihe kirekire. Yabayoboreye, baretse amaganya y'ubusa n'iby'isi, kwimenya, guterana hamwe, kubaka ibisaho by'abihaye Imana; yabigishije kwiyegurira indirimbo n'amasengesho, kwitaho abakene, gushinga amazu y'idini y'igikundiro, no gutuma ubuzima bw'ubuziranenge n'ubudahumanye bufuzwa na hafi bose. Nuko mu gihe gito isura y'intara yose yarahinduwe.»

Mutagatifu Bazili aribuka, Mutagatifu Efremu yabonye inuma imubwira inyigisho mu gutwi — inuma, ndavuga, ikaba ikimenyetso n'ishusho ya Roho Mutagatifu, nk'uko Gregori wa Nisa abyemeza. Tekereza rero uko inyigisho ye yari imeze, kandi ukuntu yari y'ishyaka n'umuhate! Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi ahamya ko inzara rusange yarokowe n'umuhate wa Mutagatifu Bazili: «Yabagaburiye bose, ni ko avuga, ariko mu buhe buryo? Umva. Mu gufungura ibigega by'abakire mu ijambo rye no mu magambo ye y'inkunga, yakoze ibyo Ibyanditswe bivuga: Amanyagura umugati w'abashonje, ahaza abakene imigati, abagaburira mu nzara, kandi yuzuza imitima ishonje ibyiza. Ariko mu buhe buryo nyine? Amaze gukusanya abashonje ahantu hamwe — bamwe bakaba babeshaho gusa — abagabo, abagore, abana, abashaje, ubukuru bwose bukwiriye imbabazi: akusanya ubwoko bwose bw'ibiribwa bisanzwe birukana inzara, abashyiriraho inkono zuzuye isupyo; hanyuma, ashushanya umurimo wa Kristu, wambaye igitambara cy'imyenda yera kandi atagira isoni zo gukaraba ibirenge by'abigishwa be, akoresheje kandi umurimo w'abagaragu be cyangwa ababashyitsi muri ibyo, yitayeho imibiri n'ubugingo bw'abakene. Uko ni ko umuyobozi wacu mushya na Yozefu wa kabiri yari ameze,» n'ibindi. Ariko murumuna wa Bazili ubwe, Gregori wa Nisa, yongera ko icyo gihe Mutagatifu Bazili na we yatanze ubutunzi bwe bwite ku bakene.

Abashumba bawe bose, abagayita n'abataranyamadini kimwe, bavuga urukundo rwawe, umwete wawe, ishyaka ryawe n'umurimo wawe kuri bose. Wasubije mu buryo bushya amakiliziya menshi, isambu n'amazu y'abepiskopi, kandi muri ibi n'ibindi bikorwa by'urukundo watanze utari gusa umusaruro wa Kiliziya ahubwo n'ubutunzi bwawe bwite. Abakene bose, abababaye n'abagoragozwa bashima urukundo rwawe; imiterere ibikutera kandi ubuntu bw'Imana bukakusunika; mu by'ukuri wavuga ayo magambo ya Yobu mutagatifu: «Kuva mu buhinja bwanjye impuhwe zakundanye, kandi kuva mu nda ya mama zaturanye.»

Wambwiye kenshi — kandi nasanze ari ukuri ku bwe — ko nta kintu ukora wishimye nk'icyo, nta kintu gishimishije, nk'ugusura ibitaro n'amazu y'abakene n'abababaye, kubahoza, kubafasha mu gutanga, no kubaha buri murimo wose w'imbabazi. Abaturage b'i Hainaut n'i Mons babigeragejwe iki ki mwaka. Kuko igihe barebwe n'icyorezo gikomeye cyane, cyahimbye ibihumbi byinshi, kandi nta muti wasigaye wo guhagarika ikibi, wabohereje ibisingizo — umubiri wa Mutagatifu Makariyo, wabaye Umwepiskopi Mukuru wa Antiyokiya mu Arumeniya — kandi uko byinjiye mu mugi, icyorezo, kimeze nk'aho ari cyo cyacecekeshejwe n'ijuru, cyatangiye gusubira inyuma no kugabanuka, kandi ntikigabanutse kugeza ubwo cyazimiye burundu. Abaturage bose b'i Mons babyemera kandi babihimbaza ku mugaragaro, kandi mu gushimira bashyizeho isanduku y'ifeza ya Mutagatifu Makariyo mu giciro kinini.

Mose yashyizeho Abanazirayiti kandi abategekera amategeko mu Ibarura v. Mutagatifu Bazili, Mose w'abihaye Imana b'amatsinda, yashyizeho ibisaho by'abihaye Imana mu Burasirazuba bwose kandi abategekera amategeko y'ubuzima bw'abihaye Imana. Abanyamadini y'ikinyoma bamushinjaga ibyo, bameze nk'aho yari umuhimbyi w'ibishya; abo yasubije mu rwandiko rwa 63: «Turashinjwa, ni ko avuga, n'ubu buzima na bwo, kuko dufite abagabo b'abapfubyi bakorera mu bwiyegurire, bahevye isi n'amaganya yayo yose, ayo Nyagasani yagereranyije n'amahwa ashyigikira ububyamo bw'ijambo; abagabo nk'abo bambara mu mibiri yabo kwicwa kwa Yezu, kandi buri wese atwaye umusaraba we akurikira Nyagasani. Naho jye, nazamara ubuzima bwanjye bwose kugira ngo ibi byaha bimpingweho, kandi mfite iruhande abagabo, abireze kuri njye bwigisha, bahisemo kugeza ubu iki gishimishije cy'ubwiyegurire,» n'ibindi. Yongera ko Misiri, Palestina na Mezopotami byuzuyemo abakurikira iyi Filozofiya y'Abakristu; kandi ko n'abagore, bashushanyije umuhate umwe, bagezeho neza amategeko amwe y'ubuzima. Kubera ko ubu buryo bw'ikirenga bwo kubaho bwari bwatangiye gukura mu bwoko bwe, yashakaga ko bwakwirakwira cyane; kandi gufitanwa ishyari n'ibi bikorwa, nk'uko abivuga mu magambo akurikira, ni nk'ugutsinda Shitani ubwe mu bugiranabi: «Ibi ni byo mbabwira kandi nemeza: ko ibyo se w'ikinyoma, Satani, atigeze ategera kuvuga, imitima y'amahirwe aravuga ubu buri gihe kandi mu bwisanzure busesuye, idahagaze n'umugozi uwo ari wo wose w'ubutabeshwe.» Muri ayo magambo tekereza uko abanyamadini y'ikinyoma n'Abakristu byangirike, abanzi b'Abihaye Imana, bagomba gufatwa.

Wowe, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, ntiwihaye Imana mu masezerano cyangwa mu nzu y'abihaye Imana; ahubwo, ibyo ari bigoye kurushaho, ubaho ubuzima bw'abihaye Imana mu isi. Inzu yawe, umuryango wawe biteye neza, ari iby'Imana bityo, ku buryo bisa nk'urusaho rw'abihaye Imana. Ibyo biterwa n'iki? Ntibizwi ko ibyo Gregori wa Nazianzi avuga kuri Mutagatifu Bazili — «ubuzima bwa Bazili bwari itegekongesha ry'ubuzima ku bantu bose» — na byo bigukwiriye? Uri incuti y'Umuryango wacu n'Abihaye Imana bose b'ukuri, cyane cyane abadabaho kuri bo bonyine ahubwo bakabaniraho n'abandi, kandi bagashyira umuhate wabo mu kuyobora ubugingo ku gakiza.

Ibisaho by'igikundiro mu bwepiskopi bwose bukuru bw'i Mechlin mbere, n'ubu mu bwepiskopi bw'i Gand, byasuwe, bisubiwemo, byubatswe kandi biyoborwa nawe n'amategeko atagatifu kenshi bityo, ku buryo bose bakubona nk'umubyeyi, bakakunda kandi bakiringira muri wowe.

Mose yahagaraye imbere ya Farawo n'Abapfumu be mu budashobora guhinduka butangaje; yihanganiye, yatsinda kandi yiganza abanzi b'ubwoko bw'Imana aho hose. Mutagatifu Bazili yatsinze kandi yica Yulyani, Umwami wahemutse: kuko Mutagatifu Yohani wa Damasiko abivuga aturutse kuri Eladiyusi, mu Ijambo rye rya mbere Ku Bishusho: «Bazili, ni ko avuga, umwizera, yahagaraye imbere y'ishusho ya Bikira Mariya, ku yandi yari na yo ishusho ya Merikuri umumaritiri uzwi, kandi yahagaraye asenga ngo Yulyani wahemutse akurweho. Kuri iyo shusho ni ho yize ibizaba. Kuko yabonye umumaritiri bwa mbere mu mwijima, ariko ntibatinze afata icumu ry'amaraso.»

Ikindi kandi, intambara za Mutagatifu Bazili na Valensi n'Abariyani byari iby'icyubahiro kingana! Modestu, Umuyobozi wa Valensi, nk'uko Gregori wa Nazianzi abyemeza, yashyize igitutu kuri Bazili ngo akurikire idini ry'Umwami. Yabyanze. Nuko Umuyobozi ati: «Twebwe dutegeka ibi — ese muratureba dute?» — «Nta cyo na kimwe, ni ko Bazili avuga, igihe mutegeka ibintu nk'ibyo; kuko Ubukristu bumenyekana atari ku cyubahiro cy'abantu, ahubwo ku budahunyuka bw'ukwizera.» Nuko Umuyobozi, yarakaye akavuga yerekanye: «Mbe, ni ko avuga, ntutinya ubu bubasha?» — «Kuki natinya? ni ko Bazili avuga; bizagenda bite? nzababazwa n'iki?» — «Uzababazwa n'iki? ni ko asubiza. Kimwe mu byinshi biri mu bubasha bwanjye.» — «Ni ibihe? ni ko Bazili yongera: dutume tubisobanukirwe.» — «Gukurwa ibintu, ni ko avuga, kwimukwa, gutotezwa, gupfa.» Nuko Bazili ati: «Niba ufite ikindi, nyinganiza icyo; kuko mu byo wavuze, nta kintu na kimwe kidukora.» — «Ni gute?» ni ko Umuyobozi avuga. «Kuko, ni ko Bazili avuga, gukurwa ibintu ntibikora udafite ikintu na kimwe — keretse ubwo wifuzaga ibi birangamibi n'ibi biyangirike byanjye, n'izi mfaho zintoya mu bitabo, ari byo butunzi bwanjye bwose. Naho kwimukwa, simbizi, kuko ntabwo mfungiwe ahantu na hamwe; sita n'ubu butaka mbaho ngo ni ubwanjye, kandi aho hose nzohanwa nzahafata nk'ahanjye; cyangwa, ndavuge neza kurushaho, nzi neza ko isi yose ari iy'Imana, kandi ko ndi umwimukira no mugenzi muri yo.» Umva ibirushijeho n'umutima urushijeho. «Naho gutotezwa, ni iki nzahabwa, mbaye ari umubiri udafite ibintu? — keretse ubwo wavuze ikiboko cya mbere: kuko icyo gusa ari cyo ufite ububasha bwo gukora. Naho urupfu ruzaba ubuntu kuri njye: ruzanteza vuba kuri ya Mana mbereho kandi nkorera, kandi nkaba nihebye gupfa ku rugero runini, kandi nkaba ndihutira kuyigana kuva kera. Umuriro n'inkota, inyamanswa n'amashami ashishaguza inyama, ni umunezero n'ibyishimo kuri twe aho kuba ubwoba. Nuko rero tutukana, tuninganize, mukore ibyo mushaka, mukoreshe ububasha bwanyu; n'Umwami abumve — ntimuzigera mudutsinda rwose, kandi ntimuzigera mutuma twemera inyigisho mbi, nubwo mwaninganiza ibyugarije kurusha ibyo.»

Avunikishijwe n'ubu bwigenge, Umuyobozi ajya ku Mwami avuga ati: «Twatsinzwe n'Umwepiskopi w'iyi Kiliziya; arusha ibinganizo, afashe cyane mu magambo, akomeye kurusha amagambo y'uburyarya. Umuntu mutinya wundi ugomba gugeragezwa.» Ku bw'ibyo, Kiru Teyo-dori yashyize mu nkeke uyu Muyobozi — waje nyuma arwaye ahatwa gusaba ubufasha bwa Bazili — mu ndirimbo zikurikira:

Uri Umuyobozi w'abantu bose, Modestu,
ariko munsi ya Bazili Mukuru wihatira.
Nubwo ukunda gutegeka, uricisha bugufi;
uri urusaku, nubwo utontoma nk'intare.

Teodoreti, Igitabo cya IV, igice cya 17, yongeraho ibi: Hari kandi, ni ko avuga, umuntu witwaga Demositeni, umuyobozi w'icyotsi cy'umwami, washinjaga Bazili, umwigisha w'isi yose, mu buryo bw'abantu b'amahanga. Ariko Mutagatifu Bazili, aseka, ati: «Twabonye Demositeni utize.» Kandi igihe uwo muntu, yashyutswe n'uburakari budasubirwaho, yatangiraga gukangiriza, Bazili ati: «Ni wowe ugomba kwitaho gushariza amashaza; kuko amatwi yawe yuzuye ubuhumane, ntushobora kumva inyigisho ntagatifu.»

Gushikama kwawe mu kurengera ukwizera no disipuline, Nyakubahwa Umwepiskopi, kushimirwa aho hose; bose babona ko utareka kugeza ubwo ubyemeje, kandi ugaruye abagome mu bugwaneza munsi y'umugogo wa Nyagasani, ku buryo nyuma ubwabo bakitangara ukuntu bahawe kandi ukuntu bahindutse. Bamwe bavuga ko ufite ikimenyetso cy'uburozi bw'igishuko, kubera ko ushobora kwemeza umuntu uwo ari we wese icyo ari cyo cyose, kandi ntutindahazwe kugeza ubwo ukwezaho umuntu uwo ari we wese mu gitekerezo cyawe — ni ukuvuga mu bwenge buzira ikizinga. Wamize ibintu byinshi bigoye muri iki gikorwa; uzamira ibigoye kurushaho, ariko Imana izaba ihari kandi izaguha kubirusha.

Mose, agenda asanga ba sekuruza, yasigiye mu bwoko bwe ifuzo rinini rye — «kandi abana b'Isiraheli bamuririye mu bibaya bya Mowabu iminsi mirongo itatu.»

Ku rupfu n'amaziko ya Mutagatifu Bazili, Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi yanditse ko iteraniro ry'ababarizwa ryari rinini cyane — ndetse Abayuda n'Abapagani — ku buryo benshi basohotseho mu rubanza bakandwa.

Umugi wose uvuga agahinda abaturage bawe b'i Gand bumva kubera kugenda kwawe, bakurira nk'aho se wabo yapfuye. Mu mararampya humvikana aya majwi: «Akabuze! ntitwari dukwiriye umugabo akomeye bityo; ibyaha byacu bidukuraho uyu Mwepiskopi. Tubitekereza nk'ikimenyetso gikomeye cy'Imana. Umumalayika wacu aragenda — ni nde uzadulinda? ni nde uzatuyobora?» Mu rundi ruhande, nk'uko gucura kw'ab'i Gand bakubura ari kwinshi, niko n'umunezero w'ab'i Cambrai bakwakirira; intara y'i Mons iranezerwa, Valenciennes iranyeganyega, Cambrai iriyamura.

Isarura rinini riguhagazeho hano, rigomba gusarurwa mu mirimo myinshi: paruwasi zigera ku magana inani zigomba kuyoborwa; ibihumbi bingahe by'abizera bigomba kugaburirwa? ibihumbi bingahe by'ubugingo bigomba gukizwa? Hano umwete wawe uzakarishwa, urukundo rwawe ruzakangurwa, ishyaka ryawe rizashyutswe — cyane cyane igihe ureba, kandi ubu ureba, irya mvugo ya Mutagatifu Gregori wa Nazianzi: «Bazili, anyuze mu Kiliziya imwe y'i Sezareya, yamurikiye isi yose.»

Mu Mateka y'imbuga ya Kiliziya y'i Cambrai — ari nkuru cyane, kandi mu za mbere z'Ububiligi — uzasanga ko abepiskopi bayo benshi cyane barimo ku rutonde rw'Abatagatifu, buri wese yamurikiriye mu butagatifu butangaje mu ngeso ze zihariye n'imyifatire ye.

Mutagatifu Vindiciani yashyize ubutunzi bwinshi n'umuhate mu kubaka ahantu hatagatifu no kubigira ibikwiriye iteraniro ry'abizera: yubatse ibisaho by'abihaye Imana n'amakiliziya mbere ya byose.

Mutagatifu Lietberti, «yirindaga ibikomeretsa by'umwitonzi cyane, ni ko nyandikubuzimabye avuga, yabyihanganiraga neza, kandi akabisoza vuba cyane; yizeraga ko urukundo rw'amafaranga ari uburozi buruta ubundi bwose bw'ibyiringiro bye byose; incuti ze yazikoresha mu gusubiza ineza, abanzi be mu kwihangana, n'abandi mu gukeza umutima mwiza.» Agenda i Yeruzalemu yajyanye na we abantu ibihumbi bitatu, bamukurikiye mu rugendo rw'ibisingizo. Ubutagatifu bwe bwagaragajwe n'igitangaza: kuko imvi ze nyuma y'urupfu zibaye zongera amabara n'ubwiza bw'imbaraga z'ubusore.

Autberti yabayeho mu baturage b'i Cambrai n'i Hainaut mu kwicisha bugufi n'ubutagatifu butangaje. Munsi ye Hainaut yatangiye gukura mu kwizera kw'Abakristu, akoresheje abagenzi benshi b'ubufasha, nka Mutagatifu Landelini, Mutagatifu Gileni, Mutagatifu Vensani Konte w'i Hainaut, na Mutagatifu Waldetirudi umugore wa Vensani. Kubera iyo mpamvu Umwami Dagoberi w'Abafaransa ntiyaburaga kuza kwakira inama za Mutagatifu Autberti. Yashishikariraga cyane guhindura umunyabyaha umwe ku buryo yagera yariganyira mu marira n'ibihano. Na we yanagiriye neza ibisingizo by'Abatagatifu.

Mutagatifu Gojeriki, akiri umuhungu, yari afite ishyaka ryinshi cyane mu by'idini: yarokoye ku buryo bw'igitangaza imfungwa nyinshi cyane mu mazu y'imbohe n'ingoyi, ubuntu yarusheho. Yubatse amakiliziya menshi mu myaka mirongo itatu n'icyenda yayoboye intebe ye.

Mutagatifu Teyodoriki yasaga na we, uwo ingeso ze zishimirwa na Hinkimari, Umwepiskopi Mukuru w'i Reims.

Kimwe na Mutagatifu Yohani, uwamusimbuye, washimirwa na Hinkimari umwe.

Mutagatifu Odoni, Umwepiskopi wa Cambrai, yari afite ukwizera no gushikama ku Mana na Kiliziya bihagije ku buryo, amaze kwirukanywa mu ntebe ye n'Umwami Henri wa IV kubera ko yanze kwongera kwakira nk'impano ye inkoni n'impeta yakiriye ku Kiliziya mu kwimikwa kwe, amaze igihe cy'ubuzima bwe bwose mu buhungiro i Anchin kandi yapfuye muri ubwo buhungiro.

Aba bazaba indorerwamo zawe z'i muhira, aba bazaba imbohe z'imirimo y'icyubahiro yakozwe kuri Kiliziya imwe, z'intambara z'icyubahiro zaraswe mu butwari ku bwayo. Komeza uko watangiye: abafatanyabikorwa b'umurava n'umwete ntibazabura; batore mu buhanga, kandi ubahamagare ubafatanye mu murimo utagatifu. Igana Mose mu bintu byose; shushanya Bazili. Nsaba ubuntu bw'Imana, kandi sinzareka gusaba, ngo igusukemo umwuka w'abo bombi — ugwanye kandi udoba — kugira ngo ugaburire ibihumbi by'ubugingo bwagushinzwe mu gutinya, isengesho n'urukundo rw'Imana, kandi ubuyobore ku mibereho y'iteka y'umunezero. Urukundo rwanjye kuri wowe n'umwete wanjye mu bintu byawe, ibyo uzi, ni byo binyoboza.

Mu masaha y'ubwisanzure uzashobora gusoma iki gitabo bishimishije: niringiye ko imbaga y'amateka, ingero, imihango n'imigirire ya kera bizagushimisha, kandi ko muri cyo, umenya Mose neza kurushaho, uzarushaho gushishikarizwa kumwigana. Uburyo bwanjye hano ni bumwe nk'ubwo nakoresheje mu bisobanuro bya Pawulo, keretse ko hano ndi mugufi mu magambo kandi ngwiye mu ngingo. Kuko hano ubwoko n'ubugari bw'ingingo ari binini, ndetse n'ubworoshye bwayo no gushimisha kwayo — kuko ibintu byinshi ari iby'amateka, ibindi ari iby'amashusho, bigiriwe neza n'amashusho n'ibimenyetso byiza — kandi ibyo byombi byanyoboje kuzigama amagambo, kugira ngo igitabo kidakura cyane; ku mpamvu imwe na yo nazigamye n'amashusho y'Isanduku, Abakerubi, altari, ihema, n'ibindi.

Nashyize hano ibyo nahurije mu myaka makumyabiri nisobanura Amategeko atanu, kandi nigisha ingingo imwe ubwa kabiri n'ubwa gatatu. Nateranirije hose mu buryo bwiza n'bushimishije ibigereranyo byiza by'imihango ya kera, biryoshyejwe n'imvugo z'ubuhanga, ingero n'inyigisho za kera. Byanshishikarije rya jambo ry'Umugani:

Atsinda bose uhuriza inyungu n'uburyohe.

Ariko ntizarenze urugero rw'urwandiko, nzavuga ibirushijeho kuri Mose n'uburyo bwanjye mu ijambo ry'ibanze.

Nuko rero, Nyakubahwa Mukuru, akira iki kimenyetso n'iki gihamya cy'urukundo n'icyubahiro njye, Ishuri ry'i Louvain n'Umuryango wacu wose dukugaragariza; kandi kubera ko ubu nhamagariwe ahandi mu yindi mirimo, kandi ndasobanukirwa nzongera kubona Nyakubahwa Mukuru wawe muri iyi si, iki kibe urwibutso rwanjye rutazima mu mutima wawe, kugira ngo, dutatanye ku mubiri mu gihe gito ariko duhora turi hamwe mu mutima, nyuma y'ubu buzima bugufi kandi bubabaje duhurire mu ikuzo ry'ijuru muri Kristu Nyagasani wacu — uwo umuhate wacu wose ucunishiriza kandi wihatiriza — kandi buri wese muri twe ahabwe, wowe ku rugero runini, njye mu rugero gusa rw'ubushobozi bwanjye buto, ibyo Daniyeli yasezeranije: «Abahanga bazamurika nk'urumuri rw'ikirere, kandi abigisha benshi gukiranuka, nk'inyenyeri mu bihe bitarondoreka.» Amen.

UBUZIMA BWA CORNELIUS A LAPIDE.

Cornelius Cornelii a Lapide, Umunyabelgike ku bwenegihugu, wavukiye i Bocholt mu karere ka Eupen, wavutse mu babyeyi b'icyubahiro, yatangiye gusenga Imana mu kwizera, mu kwizigira no mu rukundo kuva atangira gukoresha ubushobozi bwe bwo gutekereza. Akiri umusore yinjiye mu Muryango wa Yezu ku itariki ya 8 Nyakanga mu mwaka w'agakiza 1592; muri wo, mbere y'uko ubusore bwe bushira, yabaye umupadiri kandi buri munsi yatangaga Igitambo Kitagatifu nk'igitambo gihoraho, kugeza ku iherezo ry'ubuzima bwe. Yigishije Ururimi Rutagatifu n'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu ku mugaragaro i Louvain imyaka irenga makumyabiri, hanyuma asabwa na ba mukuru be kujya i Roma, aho yigishije ibyo bimwe mu myaka myinshi afite izina ryamamaye cyane, kugeza igihe, ananiwe n'uwo murimo, yihindukiye burundu ku kwandika mu ibanga. Ubuzima bw'ubuhe bwoko yashyizeho icyo gihe, nta magambo abisobanura neza kuruta aye bwite; avugana n'Imana, yabivuze atya: «Ibi bikorwa byanjye, n'imbuto zabyo, ubumenyi bwanjye bwose, ubwenge bwanjye bwose, ibisobanuro byanjye byose, nabisakajemo ikuzo ryawe, Ubutatu Butagatifu n'Ubumwe Butatu, kandi nashakaga ko igikorwa cyanjye cyose, ukubabara kwanjye kwose, n'ubuzima bwanjye bwose, bitaba ikindi kitari ishimwe ryawe ridahwema. Wihishuriye ubwenge bwanjye kera, kugira ngo ngushimire kandi ngushake wenyine, ibindi byose mbyite ibisanzwe, ibitagira umumaro kandi bihunga. Ni cyo gituma ndiruka amazu y'abami n'inkombe: nkurikira ubwigunge n'umutuzo binyishimisha kandi bitari impfabusa ku bandi, ndi kumwe na Mutagatifu Bazili, Gregori, na Jeromi, uwo Betelehemu ye yitagatifu yashakishije mu Palestina yose byimazeyo, nasanze hano i Roma. Kera mu buto bwanjye nakinye igice cya Marita; noneho mu isarurwa ry'imyaka, nkina kandi nkunda cyane igice cya Mariya Magadalena, nibuka ko ubuzima bugufi, nibuka Imana, nibuka iteka ryashyizwe hafi. Icyumba cyanjye gusa — kinyizerwa kandi nkikunda kurusha isi yose, kandi gisa n'ijuru ku isi — n'ubuceceke gusa ni byo mbariyemo; utuye mu cyumba cyanjye, ntaho ntava mu isomero ryanjye ritagatifu, ngerageza kuba utuye mu ijuru; nkurikira ubwigunge, ahubwo umurimo, w'itekerereza ritagatifu, gusoma no kwandika. Mpa Imana, imwe kandi itatu, kwakira, gutekereza no gushimira amagambo yayo n'inkunga zayo; nicara ku birenge bya Kristu, ndamanika ku minwa ye ngo nsesere amagambo y'ubugingo, ayandi rero nsuke ku bandi.»

Ibyo yabikora ari umutama, yuzuye imigisha y'ubutagatifu burambye; kuko kuva akinjira mu Muryango wa Yezu, gutekereza bidahwema ku bw'iteka ryiza ryihoraho, byamutsindagiye gutukana ibintu by'abantu no kwifuza iby'ijuru, ku buryo kuva icyo gihe nta kindi yagambiriye kitari ubushake bwa Kristu buhoraho, ishimwe n'ikuzo rye, mu buzima no mu rupfu, mu gihe no mu iteka; gushimira no guteza imbere ibyo byonyine yishimiye mu ndahiro ze zose no mu bumenyi bwe bwose, mu mbaraga zose z'umubiri n'umutima; nta cyo yategerezaga uwo ari we wese mu bantu b'isi, nta cyo yifuzaga; imyanzuro n'amashimwe y'abantu ntiyayitayeho; ashaka gushimisha Imana yonyine kandi atinya kutayishimisha, ni ibi byonyine yashishikarizaga, ni ibi byonyine yasabaga, aha ni ho gusoma kwe kwose no kwandika kwe kwose, umurimo we wose wacuriraga: kugira ngo izina ryayo ritagatifu ryezwe, kandi ubushake bwayo butagatifu bukorwe nk'uko bukorwa mu ijuru no ku isi. Icyifuzo cyaka cyane cyo kwemera kubabazwa, cyashyizwe mu mutima we n'Imana kuva mu gihe cye cya mbere cy'ubunovisi, yagikubiyemo iteka ku buryo yasabaga uwo ikamba buri gihe n'indahiro ze zose. Yari amaze kugwa hafi aho mu maboko mu mwaka wa 1604, ubwo yari atuye hafi y'urusengero rwa Bikira Mariya wa Aspromont, rwamamazwa n'ibitangaza, rutari kure ya Louvain, kandi afasha imbaga y'abantu babarurukaga ku mpamvu z'ukwizera mu guhabwa imbabazi, mu nyigisho n'andi mirimo mitagatifu; ingabo z'abafarashi b'Abaholandezi zaguye ku hantu hadateganijwe ku munsi mukuru w'Ivuka rya Bikira Mariya, zisenya byose n'inkota n'umuriro; yazingirwanyijwe, kandi gato aba yafashwe kandi yicwa. Ariko ku bufasha bw'Ukaristiya Ntagatifu yari agendana ikuyemo mu rusengero kugira ngo abanyamadini y'ikinyoma batayihumanya, no ku mfashanyo ya Bikira Mariya yamusabiye mu ndahiro idasanzwe, akaga karangiwe nta gushidikanya ko byari igitangaza; we ubwe arabungabunzwe atagize inenge n'umugambi w'Imana utangaje. Ikindi, ukuntu icyifuzo cyo kubabazwa kitarigera kimuvaho bisobanurwa n'aya magambo, ubwo yari amaze Ibisobanuro bye ku bahanuzi bane, yabwiye abatagatifu bane b'abahanuzi atya: «Mwa bahanuzi b'Uhoraho mwe, mwangize umusangiranye ubuhanuzi bwanyu n'ikamba ry'ubwigisha; mundagize, ndabinginze, umusangiranye n'igitambo cy'amaraso, kugira ngo nanjye mpfundikishe amaraso yanjye ukuri narashyize muri mwe, nigishije abandi, kandi nandika. Kuko ubwigisha bwanjye bw'ubuhanga butazaba bwuzuye kandi burangiye keretse nabuzibiranya n'uru rufuniko. Mu myaka igera mirongo itatu narahababariranye kandi ndababarira mwebwe ku bushake bwanjye, ndishimye, igitambo gihoraho cy'ubuzima bw'idini, igitambo cy'indwara, igitambo cy'ubumenyi no kwandika: mumbonere, ndabinginze, nk'igisonga, n'igitambo cya kane cy'amaraso. Nahahiriye mwebwe imyuka y'ubuzima n'imyuka y'umutima; nzahanahirira n'amaraso. Ku murimo wose nakoreye mu myaka yose mu kubasobanura ku neza y'Imana, mbabohora, kandi mbatuma muvuge kandi muhanuze mu rurimi rushya, ku buryo nananiye kumwe namwe — mumbonere, nk'ibihembo by'umuhanuzi wanyu, igitambo, ndavuga, mbibonere kuri Data w'amacyo nk'uko mubona n'impuhwe.» Ahindukira vuba kuri Nyina w'Imana uhiriye, we yari akwiriye we ubwe n'ibye byose, uwamuhamagaye nubwo atari ukwiriye mu Muryango Mutagatifu w'Umwana we, yayoboye, yafashije kandi yigisha mu buryo butangaje; amusaba ko amugereza igitambo cy'amaraso; noneho asaba cyane Nyagasani Yezu, urukundo rwe, ku migisha ya Nyina we n'iy'Abahanuzi, kugira ngo atazabaho ubuzima bw'ubunebwe cyangwa ngo apfire urupfu rw'ubunebwe ku buriri, ahubwo ari urw'igiti cyangwa icy'icyuma. Ibyo byifuzo byahuriranye n'amashusho y'izindi ngeso nziza ze, byatajya byirwa muri ibi bikorwa. Nta kintu cyaboneka cyoroshye kuruta we, nta kintu cy'umwitonzi, nta kintu cy'imyifatire myiza. Ubwicisha bugufi bwe bwari bunini cyane mu bumenyi bwinshi bw'ubwo bwoko n'ubugari bwose bw'ubuhanga bw'abantu n'ubw'Imana, ku buryo yavugaga ati: «Mu by'ukuri no mu mutima wanjye, ndi umupfapfa kurusha abantu bose, kandi ubuhanga bw'abantu ntiburi kumwe nanjye; ndi umwana muto utazi ukurwamo kwe n'ukwinjiramo kwe.» Ahandi kandi avuga atya: «Kuva mu myaka igera mirongo ine nashyizeho umutima muri ubu bumenyi butagatifu, mu myaka mirongo itatu nta kindi nakoze, kandi nkomeje kwigisha Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu, nyamara numva ukuntu ntageze kure muri byo.» Yariyamiriye cyane ku mategeko akomeye y'ubuzima bw'idini ku buryo, ngo atagirira ingaruka mbi kubera we, yanze ko bamushyirira ikintu na kimwe kidasanzwe ku biryo, nubwo ubuzima bwe bwari bwose ari buke, bwatamijwe n'imyaka, kandi bwakoreshejwe mu bumenyi bwari buzungura Kiliziya y'Imana, kandi atabashoboraga gufungura ibiryo byahabwaga abandi. Kumvira kwari iteka kumushimisha kurusha ubuzima, n'urukundo rw'ukuri. Ukuri yakwishyize imbere y'ibindi byose mu kwandika kwe kwose, kandi kumvira ni ko kwamuyoboye gushyira ibyanditswe bye mu mucyo — ibyanditswe yari gushyira mu gaceceka katagira iherezo. Arangaye muri ibi bikorwa by'ubutagatifu, amaze imyaka mirongo irindwi, yahembye hatinda ubuzima ku isi mu Mugi Mutagatifu, aho yari iteka yifuza guhuriza amagufwa ye n'ay'abatagatifu, ku itariki ya 12 Werurwe mu mwaka wa 1637. Umurambo we, ku bubasha bwa ba mukuru be, washyizwe mu isanduku ye bwite kugira ngo ushobore kumenyekana umunsi umwe, hanyuma uhambwa. Urutonde rw'ibikorwa bye ni uru rukurikira: Ibisobanuro ku Itegeko rya Mose rya Gatanu, Antwerp 1616, kongera mu 1623 mu gicapo kinini; ku bitabo bya Yozuwe, Abacamanza, Ruti, Abami, na Amateka, Antwerp 1642, mu gicapo kinini; ku bitabo bya Ezira, Nehemiya, Tobi, Yudita, Esitera, n'Abamakabe, Antwerp 1644; ku Migani ya Salomo, Antwerp na Paris, kwa Cramoisy, 1635; ku Mubwiriza, Antwerp 1638, Paris 1639; ku Buhanga; ku Ndirimbo Ihebuje; ku Mwene Siraki; ku Bahanuzi bane Bakuru; ku Bahanuzi cumi na babiri Bato; ku Mavanjiri ane ya Yezu Kristu; ku Byakozwe n'Intumwa; ku Mabaruwa yose ya Pawulo Intumwa Mutagatifu; ku Mabaruwa Gatolika; ku Ihishurirwa rya Yohani Intumwa Mutagatifu.

Yasigaye adashoje ibisobanuro bye ku bitabo bya Yobu na Zaburi.

AMATEGEKO Y'INAMA NKURU YA TRENTO
(INAMA YA KANE).

KU BYANDITSWE BITAGATIFU BYEMEWE.

Inama Nkuru ya Trento, itagatifu, iy'isi yose kandi rusange, yateranye mu buryo bwemewe mu Roho Mutagatifu, intumwa eshatu z'Inyanja Ntagatifu y'Ubutumwa ziyiyobora, ishyira imbere y'amaso yayo iteka: kugira ngo, amakosa avanyweho, ubuziranenge bw'Inkuru Nziza bubungabungwe mu Kiliziya; iyo Nkuru Nziza, yasezeranyijwe mbere binyuze mu bahanuzi mu Byanditswe Bitagatifu, Nyagasani wacu Yezu Kristu, Umwana w'Imana, yayitangaje ubwa mbere n'akanwa ke bwite, hanyuma ategeka ko itangarizwa ibinyabuzima byose n'Intumwa ze nk'isoko y'ukuri kwose gukiza n'iyobokamana ry'imyifatire: ibonye ko uku kuri n'iyi yobokamana birimo mu bitabo byanditswe no mu migenzo idanditswe, yabikiwe n'Intumwa bavuye mu kanwa ka Kristu ubwe, cyangwa n'Intumwa ubwabo, Roho Mutagatifu ibivugisha, yakurikiranywe nko guhabwa ikiganza ku kiganza kugeza kuri twe: ikurikiza urugero rw'Abakurambere b'ukuri, yakira kandi yubaha n'urukundo rumwe rw'ukwizera n'icyubahiro ibitabo byose by'Isezerano rya Kera n'Isezerano Rishya — kubera ko Imana imwe ni yo yabyanditsi byombi — ndetse n'imigenzo yavuzwe, iyo ipfana n'ukwizera n'iyo ipfana n'imyifatire, nk'iyavuzwe n'akanwa ka Kristu cyangwa yandikiwe na Roho Mutagatifu, kandi ikabungabungwa mu Kiliziya Gatolika mu guhabana bidahwema.

Yabonye ko ari ngombwa ko urutonde rw'ibitabo bitagatifu rushyirwa muri iri tegeko, kugira ngo hatagira umuntu ushidikanya ku bitabo byemewe n'iyi Nama Nkuru. Ni ibi bikurikira:

By'Isezerano rya Kera: ibitabo bitanu bya Mose, ni ukuvuga, Intangiriro, Iyimukamisiri, Abalevi, Ibarura, Ivugururamategeko; Yozuwe, Abacamanza, Ruti; ibitabo bine by'Abami; bibiri by'Amateka; igitabo cya mbere na cya kabiri cya Ezira, icya kabiri kikitwa Nehemiya; Tobi, Yudita, Esitera, Yobu, Zaburi za Dawidi ari zo ijana na mirongo itanu; Imigani, Umubwiriza, Indirimbo Ihebuje, Ubuhanga, Mwene Siraki, Ezayi, Yeremiya hamwe na Baruki, Ezekiyeli, Daniyeli; abahanuzi cumi na babiri bato, ni ukuvuga, Hoseya, Yoweli, Amosi, Obadiya, Yonasi, Mika, Nahumu, Habakuki, Sofoniya, Hagayi, Zakariya, Malakiya; ibitabo bibiri by'Abamakabe, igitabo cya mbere n'icya kabiri.

By'Isezerano Rishya: Amavanjiri ane, uko Matayo, Mariko, Luka na Yohani bayanditse; Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa byanditswe na Luka umwanditsi w'inkuru nziza; Amabaruwa cumi n'ane ya Pawulo Intumwa: ku Banyaroma, abiri ku Banyakorinti, ku Banyagalatiya, ku Banyefezi, ku Banyafilipi, ku Banyakolosi, abiri ku Banyatesaloniki, abiri kuri Timote, kuri Tito, kuri Filemoni, ku Bahebureyi; abiri ya Petero Intumwa; atatu ya Yohani Intumwa; rimwe rya Yakobo Intumwa; rimwe rya Yuda Intumwa; n'Ihishurirwa rya Yohani Intumwa.

Ariko umuntu wese utakira ibyo bitabo byose mu buryo bwuzuye hamwe n'ibice byabyo byose, nk'uko bisanzwe bisomwa mu Kiliziya Gatolika kandi nk'uko biri mu gicapo cya kera cy'Igilatini cya Vuligata, kandi uzi neza kandi abigambiriye agasuzugura imigenzo yavuzwe haruguru, nahabwe igihano.

II.
KU GICAPO NO KU GUKORESHA IBITABO BITAGATIFU.

Ikindi kandi, iyi Nama Nkuru itagatifu, ibonye ko Kiliziya y'Imana ishobora kubona inyungu itari nke niba muri za mpinduro zose z'Ikilatini z'ibitabo bitagatifu zikoreshwa ubu hamenyekana iyari ikwiriye kwemezwa nk'ukuri, yashyizeho kandi itangaza ko igicapo cya kera cya Vuligata, cyemejwe n'imikoreshereze y'ibinyejana byinshi muri Kiliziya ubwayo, kigomba kwemezwa nk'ukuri mu gusoma ku mugaragaro, mu biganiro, mu nyigisho no mu bisobanuro; kandi ko nta muntu ukwiriye gushidikanya cyangwa kurwanya icyo gicapo ku mpamvu iyo ari yo yose.

Ikindi, kugira ngo hakumirwe imitekerereze idasobanutse, yashyizeho ko nta muntu, wiringiye ubuhanga bwe bwite, mu bibazo by'ukwizera n'imyifatire bijyanye no kwimakaza inyigisho ya gikristu, ugoreka Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu ajyana n'ibitekerezo bye, ukwiriye gusobanura Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu ahabanye n'icyo gisobanuro Kiliziya Nyina wacu itagatifu — ishinzwe gucira urubanza ku gisobanuro cy'ukuri n'ibisobanuro by'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu — yagikundiye kandi ikagikunda; cyangwa ndetse ahabanye n'ibyo Abakurambere ba Kiliziya bose bahuriyeho; nubwo ibyo bisobanuro bitari gushyirwa mu mucyo na rimwe. Abarenze kuri ibyo bazamenyeshwa n'ababashinzwe kandi bahanwe ibihano bishyizweho n'amategeko.

Nanone, ishaka gushyiraho umupaka ukwiriye ku bacapyi muri ibi — bakora ubu nta mupaka, ni ukuvuga, batekereza ko ibashimishije byose byemewe — bacapa ibitabo by'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu ubwabyo n'ibisobanuro n'ibitekerezo bya buri wese uko byagenda, kenshi batavuze icapiro, kenshi ndetse bashyiraho icapiro ry'ibinyoma, ikindi kigaragaza cyane, nta zina ry'umwanditsi; kandi bagurisha nta mahane ibitabo nk'ibyo byacapishijwe ahandi: yashyizeho kandi yatanze ko kuva ubu Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu, by'umwihariko igicapo cya kera cya Vuligata, bigomba gucapwa neza ibishoboka byose; kandi ko nta muntu wemewe gucapa cyangwa gutuma hacapwa ibitabo ibi ari byo byose ku bintu bitagatifu nta zina ry'umwanditsi; cyangwa kubigurisha mu gihe kizaza cyangwa kubibika iwe keretse byabanje gusuzumwa no kwemezwa n'umuyobozi w'aho hantu, ku gihano cy'igihano n'amafaranga yashyizweho mu mategeko y'Inama Nkuru y'aha bugufi ya Laterano. Kandi niba ari abo mu nzego z'amadini, hiyongereyeho igenzura n'uruhushya, bagomba kandi kubona uruhushya ku bayobozi babo, ibitabo bamaze kubyisuzumira nk'uko amategeko yabo abisaba. Abishyira mu nyandiko cyangwa abashyira hanze batarashyize aho kubisuzumwa no kwemezwa, bagomba guhura n'ibihano bimwe nk'iby'abacapyi. Abikorera cyangwa abisoma, bataravuze abanditsi, bafatwa nk'abanditsi ubwabo. Kwemezwa kw'ibitabo nk'ibyo kugomba gutangwa mu nyandiko, kandi bikagaragara mu buryo bwemewe ku ipaji rya mbere ry'igitabo, cyaba icyanditswe cyangwa igicapwe; kandi ibi byose, ni ukuvuga kwemezwa no gusuzumwa, bigomba gukorwa ku buntu, kugira ngo ibikwiriye kwemezwa byemezwe n'ibitakwiriye bivanweho.

Nyuma y'ibyo, ishaka gukumira iyo mitekerereze itabogamye amagambo n'interuro z'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu zigakoreshwa mu bintu bibi — ni ukuvuga mu bikinisho, mu bintu by'ibinyoma, ibitagira umumaro, ibishyeshye, ibirego, imihango mibi ya satani, ubupfumu, imikino y'amahirwe, ndetse n'ibitabo bishinja — itegeka kandi ikurikiranira, kugira ngo ivanweho iryo siga n'iryo suzuguro, ko kuva ubu nta muntu ukwiriye gukoresha amagambo y'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu mu bintu nk'ibyo n'ibisa nabyo mu buryo ubwo ari bwo bwose, kugira ngo abantu bose b'ubwo bwoko, abagoreka n'abahumanya ijambo ry'Imana, bakumirwe na ba episkopi n'ibihano by'amategeko ku bwigenge bwabo.

IJAMBO RIGENEWE UMUSOMYI (1)

Mu nyinshi n'ingenzi z'impano Imana yagiriye Kiliziya yayo binyuze mu Nama Ntagatifu ya Trento, iy'isa nk'igomba guharirwa umwanya wa mbere: ni uko mu nyandiko nyinshi z'ikilatini z'Ibyanditswe by'Imana, yemeje mu itegeko ry'ingirakamaro cyane ko inyandiko imwe gusa ya kera kandi Vuligata — yari yemejwe n'imikoreshereze y'ibinyejana byinshi muri Kiliziya — ari yo kuri koko nyakuri.

Kuko (ntitwibuke ko mu nyandiko nshya zishyize zitigeze ari nke zisa n'izagenzurwaga ku bushake bwo kwemeza amahiriwe y'iki gihe), ubwo bwinshi n'ubwo butandukanye bw'impinduka bwashoboraga koko guteza urujijo rukomeye muri Kiliziya y'Imana. Kuko ubu bimaze kwemezwa ko mu gihe cyacu byagenze hafi nk'uko Mutagatifu Jeromi yahamije ko byabaye mu gihe cye: ni uko hari ibikopi byinshi nk'uko hari inyanditswe, kuko buri muntu yongerwagaho cyangwa akavana ibyo yishakiye.

Nyamara ububasha bw'iyi nyandiko ya kera ya Vuligata bwagumye ari bukomeye uko byagenze kose, n'ubwiza bwayo bwagumye ari buhebuje, ku buryo abacamanza banyuzwe batashoboraga gushidikanya ko igomba gushyirwa kure mbere y'izindi nyandiko zose z'ikilatini. Kuko ibitabo birimo (nk'uko bitwegerewe hafi nk'intoki n'intoki binyuze ku bakurambere bacu) byakiriwe igice biturutse ku buhinduzi cyangwa igikosora cya Mutagatifu Jeromi, n'igice kandi kigakomoka ku nyandiko y'ikilatini ya kera cyane, Mutagatifu Jeromi ayita Rusange na Vuligata, Mutagatifu Agusitini akayita Italika, naho Mutagatifu Gregori akayita Ubuhinduzi bwa Kera.

Ndetse kuri ubu buzima n'ubwiza bw'iyi nyandiko ya kera (cyangwa Italika), hari igihamya gihebuje cya Mutagatifu Agusitini mu gitabo cya kabiri cy'Inyigisho y'Abakristu, aho yaciye urubanza ati mu nyandiko zose z'ikilatini zari zihari byinshi icyo gihe, Italika ni yo igomba gushyirwa imbere kuko yari — nk'uko ubwe abivuga — «ihambiriye ku magambo mu gihe ibika ubusobanuro bw'ibisobanuka.»

Naho kuri Mutagatifu Jeromi, hariho ibihamya byinshi bihebuje by'Abakurambere ba kera: Mutagatifu Agusitini amwita umuntu w'umuhanga cyane kandi ufite ubumenyi bw'indimi eshatu, akanemeza akoresheje n'igihamya cy'Abahebureyi ubwabo ko ubuhinduzi bwe ari bw'ukuri. Na Mutagatifu Gregori amushima cyane ku buryo avuga ko ubuhinduzi bwe (abwita bushya) bwahinduye ibintu byose mu buryo bw'ukuri cyane buvuye mu mvugo y'Igiheburayo, bityo ni bwo bukwiriye cyane ko bwemerwa burundu mu bintu byose. Mutagatifu Izidori, mu mwanya utari umwe, ashyira imbere ubuhinduzi bwa Jeromi ku bundi bwose kandi avuga ko bwakiriwe rusange kandi bwemejwe n'amatorero y'Abakristu kuko ari bw'amagambo asobanutse kandi bw'ubusobanuro bw'ukuri. Na Sofroniyusi, umuntu w'umuhanga cyane, abonye ko ubuhinduzi bwa Mutagatifu Jeromi bwashimwe cyane atari n'Abalatini gusa ahubwo no n'Abagiriki, yabuhaye agaciro kanini ku buryo yahinduye Zaburi n'Abahanuzi avuye mu buhinduzi bwa Jeromi abijyana mu Kigiriki cyiza.

Ikindi kandi, abagabo b'abahanga cyane baje nyuma — Remigi, Beda, Rabani, Hayimo, Anselimo, Petero Damiyani, Rishardi, Hugo, Berinardo, Ruperi, Petero Lombardi, Alegizanderi, Aluberi, Tomasi, Bonaventura, n'abandi bose bahemuye muri Kiliziya mu myaka magana icyenda — bakoresheje ubuhinduzi bwa Mutagatifu Jeromi ku buryo izindi mpinduka (zari hafi zitagira ingano) zagiye zisohoka mu ntoki z'abahanga b'iyobokamana kandi zigasazira burundu.

Niyo mpamvu Kiliziya gatolika itashimiye nta mpamvu Mutagatifu Jeromi nk'Umuhanga Mukuru wa Kiliziya kandi nk'uwahagurutswe n'Imana kugira ngo asobanure Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu, ku buryo ubu bidashoboka gucira urubanza rubi icyemezo cy'abo bose banze kwakira imirimo y'Umuhanga akomeye nk'uwo, cyangwa biringiye ko bashobora gutanga icyo ari cyo cyose cyiza — cyangwa nibura kigereranwa.

Nyamara, kugira ngo ubuhinduzi bwizewe ku buryo bwose, kandi bufitiye akamaro Kiliziya mu mpande zose, butangirika mu gice na kimwe n'igihe kibeshya, cyangwa ubunangizi bw'abanyamashini, cyangwa ubutwari bw'ibijishijwe bw'abo basana ku bushake: iryo Nama Ntagatifu rya Trento ryongereyeho mu itegeko ryayo ubwenge buvuga ko iyi nyandiko ya kera ya Vuligata igomba gucapwa neza cyane uko bishoboka, kandi ko nta muntu wemerewe kuyicapa atabanje kubona uruhushya n'icyemezo cy'Abakuru. Muri iryo tegeko ryanashyizeho ikimenyetso ku butwari n'ubwigenge bw'abanyamashini, kandi ryamurikiye ubumaranire n'ubushishozi bw'Abashumba ba Kiliziya mu kubungabunga no gufata neza iki kintu gihebuje.

Nubwo abahanga b'iyobokamana bo mu mashuri makuru y'icyubahiro bakoze umurimo ukomeye w'ishimwe mu gusubiza inyandiko ya Vuligata mu bwiza bwayo bwa mbere, nyamara kubera ko mu kintu gikomeye nk'iki nta bumaranire bushobora kuba burenze, kandi kubera ko inyandiko nyinshi z'amashusho za kera cyane zari zasabwe n'itegeko rya Papa Mukuru zikazanwa mu Murwa Mukuru, n'amaherezo, kubera ko kumenya ko ishyirwa mu bikorwa ry'amategeko y'amakoraniro rusange, hamwe n'ubuzimagatozi n'ubudahunyuka bw'Ibyanditswe ubwabyo, bigomba gukorwa n'Intebe ya Papa by'umwihariko: Papa Piyo wa IV, Papa Mukuru, hamwe n'ubumaranire bwe budashoboka ku mpande zose za Kiliziya, yashinze uwo murimo abakaridina bamwe bashyizweho ba Kiliziya Ntagatifu y'i Roma, n'abandi bagabo b'abahanga cyane mu nyandiko ntagatifu no mu ndimi zitandukanye, kugira ngo bakosore inyandiko y'ikilatini ya Vuligata neza cyane, bakoresheje inyandiko za kera cyane z'amashusho, basuzumye kandi inkomoko z'Igiheburayo n'Ikigiriki by'Ibibliya, n'amaherezo bagisha inama ibisobanuro by'Abakurambere ba kera.

Papa Piyo wa V na we yakomeje uwo murimo. Ariko iryo koraniro, ryari ryarateranukiwe igihe kirekire bitewe n'imirimo itandukanye kandi ikomeye cyane y'Intebe ya Papa, ryahamagawe n'umwete ugurumana na Papa Sikisito wa V, wahamagawe n'Iyobera ry'Imana mu Busaserdoti bw'Ikirenga, kandi amaherezo ategeka ko umurimo urangiye ugomba gucapishwa. Umurimo umaze gucapwa, na wa Papa akaba ahari akora kugira ngo usohoke mu mucyo, abonye ko ibintu bitigeze ari bike byinjiye muri Bibiliya Ntagatifu kubera ikosa ry'igicapo, bikaba bisa n'ibikeneye ubumaranire bushya, yaciye urubanza kandi ategeka ko umurimo wose usubizwa ku nyundo. Ariko kubera ko yapfuye atarabashije kubirangiza, Papa Gregori wa XIV, waje nyuma y'ubupapa bwa Papa Urbani wa VII bumaze iminsi cumi n'ibiri, asohoze umugambi wo mu mutima we, yatangiye kubirangiza, abakaridina bamwe b'icyubahiro kinini n'abandi bagabo b'abahanga cyane basubiye gushyirwaho ku bw'uwo murimo.

Ariko na we, n'uwaje inyuma ye, Papa Inosenti wa IX, bamaze gukurwa muri uru rucyo mu gihe gito cyane, amaherezo mu itangira ry'Ubupapa bwa Papa Klementi wa VIII, ubu afite isuka y'ubuyobozi bwa Kiliziya yose, umurimo Papa Sikisito wa V yari agamije warangiye, Imana ibifashijemo neza.

Akire rero, musomyi w'umukristu, hamwe n'icyemezo cya Papa Klementi, Papa Mukuru, uturutse ku Kinyamakuru cya Vatikani, inyandiko ya kera ya Vuligata y'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu, ikosowe n'ubumaranire bunini bushoboka bwose: iyo nyandiko, nk'uko bigoye kwemeza ko itunganye mu buryo bwose bitewe n'ubugoyigoyi bwa muntu, ni ko kandi bidakwiriye gushidikanywa ko ari yo ikosowe kandi itanduye kurusha izindi zose zagaragaye kugeza uyu munsi.

Ndetse nubwo muri iki gisuzumwa cy'Ibibliya, umwete munini washyizwe mu kugereranya inyandiko z'amashusho, inkomoko z'Igiheburayo n'Ikigiriki, n'ibisobanuro ubwabyo by'Abakurambere ba kera: ariko muri iyi nyandiko isanzwe, nk'uko ibintu bimwe byahinduwe ku bushake, ni ko n'ibindi, byasaga n'ibigomba guhindurwa, byasigiwe ku bushake bitagihindutse: byombi kubera ko Mutagatifu Jeromi yaburiye kenshi ko bigomba gukorwa gutyo, kugira ngo hatazagwa inkeke mu baturage; kandi kubera ko bikwiriye kwizera ko abakurambere bacu, bahinduye ibivuye mu Giheburayo n'Ikigiriki mu kilatini, bari bafite ibitabo byiza kandi bikosowe kuruta ibyo byadukeye nyuma y'igihe cyabo (ibyo bishobora kuba, kubera gusubirwamo igihe kirekire, byahindutse bidatanduye kandi bidakwiye nk'uko byari bimeze); n'amaherezo, kubera ko umugambi w'inama ntagatifu y'abakaridina b'icyubahiro kinini n'abandi bagabo b'abahanga cyane bashyizweho n'Intebe ya Papa ku bw'uyu murimo utari uguha inyandiko nshya, cyangwa ugukosora cyangwa ugusana umuhinduzi wa kera mu gice na kimwe; ahubwo ni ugusubiza inyandiko ya kera y'ikilatini ya Vuligata ubwayo — yejejwe amakosa y'abasubiriye kera n'amakosa y'ibikosora byononekaye — mu buzima bwayo bwa mbere n'ubudahunyuka, no kumaze kuyisubiza, guharanira n'imbaraga zose ko icapwa neza cyane hakurikijwe itegeko ry'Inama Nkuru y'Isi Yose.

Ikindi kandi, muri iyi nyandiko byabonye ko ari byiza kutiyongeraho ikintu na kimwe kitari icy'amategeko, ntanakimwe cy'ibinyoma, ntanakimwe cy'ikindi. Kandi iyi ni yo mpamvu ibitabo byandikwaho Ezira wa III na wa IV (Inama Ntagatifu ya Trento itibariye mu bitabo by'amategeko), hamwe n'Isengesho rya Umwami Manase (ridahari mu Giheburayo cyangwa mu Kigiriki, ridabonetse mu nyandiko za kera, ridafite igice cy'igitabo na kimwe cy'amategeko) byashyizwe hanze y'urutonde rw'Ibyanditswe by'amategeko. Kandi nta mahuza (adabuza kongerwaho nyuma), nta mpapuro, nta misomyo itandukanye, nta ntangiriro na imwe, nta bintu bikubiye ku itangira ry'ibitabo bigaragara mu mpande.

Ariko nk'uko Intebe ya Papa idacira urubanza ubushishozi bw'abo bategura amahuza y'imyandiko, imisomyo itandukanye, intangiriro za Mutagatifu Jeromi n'ibindi bimeze bityo mu nyandiko zindi: ni ko kandi idabuza ko, mu bwoko bundi bw'igicapo mu iyi nyandiko ya Vatikani, ubufasha bw'ubwo bwoko bushobora kongerwaho mu gihe kizaza ku bw'ubworoshye n'inyungu by'abiga, ariko hashyizweho ko imisomyo itandukanye idashyirwa ku mpande z'Inyandiko ubwayo.

PAPA KLEMENTI WA VIII.
KUGIRA NGO IBYO BIBUKWE ITEKA RYOSE.

Kubera ko inyandiko ya Vuligata ya Bibiliya Ntagatifu, yasubijwe hamwe n'imirimo n'ubumaranire bikomeye kandi yejejwe amakosa neza cyane, ku mubano w'Imana, irasohoka mu mucyo iturutse ku Kinyamakuru cyacu cya Vatikani: Twebwe, twifuza gutanga mu gihe gikwiriye kugira ngo iyi nyandiko ibungabungwe idahunyutse, nk'uko bikwiriye, twisunze ububasha bwa Papa, tubuza by'ingirakamaro ko mu myaka icumi ibarwa uhereye ku itariki y'izi nyandiko, haba hakuno cyangwa hakurya y'imisozi, icapwa n'umuntu uwo ari we wese ahandi hantu atari ku Kinyamakuru cyacu cya Vatikani. Iyo myaka icumi ishize, dutegeka ko iki cyitonderwa gikurikizwa: ko nta muntu uzigera ashaka gucapisha iyi nyandiko y'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu atabanje kubona urugero rucapishijwe ku Kinyamakuru cya Vatikani, kandi ko ishusho y'urwo rugero igomba gukurikizwa nta mupaka uhindurwa, n'akanyarenge gato ka kinyandiko katagomba guhindurwa, kongerwaho, cyangwa gukurwamo, keretse mu gihe hari ikigaragara neza ko ari ikosa ry'igicapo.

Niba umucapyi uwo ari we wese wo mu bwami ubwo ari bwo bwose, imijyi, intara, n'ahantu hose, haba hagengwa n'ubutware mu by'isi bwa Kiliziya yacu Ntagatifu y'i Roma cyangwa hadagengwa, azigera ashaka mu buryo ubwo ari bwo bwose gucapa, kugurisha, gushyira ku isoko, cyangwa ukundi gutangaza cyangwa gukwirakwiza iyi nyandiko y'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu mu gihe cy'imyaka icumi yavuzwe, cyangwa nyuma y'imyaka icumi, mu buryo butandukanye n'uko buhuje n'urwo rugero nk'uko byavuzwe haruguru; cyangwa niba umucuruzi w'ibitabo uwo ari we wose azigera, nyuma y'itariki y'izi nyandiko, gucuruza, gushyira ku isoko, cyangwa gukwirakwiza ibitabo byacapishijwe by'iyi nyandiko, cyangwa ibitabo bigomba gucapishwa, bitandukanye mu kintu icyo ari cyo cyose n'Inyandiko yavuzwe yasubijwe kandi ikosowe, cyangwa byacapishijwe n'undi utari umucapyi wa Vatikani mu gihe cy'imyaka icumi: azagerwaho, usibye gutakaza ibitabo byose n'ibindi bihano by'igihe bihabwa ku bushake bwacu, n'urubanza rwa gusezererwa mu Kiliziya nini ruhita rumugeraho; ariko ntashobora gusezererwamo keretse na Papa w'i Roma, uretse igihe ari mu kaga k'urupfu.

Nuko rero dutegeka abayobozi bose n'umwe umwe muri bo, Abapatiriyariki, Abepiskopi Bakuru, Abepiskopi, n'abandi batware b'amatorero n'ahantu, ndetse n'ab'amategeko y'abihaye Imana, ko bareba kandi bakagenzura ko izi nyandiko zikurikizwa burundu n'iteka ryose n'abantu bose mu matorero yabo n'ubutware bwabo. Bashyire mu mwanya abanyuranyi bakoresheje ibihano by'ubutegetsi bwa Kiliziya n'izindi nzira z'amategeko n'ibikorwa bikwiriye, bateretse ubujurire, basaba kandi, nibiba bikenewe, ubufasha bw'ubutegetsi bw'isi, nubwo amategeko n'amabwiriza ya Papa, n'amategeko n'imigenzo by'amakoraniro rusange, y'intara, cyangwa y'urwego, haba rusange cyangwa by'umwihariko, n'ayo amatorero ayo ari yo yose, amategeko y'ubuhaye Imana, amashyirahamwe, za seminari na za kaminuza, ndetse n'amashuri rusange, yashimangijwe n'indahiro, icyemezo cya Papa cyangwa undi mwanya w'imbaraga uwo ari wo wose, n'imitako, impushya, n'inyandiko za Papa zatanzwe cyangwa zigomba gutangwa zinyuranye muri ubu buryo ubwo ari bwo bwose: ibyo byose tubikuraho binyuze muri uyu mugambi by'ingirakamaro kandi dutegeka ko bikuwemo.

Dushaka kandi ko amakopi y'izi nyandiko, ndetse n'iyo zacapishijwe mu bitabo ubwabyo, ahose mu rubanza no hanze yarwo, zemerwa nk'uko izi nyandiko ubwazo zazemerwa, iyo zigaragajwe cyangwa zerekanywe.

Byatanzwe i Roma, kuri Mutagatifu Petero, munsi y'Impeta y'Umurezi, ku munsi wa 9 Ugushyingo 1592, mu mwaka wa mbere w'Ubupapa bwacu.

M. VESTRIUS BARBIANUS.
