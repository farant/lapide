Commentaries on Sacred Scripture by the Reverend Father Cornelius a Lapide, of the Society of Jesus, formerly Professor of Sacred Scripture at Louvain, afterwards at Rome. Accurately revised and illustrated with notes by Joseph Maximilian Peronne, Honorary Canon and Parish Dean of the Diocese of Soissons, formerly Professor of Sacred Scripture.

New Edition, carefully purged of the errors which had crept into the former one.

Volume the Thirteenth, containing the literal and moral exposition of Daniel, Hosea, Joel, and Amos.

Paris. Published by Ludovicus Vives, Bookseller-Editor, 13, the street commonly called Delambre, 13. 1891.
