Dusoma ibitabo by'Abahanuzi n'iby'Intumwa kugira ngo twibutse ukwizera kwacu, duhozwe n'ibyiringiro byacu, kandi twishishikarizwe urukundo rwacu, duhuza amajwi yacu; kandi uwo muryango, nk'impanda yo mu ijuru, ukadutura mu gusinzira kw'iki gihe gipfa, akadutera guharanira igihembo cy'umuhamagaro wo mu ijuru.
