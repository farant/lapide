Mose yinjira akenshi kandi akava mu ihema, kandi uwo imbere yigarurirwa no gutekereranya, hanze aremererwa n'ibibazo by'intege nke; imbere atekerereza ibanga ry'Imana, hanze atwara umutwaro w'abantu b'umubiri, atanga urugero ku bayobozi: ko igihe bafite amakenga hanze ku byo bagomba guteganya, bagomba kubanza kubaza Uhoraho mu isengesho.

— Mutagatifu Gregori, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ijambo ry'Ibanze n'Ishimwe ry'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu — « Ishimwe rya Mose mu Byanditswe Bitagatifu no mu Bakurambere ba Kiliziya »
