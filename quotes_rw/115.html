Imana yabahaye izina rimwe, kugira ngo abashakanye bamenye ko ari nk'umuntu umwe mu mibiri ibiri, kandi ko bagomba guhuza mu mutima no mu bushake, nk'uko bahujwe n'izina. Izina rya Adamu ribaburira ko ari abana b'ubutaka — banyotewe, babumbwe mu ibumba, bafite ubugoyigoyi, abapfa, kandi ko bazasubira mu butaka. Ibuka, Adamu, ko uri adama, ni ukuvuga ubutaka n'umukungugu, kandi ko mu mukungugu uzasubira.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya V — « Umurongo wa 2: Yabise Adamu »
