Umuntu udafite icyo atunze ntashobora gufatirwa ibintu bye — keretse wenda ukeneye izi mpagarara zanjye zishaje kandi zangirika, n'izi bitabo bike, ari byo ubutunzi bwanjye bwose n'ibikoresho byanjye byose bigizwe na byo. Naho kwimurwa, simbizi, kuko ntabwo ndi mu gihugu na kimwe runaka; n'iki gihugu ntuyemo ubu sinacyita icyanjye, kandi aho ndi hose, ndahabara nk'ahanjye; cyangwa, kuvuga ukuri kurushaho, nzi ko isi yose ari iy'Imana, kandi njyewe ndi umunyamahanga n'umugenzi kuri yo.

— Mutagatifu Bazili Mukuru, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibanze — Urwandiko rw'Ishimwe
