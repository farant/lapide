Ubu buhanga nyabwo kandi bw'ukuri ntibwigishwa no gusoma, ahubwo n'ugusigwa; ntibwigishwa n'inyuguti, ahubwo n'umwuka; ntibwigishwa n'ubuhanga, ahubwo no gukurikiza amategeko y'Uhoraho. Urihenze, urihenze, niba wibwira ko uzabona mu barimu b'isi ibyo abigishwa ba Kristu bonyine — ni ukuvuga abasuzugura isi — bagera kuri byo ku bw'impano y'Imana.

— Mutagatifu Berinardo, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ijambo ry'Ibanze n'Ishimwe ry'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu — « Igice cya V: Ku Bitegurwa Bisabwa kuri Iri Giga »
