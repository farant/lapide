Umugore yaremwe n'Umuremyi ashobora kugira ingeso nziza kimwe n'umugabo. Kuko si inyama gusa zakoreshejwe mu kubaka umugore, ahubwo n'igufa ry'amagufwa ye; bivuze ko twe abagore tugomba gusubiza Nyagasani, nta kundi kuruta abagabo, gukomera mu kwizera no kwihangana, ndetse no kwihangana mu makuba.

— Yulita, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya II — « Ubu nguyu igufa ry'amagufwa yanjye »
