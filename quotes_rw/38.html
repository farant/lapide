Umwepiskopi agomba kuba umumalayika, udashobora kugerwaho n'ibibazo cyangwa amakosa y'abantu. Bikwiye ko ugira ubutegetsi ku bandi yihariye icyubahiro cy'ingeso nziza ku buryo, nk'izuba, arusheho kubengerana kurusha abandi bose, nk'imirasire mito y'inyenyeri, mu kubengerana kwe bwite.

— Mutagatifu Krizostomo, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ijambo ry'Ibanze n'Ishimwe ry'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu — « Ingeso Nziza za Mose »
