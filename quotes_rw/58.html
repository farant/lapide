Ishusho mu by'ukuri ishobora gutwikwa muri Gehinomu, ariko ntishobora guherera; ishobora kuraka, ariko ntishobora kurimburwa. Ikimeze si ko bimeze; ahubwo kiguma mu beza, cyangwa niba ubugingo bukora icyaha, gihindurwa mu buryo bw'agahinda, bukamera nk'inyamaswa zidafite ubwenge.

— Mutagatifu Berinardo, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya I — « Dureme Umuntu mu Ishusho Yacu no mu Kimeze Cyacu »
