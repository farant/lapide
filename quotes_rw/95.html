Umubyibumbe aha uhuzwa n'amahwa, cyane cyane utangariza mu ijwi rirenga uti: Ibintu bibanezeza, namwe bantu, bivanze n'imiborogo. Kuko mu by'ukuri mu by'abantu nta kintu kidafite ikindi, ahubwo umubabaro umanikwa ku byishimo, ubupfakazi ku bukwe, amaganya ku gurera abana, icyisoni ku bwiza bw'ubugingo, igihombo ku ntsinzi, kurambirwa ku binezeza, uburwayi ku buzima. Umubyibumbe ni mwiza rwose, ariko undereka intuntu. Buri gihe mbonye uru rurabyo, nibuka icyaha cyanjye, icyatumye ubutaka buvumwa kuzamera amahwa n'ibyatsi by'urushinge.

— Mutagatifu Bazili Mukuru, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya III — « Ubutaka Buvumwe mu Murimo Wawe »
