Urupfu ruzaba ineza kuri njye: ruzandoherereza vuba Imana, ari yo mbereho kandi ari yo nkorera, kandi urupfu rwayo ndarupfuye ku mugabane munini, kandi ari yo maze igihe kirekire nyihutira. Umuriro n'inkota, inyamaswa z'inkazi n'inzara zitanyaguza umubiri, ni ibyishimo n'umunezero kuri twe aho kuba ubwoba. Nuko rero, nimutwivuze, mudutereze ubwoba, mukore ibyo mushaka byose, mwishimire ubushobozi bwanyu; nibura n'Umwami yumve ibi — ntimuzigera mudutsinda, kandi ntimuzigera mubigeraho ko twemera inyigisho y'ubugome, naho mwadukangisha ibintu bibi kurushaho ibi.

— Mutagatifu Bazili Mukuru, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibanze — Urwandiko rw'Ishimwe
