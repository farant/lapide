Ibintu bitatu tugomba kuzirikana mu gikorwa gikomeye cy'isi, ni ukuvuga iki ari cyo, uko kimeze, n'icyagiremwe. Kandi mu kubaho kw'ibintu ubwakwo, ububasha butagereranywa burashimwa; mu buryo bwabyo, ubuhanga budasanzwe burabonerana; ariko nutekereza ku mpamvu yabyo byaremerewe, hagaragara ubuntu bufitiye akamaro, akamaro gafitiye ubuntu, ku buryo bushobora gutsinda n'uw'ubutaha bwose ku bwinshi n'ubunini bw'ibikorwa byiza. Mu by'ukuri, ku bubasha bwinshi biturutse mu busa, mu bwiza bwinshi biturutse mu buhanga, mu buntu bwinshi butanga akamaro, ibintu byose byaremewe.
