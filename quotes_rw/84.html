Mu magambo y'Igiheburayo avuga umugabo n'umugore harimo izina ry'Imana ryagufi, Yah, ari yo yashyize mu mwanya w'ishyingiranwa; kandi igihe cyose iryo zina rikomeza kuba mu ishyingiranwa — kandi rikomeza igihe abashakanye batinya Imana kandi bakundana — ni ho Imana iba ihari kandi ihezagira ubwo bumwe. Ariko nibanga kandi bibagirwa Imana, abashakanye bata iryo zina; kandi inyuguti zigize Yah nimavaho, ibisigara mu mugabo n'umugore ni esh na esh, ni ukuvuga umuriro n'umuriro — ari wo muriro w'amakimbirane n'amaganya muri ubu buzima, kandi mu bundi buzima, umuriro w'iteka ryose.

— R. Aburahamu ben Ezira, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya II — « Ubu nguyu igufa ry'amagufwa yanjye »
