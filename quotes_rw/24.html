Nk'uko ingano y'izahabu, ifeza, n'imyambaro ubwoko bw'Abahebureyi bwitwaje buvuye muri Misiri iri munsi y'ubutunzi bahawe nyuma i Yeruzalemu, cyane cyane mu gihe cya Salomo, ni ko ubumenyi bwose, ndetse n'ubwiza, buvanwe mu bitabo by'Abanyamahanga, niba bugereranijwe n'ubumenyi bw'Ibyanditswe by'Imana: kuko icyo umuntu yize ahandi cyose, niba ari kibi, kihanwa muri byo; kandi igihe umuntu asangamo ibintu byose yize ahandi mu buryo bwiza, azasanga byinshi cyane ibidashobora kuboneka ahandi na rimwe, ahubwo bigishwa mu burebure n'ubwicisha bugufi butangaje bw'ibyo Byanditswe gusa.

— Mutagatifu Agusitini, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ijambo ry'Ibanze n'Ishimwe ry'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu — « Igice cya II: Ku Cyo Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu Bivugaho n'Ubugari Bwabyo »
