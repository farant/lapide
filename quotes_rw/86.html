Dido, amaze guteranya zahabu n'ifeza nyinshi, yagendanye ubwato kugera muri Afurika maze yubaka Karitago; kandi igihe yasabwaga gushyingirwa na Hiyarabasi, umwami wa Libiya, yubatse igitereko cy'umuriro mu bwibutso bw'umugabo we Sikeyusi wari warapfuye, maze yitera mu muriro, akunda gushya aho gushyingirwa undi. Umugore w'ingeso nziza ni we wubatse Karitago; kandi na none uwo mugi wrangiye mu gushimira ubwere. Kuko umugore wa Hazidirubali, igihe Karitago yafatwaga ikongezwamo umuriro, abonye ko agiye gufatwa n'Abanyaroma, afata abana be babiri bato, umwe mu kiganza kimwe n'undi mu kindi, yiteye mu muriro wari ukaka munsi y'inzu ye bwite.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya II — « Ni yo mpamvu umugabo azasiga se na nyina »
