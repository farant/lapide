Ibigeragezo ni ubwoko butandukanye: ubwa mbere ni ubw'amagorwa, bumanika budasoni; ubwa kabiri ni ubw'amakenga, butwikira ubwenge mu gihu cy'amashitani; ubwa gatatu ni ubw'ibihutirwa, biba mbere y'uko ubwenge buca urubanza; ubwa kane ni ubw'ibihishwe, bihungabanya urutonde rw'imigambi; ubwa gatanu ni ubw'ibikomereye, biruta imbaraga zacu; ubwa gatandatu ni ubw'uburiganya, bushukashuka ubwenge; ubwa karindwi ni ubw'amahane, butangirwa n'inzira nyinshi zitandukanye.

— Mutagatifu Berinardo, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya III — « Inzoka Yari Inyaryenge Kurusha Ibinyabuzima Byose »
