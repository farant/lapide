Ibibi byinshi bikorwa, nyamara inyenyeri ntiziyobagura hejuru, zihamye mu ijuru, zigenda mu nzira z'ijuru Umuremyi wazo yategetse kandi yashyizeho: ni ko Abatagatifu bagomba kuba, ariko gusa iyo imitima yabo ihamye mu ijuru. Abo rero bari mu bijo, batekereza ku byo mu ijuru, baturuka muri izo tekereza z'ibyo mu ijuru bagahinduka abihangana. Kandi icyakorerwa ku isi ntibakigira imbere, kugeza igihe barangije urugendo rwabo. Kuko umuntu wese wataye kwihangana yaguye avuye mu ijuru.

— Mutagatifu Agusitini, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya I — « Imana Irema Imirasire Ibiri Ikomeye »
