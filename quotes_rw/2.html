Yibwiraga ko yakwishimira cyane aramutse ashoboye gutanga ikibazo gikwiriye ku Munsi wa Nyagasani ku bw'ubusho buto bwashyikirijwe; kuko icyo gihe hazasabwa konti ikomeye kurushaho ku bw'ubugingo bwitaweho neza no ku mafaranga yakoreshejwe neza, kuruta uko abantu bisanzwe babitekereza cyangwa bashaka kubitekereza.

— Yohani Fisher, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibanze — Urwandiko rw'Ishimwe
