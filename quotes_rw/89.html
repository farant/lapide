Ugomba kwigenza mu gikorwa cyose no mu bitekerezo byose nk'aho ugiye gupfa uyu munsi cyangwa vuba aha. Iyo bukeye, utekereze ko ushobora kudasoboka kugera ku mugoroba; kandi iyo bugorobye, ntiwiyemeze ko uzabona igitondo. Ube rero witeguye igihe cyose, kandi ubeho mu buryo urupfu rutazigera rugusanga utiteguye. Mu gihe ufite umwanya, witoranyirize ubutunzi budashira; utekereze gusa ku gakiza kawe; witwararike ibintu by'Imana gusa; wibike nk'umusuhuke n'umushyitsi kuri iyi si; ugumane umutima wawe uboneye kandi wunamutseho Imana, kuko hano nta mugi uhoraho ufite.

— Tomasi a Kempisi, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya III — « Oya, Ntimuzapfa Rwose »
