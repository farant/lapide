Adamu arasinzira, kugira ngo Hawa aremwe; Kristu arapfa kugira ngo Kiliziya iremwe. Igihe Adamu asinziriye, Hawa aremwa mu rubavu rwe; igihe Kristu apfuye, urubavu rwe rucishwa icumu, kugira ngo Amasakramentu atembe, ayo Kiliziya iremwamo.

— Mutagatifu Agusitini, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya II — « Uhoraho Imana ateza ibitotsi bikomeye kuri Adamu »
