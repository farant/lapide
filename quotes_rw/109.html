Ni ko Mutagatifu Kiristina, isugi n'umumaritiri, amaze kumena ibishushanyo bya zahabu bya se Uribanusi, yasuzuguye ibyifuzo bye byiza n'ubushake bukomeye, yitwaye ibihano bye; ntiyahindukijwe n'inkoni cyangwa amashoka ngo ahindure ukomera kwe; ahubwo, atera igice cy'inyama ye yaciwemo imbere ya se, aravuga ati: « Ihage ku nyama, mubi we — ku nyama wabyaye; ushobora kurya umukobwa wawe, ariko ntushobora rwose kumutera kwemera ububisha bwawe. » Dore uko ubushake bukomeye bugenga ibyiyumvo, imibabaro, abagome, n'urupfu.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya IV — « Niba Ukora Neza »
