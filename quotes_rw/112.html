Hari ibyaha bine bikomeye cyane, mu rurimi rw'Ibyanditswe, bitakira mu ijuru: icya mbere, kwica umuvandimwe, nk'icya Kayini; icya kabiri, icyaha cya Sodomu; icya gatatu, ibihembo by'abakozi byibiwe; icya kane, gutoteza abapfakazi, imfubyi, n'abakene.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya IV — « Ijwi ry'Amaraso »
