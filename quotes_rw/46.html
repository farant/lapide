Isi ubwayo, mu guhinduka kwayo gukurikirana neza no mu kugenda kwayo, no mu ishusho nziza cyane y'ibintu byose bigaragara, itangaza mu buryo bw'ubucece ko yaremewe kandi ko idashoboraga kurengwa keretse n'Imana, Yo ari nkuru ku buryo butagereranywa kandi butagaragara, nziza ku buryo butagereranywa kandi butagaragara.
