Ikuzimu ry'amagambo yawe ritangaje, Nyagasani, ryo imbere yayo, dore, iri imbere yacu, rinezeza abana bato; ariko ikuzimu ryayo ritangaje, Mana yanjye, ikuzimu ritangaje; bitera ubwoba kuritegereza: ubwoba bw'icyubahiro, n'umushyitsi w'urukundo.

— Mutagatifu Agusitini, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ijambo ry'Ibanze n'Ishimwe ry'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu — « Abakurambere ba Kiliziya barwanira Isezerano rya Kera »
