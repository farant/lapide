Icumbi ryanjye ryonyine — ari ryo rindushira kandi nkunda kuruta isi yose, kandi rikambera nk'ijuru ku isi — n'ubuceceke bwonyine ni byo mbamo; ntuye mu cumbi ryanjye, mpita cyane mu busomero bwanjye butagatifu, nkagerageza kuba umwenzi w'ijuru; nkurikira ubwihurizo, nyamara n'umurimo, w'itekereraniro ritagatifu, gusoma, no kwandika. Nitangira Imana, Imwe kandi Eshatu, kugira ngo nakire, ntekerezeho, kandi mpimbaze amagambo n'ibyahishuriwe na Yo; nicara ku birenge bya Kristu, mpanze ku minwa ye ngo nyoherwe amagambo y'ubugingo, kugira ngo mbisuke ku bandi.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Ibanze — Ubuzima bwa Cornelius a Lapide
