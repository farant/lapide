Rinda, yewe Hawa, ibyo wahawe; utegereze ibyo wasezeranyijwe; wirinde ibyo wabujijwe, kugira ngo utabura ibyo wahawe. Kuki urabana ubwitonzi urupfu rwawe? Mu gihe amaso yawe arebye ahandi, inzoka mu gihe cyo yinjira mu mutima wawe mu ibanga, ikakubwira mu magambo ashimishije; ikunesheshera imbabazi, ikakugoreka mu binyoma ikakuvana ubwoba; igukurira umururumba mu gihe ikwinjiriza irari; iteza amatsiko mu gihe ishyira imbere icyifuzo; hanyuma ikaguha ibyo wabujijwe ikagukura ibyo wahawe; igushyikira imbuto ikagusahura paradizo.

— Mutagatifu Berinardo, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya III — « Umugore Rero Abona »
