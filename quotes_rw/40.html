Mose, umugaragu wizerwa cyane w'Imana, wicishaga bugufi mu kwanga ubutumwa bukomeye cyane, wumviraga mu kubwakira, wizerwa mu kububungabunga, ukomeye mu kubusohoza, witonze mu buyobozi bw'ubwoko, ukaze mu gukosora, ushyuha mu rukundo, wihangana mu kwemera.
