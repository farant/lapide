Reba mu mubiri w'umuntu umurimo w'Umukoranyi: uko yashyizemo amagufwa nk'inkingi zishyigikira inyama kandi zikayitwara; hanyuma ko ku mpande zombi, ni ukuvuga iburyo n'ibumoso, urugero rumwe rurindwa, ku buryo ikirenge gihuye n'ikindi kirenge, ikiganza n'ikindi kiganza, n'intoki ku ntoki, kugira ngo buri kimwe gihuze n'icy'ahandi mu kungana kuzuye. Ndetse n'ijisho ku rindi jisho, ugutwi ku kundi gutwi, byubatswe mu bunyacyaha n'ubwumvikane hagati yabyo, ariko kandi bikwiriye n'imikoreshereze ikenewe.

— Mutagatifu Klementi, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Ibisobanuro ku Ntangiriro, Igice cya II — « Uhoraho Imana Abumba Umuntu mu Ivumbi ry'Ubutaka »
