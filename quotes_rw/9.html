Mu ngoro y'Imana, buri wese atanga icyo ashoboye: bamwe batanga zahabu, ifeza n'amabuye y'igiciro cyinshi; abandi batanga imyenda myiza n'umweru n'umutuku n'ubururu; biratugendekera neza nitubasha gutanga impu z'intama n'ubwoya bw'ihene.

— Mutagatifu Jeromi, muri Cornelius a Lapide, Prologus Galeatus
