Cornelius a Lapide

Ibisobanuro ku Byanditswe Bitagatifu

Cornelius a Lapide (Cornelius Cornelissen van den Steen, 1567–1637) yari umusobanura Bibiliya w'Umunyabeligika wo mu Muryango wa Yezu, wavukiye i Bocholt mu Bushumba bwa Liege. Yigishije Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu muri Kaminuza ya Louvain mu myaka irenga makumyabiri mbere y'uko ahamagarirwa i Roma, aho yamazeho ubuzima bwe bwose bwasigaye yandika. Ibisobanuro bye birimo hafi ibitabo byose by'Ibyanditswe Bitagatifu kandi bimenyekana ku buhanga bwabyo butangaje, bihuza ibitekerezo by'Abakurambere ba Kiliziya, iby'ibihe byo hagati n'iby'igihe cye mu bisobanuro birambuye. Ibi bisobanuro bigumaho mu bisobanuro bya Bibiliya bya Gatolika bikoresha cyane, cyane cyane bikubahwa n'abigisha kubera ubutunzi bw'imikoreshereze y'umuco n'ingero.
