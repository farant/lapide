Commentaries on Sacred Scripture of the Reverend Father Cornelius a Lapide

Commentaries on Sacred Scripture

Of the Reverend Father Cornelius a Lapide, of the Society of Jesus, formerly Professor of Sacred Scripture at Louvain, afterwards at Rome. Carefully revised and illustrated with notes by Joseph Maximilian Peronne, Honorary Canon and Parish Dean of the Diocese of Soissons, formerly Professor of Sacred Scripture.

New edition, carefully purged of the errors which had crept into the previous one.

Volume Twelve: On Jeremiah, Lamentations, Baruch, and Ezekiel.

Paris, at the shop of Ludovicus Vives, Bookseller and Publisher, 13 Rue Delambre, 13. 1891.
