Chineke bụ Onye ahụ nke, mgbe a kpọrọ aha Ya, a na-apụghị ịkọwa Ya; mgbe a na-atụle Ya, a na-apụghị ịtụle Ya; mgbe a na-akọwa Ya, Ọ na-etolite site n'ịkọwa ahụ n'onwe ya; n'ihi na Ọ ji aka Ya kpuchie eluigwe, Ọ ji ikpere Ya mechie gburu gburu ụwa niile: Onye ihe niile na-amaghị, ma site n'ịtụ egwu ha na-amata Ya.

— Nsọ Gregori nke Nazịanzọs, n'ime Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi nke Mbụ — "Chineke (Elohim): Nkọwapụta Iri na Atọ"
