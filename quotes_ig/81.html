Adam na-arahụ ụra, ka e mee Iiv; Kraịst nwụrụ ka e mee Ụka. Mgbe Adam na-arahụ ụra, e si n'akụkụ ya mee Iiv; mgbe Kraịst nwụrụ anwụ, e ji ube mapuo akụkụ Ya, ka Saakrament ndị ahụ si na ya asọpụta, ndị e ji kee Ụka.

— Nsọ Augustin, n'ime Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi Nke Abụọ — "Onyenweanyị Chineke Mee Ka Ụra Dị Omimi Daa Adam"
