Ọlaọcha bụ ịchawapụta nke okwu maọbụ nke amamihe; akwara ndị ahụ bụ Akwụkwọ Nsọ, dịka a ga-asị n'ụzọ doro anya: Onye na-akwadebe onwe ya maka okwu nkwusa eziokwu ga-esite n'ibe akwụkwọ nsọ ndị ahụ wepụta isi ihe nke arụmụka ya; ka ọ wee tụgharịa ihe niile ọ na-ekwu laghachi n'isi ntọala nke ikike Chineke, wee wukwasị ụlọ okwu ya nke ọma n'elu ya.

— Nsọ Gregori, n'ime Cornelius a Lapide, Prooemium et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Isi Nke Mbụ: "Banyere Ịdị Mma, Mkpa, na Mkpụrụ nke Akwụkwọ Nsọ"
