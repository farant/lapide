Ọnwụ sitere na Adam, ndụ sitere na Kraịst; agwọ duhiere Iiv, Marịa kwenyere Gebriel; mana ịduhie nke Iiv wetara ọnwụ, nkwenye nke Marịa mụtaara ụwa Onye Nzọpụta. Site na Marịa ka a tọghachiri ihe furu efu site na Iiv; site na Kraịst ka a gbapụtara ihe e doro n'agha site na Adam; site na Gebriel ka e kwere nkwa ihe a tụfuru olileanya ya site na ekwensu.

— Nsọ Jọn Krisọstọm, na Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa na Jenesis, Isi Nke Atọ — "Ọ Ga-Azọpịa Isi Gị"
