Mmadụ nwere ụkwụ abụọ; n'ihi na anụ ọhịa na anụmanụ nwere ụkwụ anọ, nnụnụ nwere ụkwụ abụọ. Ya mere mmadụ dịka otu n'ime ihe ndị nwere nku, onye ji anya ya na-achọ elu, na-efe efe site n'elu echiche; ya mere a na-ekwu banyere ya: "A ga-eme ka ịdị okorobịa gị dị ka nke ugo," n'ihi na ọ nọ nso ihe ndị eluigwe karịa ma dị elu karịa ugo ndị nwere ike ikwu: "Ebe obibi anyị dị n'eluigwe."

— Nsọ Ambroz, n'ime Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi Nke Abụọ — "Onyenweanyị Chineke Kpụrụ Mmadụ site n'Ite Aja nke Ụwa"
