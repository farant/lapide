Ya mere Chineke kere ụwa maka nke a: nke mbụ, iji kwadooro mmadụ ụlọeze, n'ezie alaeze; nke abụọ, iji nyefee ya ebe nlele ihe niile, na paradaịs nke ụtọ niile dị iche iche; nke atọ, iji nye ya akwụkwọ nke ya ga-eji hụ na gụọ banyere Onye kere ya.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi nke Mbụ — "Chineke (Elohim): Nkọwapụta Iri na Atọ"
