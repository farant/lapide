Adam ezereghị nche ichekwa onwe ya pụọ n'aghụghọ agwọ. Ọ bụ onye nkwụsị; o nweghị ụzụ megide ekwensu. N'ihi na o kwetara na ekwensu onye dụrụ ya ọdụ kama Onyenweanyị onye yiri ya egwu, wee tufuo ndụ nke o nwere, nata ọnwụ nke ọ na-amaghị.

— Nsọ Jọn Krisọstọm, n'ime Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi Nke Abụọ — "N'Osisi Ihe Ọmụma, Erila"
