Amaara m naanị otu ihe ga-eguzo n'akụkụ Oziọma: nleta mbụ nke ugwu Alps n'otu n'ime oge ndị ahụ nke snoo, eluigwe, anyanwụ, ahịhịa ndụ, na onyinyo nyere onwe ha ịdị n'otu zuru oke. Onye na-akwụsị, mkpu na-esipụ. Otu a ka ọ dị na Oziọma; ọ na-akwụsị gị ma mee ka ị tiku mkpu.

— Fadaa Lacordaire, Banyere Ife Jizọs Kraịst n'Akwụkwọ Nsọ
