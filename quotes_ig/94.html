Nsọ Hilarịọn, na-atụ egwu n'ọnwụ, gwara onwe ya sị: "Ị jere ozi Onyenweanyị ihe fọrọ nke nta ka ọ bụrụ iri afọ asaa, ma ị na-atụ egwu ịnwụ?" Abọt Pambọ, na-anwụ, kwuru sị: "Ugbu a, ana m ala n'ebe Chineke m nọ; mana dị ka onye bidoro naanị ife Chineke n'eziokwu na n'ụzọ kwesịrị ekwesị." Arsenịọs kwuru sị: "Nye m, O Onyenweanyị, ka ọ dịkarịa ala ugbu a, ka m bidoo ibi ndụ n'ụzọ nsọ." Nsọ Fransịs nọ nso ọnwụ kwuru sị: "Ụmụnna m, ruo ugbu a anyị emeghị nnukwu ọganihu; ka anyị bido ugbu a ife Chineke." Antọni kwuru sị: "Taa, were ya na ị malitere ndụ nke okpukpe." Agatọ, onye biri ndụ nsọ, na-ekwu sị: "Ana m atụ egwu ọnwụ, n'ihi na ikpe Chineke dị iche na nke ndị mmadụ."

— Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa na Jenesis, Isi Nke Atọ — "Ọ Ga-Azọpịa Isi Gị"
