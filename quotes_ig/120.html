Otu a ka Makariọs bi n'eluigwe na Chineke, ma na-asị onwe ya: "Ị nwere ndị Mmụọ Ozi, ndị Ịsị Mmụọ Ozi, ike eluigwe niile, ndị Cherubim na Serafim, Chineke onye kere ihe ndị a niile; biri ebe ahụ, erịdatala n'okpuru eluigwe, adala n'echiche ụwa."

— Nsọ Makariọs, na Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi Nke Ise — "Ịnọk Soro Chineke Jee Ije"
