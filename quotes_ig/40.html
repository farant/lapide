Mosis, odibo Chineke kacha kwesị ntụkwasị obi, onye dị umeala n'ịjụ ọrụ dị ukwuu dị otú a, onye na-erubere isi n'ịnata ya, onye kwesịrị ntụkwasị obi n'ichekwa ya, onye siri ike n'ime ya, onye nche n'ịchị ndị mmadụ, onye siri ike n'ịdọ aka ná ntị, onye na-ekpo ọkụ n'ịhụnanya, onye na-enwe ndidi n'ịnagide.

— Nsọ Augustin, n'ime Cornelius a Lapide, Prooemium et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Otuto Mosis site n'Akwụkwọ Nsọ na Ndị Nna Ụka
