Saladịn, eze Ijipt na Sịrịa, onye napụrụ ndị Kraịst Ala Nsọ, mgbe ọ nọ nso ịnwụ, nyere iwu ka e buru ọkọlọtọ nwere akwa olili site n'ogige agha ya niile, ka onye ozi na-ekwupụta sị: "Nke a bụ ihe niile Saladịn, onye ọchịchị Sịrịa na Ijipt, ga-esite n'alaeze ya niile buru n'aka ya ugbu a."

— Cornelius a Lapide, Nkọwa banyere Jenesis, Isi Nke Abụọ — "N'Osisi Ihe Ọmụma, Erila"
