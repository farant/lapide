Fuit enim ipse innocens in domo patris, patiens in adversis, fidelis in servitio, castus in tentatione, sapiens in occultorum reseratione, prudens in futurorum provisione, justus in fratrum castigatione, et nunc pius in ipsorum susceptione.

— Cornelius a Lapide, Commentarium in Genesim, Caput XLIII — "Versus 23"
