# Chinese (Mandarin)

Use 书面语 (formal written Mandarin baihua). File suffix `_zh`. Simplified Chinese (简体字), LTR (no `dir` attribute needed). `lang="zh"` on the `<html>` tag.

## Biblical proper names

Follow 思高本 (Studium Biblicum / Catholic) tradition: 梅瑟 (Moses), 亚巴郎 (Abraham), 雅各伯 (Jacob), 依撒格 (Isaac), 亚郎 (Aaron), 达味 (David), 若苏厄 (Joshua), 依撒意亚 (Isaiah), 耶肋米亚 (Jeremiah), 亚当 (Adam), 厄娃 (Eve), 诺厄 (Noah).

**Biblical book names** likewise follow 思高本: 创世纪, 出谷纪, 肋未纪, 户籍纪, 申命纪, 若苏厄书, 民长纪, 卢德传, 列王纪, 编年纪, 圣咏 (Psalms), 箴言, 训道篇, 雅歌, 智慧篇, 德训篇, 依撒意亚, 耶肋米亚, 厄则克耳, 达尼尔, 玛加伯, 多俾亚传, 友弟德传, 艾斯德尔传, 约伯传, 默示录 (Revelation).

The 思高本 convention was chosen because it is the standard Catholic Chinese Bible used in liturgy, catechesis, and theological writing — consistent with the Catholic saint names and theological terminology used throughout.

圣热罗尼莫 (St. Jerome), 圣巴西略 (St. Basil), 圣奥思定 (St. Augustine), 圣盎博罗削 (St. Ambrose), 圣金口若望 (Chrysostom), 圣额我略 (Gregory), 圣伯尔纳铎 (Bernard), 圣依拉略 (Hilary), 圣西彼廉 (Cyprian), 圣犹斯定 (Justin), 圣克莱孟 (Clement), 圣亚大纳削 (Athanasius), 圣厄丕法尼 (Epiphanius), 圣依纳爵 (Ignatius), 大马士革的圣若望 (John of Damascus), 圣文德 (Bonaventure), 圣方济各 (Francis), 圣多明我 (Dominic), 圣多玛斯 (Thomas), 圣良 (Leo), 圣保禄 (Paul), 圣厄弗冷 (Ephrem).

## Quotation marks

Use 「...」for standard quotations and 『...』for nested quotes or Scripture titles.

## Theological terminology

圣经 (Holy Scripture), 圣事 (sacrament), 恩宠 (grace), 罪 (sin), 救恩 (salvation), 教父 (Church Father), 福音 (Gospel), 创世纪 (Genesis), 出谷纪 (Exodus).

## Bible references and psalm numbering

Gospel references use 思高本 convention (e.g., 「若望福音 8:12」, 「玛窦福音 5:3」, 「路加福音 24:27」, 「马尔谷福音 1:1」).

## Register

Formal scholarly Chinese suitable for theological commentary — 书面语, avoiding colloquial expressions (口语). Proper character usage is critical — agents must use Simplified Chinese characters consistently and avoid Traditional character substitutions.

## Agent pitfalls

- **Western-style quotes**: Agents must be explicitly instructed to use 「...」quotation marks (not English "..." or Chinese ""), as they default to Western-style quotes.
- **Traditional character substitutions**: Agents may mix Traditional Chinese characters into Simplified Chinese text.
- **Protestant Bible names**: Agents may use Protestant 和合本 names (摩西, 亚伯拉罕) instead of Catholic 思高本 names (梅瑟, 亚巴郎). Always verify against the 思高本 list.
