    The Hebrew proper name of the prophet Obadiah, meaning "Servant of God." Jerome provides the etymology in his survey of the prophets.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome glosses Obadiah's name as meaning "servant of God"
      "Obadiah, whose name means servant of God, thunders against Edom, the bloody and earthly man"
