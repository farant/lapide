    עָלַם

    Transliteration
    alam
    Meaning
    to hide, to conceal

    Hebrew root verb meaning "to hide" or "to conceal." Lapide cites it as the etymological root of *olam* ("eternal"), explaining that "eternal" in Hebrew signifies something whose end is hidden or concealed.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Root of olam, explaining the etymology of "eternal" as "hidden"
