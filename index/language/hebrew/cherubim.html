    Meaning
    Multitude of knowledge — Jerome's etymological interpretation in the context of the four Evangelists
    Root
    כרב

    Jerome interprets Cherubim as meaning "multitude of knowledge" while describing the four Evangelists as the true Cherubim.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome interprets Cherubim as meaning "multitude of knowledge" while describing the four Evangelists as the true Cherubim
      "Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John -- the four-horse chariot of the Lord and the true Cherubim, which is interpreted as 'multitude of knowledge' -- are covered with eyes throughout the whole body"
