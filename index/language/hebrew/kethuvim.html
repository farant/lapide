    כְּתוּבִים

    Transliteration
    kethuvim
    Old Hebrew
    𐤊𐤕𐤅𐤁𐤉𐤌
    Meaning
    Writings — the third division of the Hebrew canon (Hagiographa)
    Root
    כתב

    The third division of the Hebrew canon, meaning "Writings." Rendered in Greek as Hagiographa ("sacred writings").

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome identifies the Hagiographa as the third order of the Hebrew canon
      "The third order possesses the Hagiographa."
    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome notes that some write Ruth and Cinoth among the Hagiographa
      "Although some write Ruth and Cinoth among the Hagiographa and think these books should be counted in their own number"
