אֵלֶּה הַדְּבָרִים

Transliteration
elleh haddevarim
Old Hebrew
𐤀𐤋𐤄 𐤄𐤃𐤁𐤓𐤉𐤌
Meaning
These are the words — Hebrew name for the book of Deuteronomy
Root
דבר

The Hebrew name for the book of Deuteronomy, meaning "These are the words." Shares the root דבר with Vajedabber (Numbers).

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Helmeted Prologue P7 — Jerome identifies Elle Haddebarim as the Hebrew name for Deuteronomy
