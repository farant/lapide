    אֲדֹנָי

    Transliteration
    adonai
    Old Hebrew
    𐤀𐤃𐤍𐤉
    Meaning
    The divine name — God revealed this name to Moses but not to the earlier patriarchs
    Root
    אדן

    The divine name. Lapide notes that God revealed this name to Moses but not to the earlier patriarchs Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Praises Of Moses P16 — Lapide cites God's words distinguishing Moses's knowledge from that of the patriarchs
      "I appeared to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, and my name Adonai (Jehovah) I did not make known to them"
