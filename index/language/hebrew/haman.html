    Iniquity — Jerome's etymology of the name Haman in the context of the book of Esther

    Jerome provides the etymology of Haman's name as meaning "iniquity" in the context of the book of Esther.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome glosses Haman's name as meaning iniquity
      "Esther, in the type of the Church, frees the people from danger; and with Haman slain -- whose name means iniquity -- she sends the portions of the feast and the celebrated day to posterity."
