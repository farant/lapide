שָׁפָן

Transliteration
saphan
Meaning
coney, hare, or hedgehog — a general Hebrew animal name with different referents

Hebrew animal name that Lapide uses to illustrate Canon 29: Hebrew names for animals, trees, and stones are general and common to many species. *Saphan* signifies the coney in Leviticus 11:5, the hare in Proverbs 30:26, and the hedgehog in Psalm 104:18.
