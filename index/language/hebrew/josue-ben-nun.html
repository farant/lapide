יְהוֹשֻׁעַ בִּן נוּן

Transliteration
yehoshua bin nun
Old Hebrew
𐤉𐤄𐤅𐤔𐤏 𐤁𐤍 𐤍𐤅𐤍
Meaning
Joshua son of Nun — Hebrew name used for the book of Joshua and its author

The Hebrew name for Joshua son of Nun, used for the book of Joshua and its author.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Helmeted Prologue P9 — Jerome notes that Joshua son of Nave is called Josue ben Nun among the Hebrews
    "The second order they make of the Prophets, and they begin with Joshua the son of Nave, who among them is called Josue ben Nun."
