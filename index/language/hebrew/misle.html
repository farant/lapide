    The Hebrew name for the book of Proverbs, meaning "Parables."

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome identifies Misle as the Hebrew name for Proverbs, meaning "Parables"
      "The third is Solomon, having three books: Proverbs, which they call Misle, that is, Parables."
