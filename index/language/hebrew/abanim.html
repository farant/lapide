אֲבָנִים

Transliteration
abanim
Meaning
stones — connected etymologically by Lapide to banim (sons)

Hebrew word for "stones." Lapide connects it etymologically to *banim* ("sons"), explaining that families are built from sons as buildings are built from stones.
