שִׁיר הַשִּׁירִים

Transliteration
shir hashirim
Old Hebrew
𐤔𐤉𐤓 𐤄𐤔𐤉𐤓𐤉𐤌
Meaning
Song of Songs — Hebrew name for the Song of Solomon (Canticle of Canticles)
Root
שׁיר

The Hebrew name for the Song of Solomon (Canticle of Canticles), meaning "Song of Songs."

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Helmeted Prologue P23 — Jerome identifies Sir Hassirim as the Hebrew designation for the Song of Songs
