    שֵׁם הַמְפֹרָשׁ

    Transliteration
    shem hamephorash
    Old Hebrew
    𐤉𐤄𐤅𐤄
    Meaning
    The four-letter name of God (YHWH) — found in ancient Hebrew characters even in Greek manuscripts

    The four-letter name of God (YHWH). Jerome notes it was written in ancient Hebrew (paleo-Hebrew) characters even in certain Greek manuscripts.

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Helmeted Prologue P2 — Jerome reports that the tetragrammaton name of the Lord is found in certain Greek manuscripts still written in the ancient characters
      "And the tetragrammaton name of the Lord, in certain Greek manuscripts, is found expressed in the ancient letters even to this day."
