    The collective Hebrew designation for the five books of Moses (Pentateuch), meaning "the Law."

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome explains that the five books of Moses are properly called Torah, meaning the Law
      "These are the five books of Moses, which they properly call Torah, that is, the Law."
