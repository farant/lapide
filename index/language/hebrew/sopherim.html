סוֹפְרִים

Transliteration
sopherim
Old Hebrew
𐤎𐤐𐤓𐤉𐤌
Meaning
Scribes — Hebrew title for the ancient rabbis devoted to Sacred Scripture
Root
ספר

Hebrew title for the ancient rabbis who were wholly devoted to Sacred Scripture. Lapide explains that the rabbis were called sopherim (and in Greek, grammateis) because of their devotion to Scripture.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P12 — Lapide explains that the ancient Jewish rabbis were called sopherim (and in Greek, grammateis) because of their devotion to Sacred Scripture
    "The ancient Rabbis of the Jews were wholly devoted to Sacred Scripture; and from this they were called sopherim, grammateis, and Scribes."
