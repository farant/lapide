בָּנָה

Transliteration
bana
Meaning
he built

Hebrew root verb meaning "he built." Lapide cites it as the root from which *banim* ("sons") derives, explaining the metaphor that families are built from sons as buildings from stones.

References in Commentary

  • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Canons P11 — Root verb from which banim derives
    "from the root bana, that is, "he built"; for from sons as from stones the houses and families of parents are built"
