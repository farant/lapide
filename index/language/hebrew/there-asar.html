    תְּרֵי עָשָׂר

    Transliteration
    tere asar
    Old Hebrew
    𐤕𐤓𐤉 𐤏𐤔𐤓
    Meaning
    Twelve — Hebrew (Aramaic-influenced) designation for the book of the Twelve Minor Prophets

    The Hebrew (Aramaic-influenced) designation for the book of the Twelve Minor Prophets, meaning "Twelve."

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome identifies There Asar as the Hebrew name for the book of the Twelve Prophets
      "Eighth, the book of the Twelve Prophets, which among them is called There Asar."
