    In the beginning — Hebrew name for the book of Genesis

    The Hebrew name for the book of Genesis, meaning "In the beginning."

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome identifies Bereshith as the Hebrew name for Genesis
      "The first book among them is called Bereshith, which we call Genesis."
