    λόγος

    Transliteration
    logos
    Meaning
    Word, reason, reckoning, and the cause of each thing — Jerome explains the multiple meanings as applied to Christ

    The Greek word Logos, rich with multiple meanings. Jerome explains that it signifies word, reason, reckoning, and the cause through which all things subsist -- all of which are rightly understood in Christ.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome discusses the richness of the Greek word Logos, explaining it means word, reason, reckoning, and the cause through which all things subsist
      "For the Word (Logos) in Greek means many things: it is both word, and reason, and reckoning, and the cause of each thing through which all individual things that exist subsist -- all of which we rightly understand in Christ."
