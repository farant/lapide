γραμματεῖς

Transliteration
grammateis
Meaning
Scribes — the Greek equivalent of the Hebrew sopherim

The Greek word for "Scribes," the equivalent of the Hebrew sopherim. Used to designate the ancient rabbis who devoted themselves to Sacred Scripture.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P12 — Lapide gives grammateis as the Greek title alongside the Hebrew sopherim for the ancient rabbis
    "The ancient Rabbis of the Jews were wholly devoted to Sacred Scripture; and from this they were called sopherim, grammateis, and Scribes."
