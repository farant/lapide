διήγησις

Transliteration
diegesis
Meaning
Narration, exposition — the Greek reading of Ecclesiasticus where the Latin has 'discourse'

Greek word meaning "narration, exposition." Lapide notes the Greek reading of Ecclesiasticus where the Latin has "discourse," the Greek has diegesis (the narration, the exposition of famous men).

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P11 — Lapide cites the Greek term from Ecclesiasticus, glossing diegesis as "the narration, the exposition" of famous men
    "he will preserve the discourse (in Greek diegesis — the narration, the exposition) of famous men"
