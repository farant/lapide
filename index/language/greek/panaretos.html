    πανάρετος

    Transliteration
    panaretos
    Meaning
    All virtue — Greek epithet for the book of Ecclesiasticus (Sirach)

    Greek epithet for the book of Ecclesiasticus (Sirach), meaning "all virtue." Lapide explains this etymologically as signifying comprehensive moral wisdom.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter II: On the Object and Breadth of Sacred Scripture — Lapide explains the Greek title Panaretos for Ecclesiasticus, glossing it as "all virtue"
      "Ecclesiasticus embraces these topics as well — whence it was called Panaretos, as if you were to say, 'all virtue.'"
