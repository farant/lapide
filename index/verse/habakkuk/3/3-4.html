    Habakkuk 3:3-4 (Vulgate)

    Description
    His glory covered the heavens, and the earth was full of his praise. His brightness shall be as the light; horns are in his hands

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome quotes Habakkuk's vision of Christ on the cross
      "His glory covered the heavens, and the earth was full of his praise. His brightness shall be as the light; horns are in his hands: there his strength is hidden."
