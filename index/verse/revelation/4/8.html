    Revelation 4:8

    Description
    Holy, holy, holy, Lord God Almighty, who was, and who is, and who is to come

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome quotes this as the cry of the twenty-four elders and the four living creatures, connected to the twenty-four books of the Old Law
      "Holy, holy, holy, Lord God Almighty, who was, and who is, and who is to come."
