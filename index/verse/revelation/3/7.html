Revelation 3:7

Description
Christ holding the key of David, who opens and no one shuts

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Jerome alludes to this verse in discussing who can open the sealed book of Scripture
    "unless he opens it who has the key of David, who opens and no one shuts, who shuts and no one opens"
  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P6 — Cited by Jerome (via Lapide) as the key that alone opens Scripture
    "unless all the things that are written are opened by Him who has the key of David, who opens and no one shuts, who shuts and no one opens, they will be disclosed by no one else."
