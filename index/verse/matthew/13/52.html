    Matthew 13:52

    Description
    Every scribe instructed in the kingdom of heaven brings forth from his treasure things new and old

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Sacred Writers As Pens P2 — Quoted as Christ's teaching that the theologian must draw from both Old and New Testaments
      "Every scribe," that is, every Doctor, every Theologian, "instructed in the kingdom of heaven, brings forth from his treasure things new and old."
