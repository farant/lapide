    John 3:30

    Vulgate
    Illum oportet crescere, me autem minui.

    Preliminaries

    • TO THE MOST REVEREND AND MOST ILLUSTRIOUS LORD HENRY FRANCIS VAN DER BURCH, ARCHBISHOP AND DUKE OF CAMBRAI, PRINCE OF THE HOLY ROMAN EMPIRE, COUNT OF CAMBRAI. — Lapide applies the Baptist's words to Van der Burch's rising ecclesiastical career and his own diminishing role
