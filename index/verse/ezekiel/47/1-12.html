    Ezekiel 47:1-12

    Description
    Ezekiel's vision of a river flowing from beneath the threshold of the temple, with trees on both banks

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter I P10 — Quoted: Ezekiel saw a great river flowing out from beneath the threshold of the house of the Lord, which he could not cross
      "because the waters of the deep torrent had swollen," he says, "which cannot be forded: and when I turned, behold on the bank of the torrent on both sides were very many trees."
