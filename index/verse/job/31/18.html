Job 31:18

Vulgate
Ab infantia mea crevit mecum miseratio, et de utero matris meae crevit mecum.
English
From my infancy compassion grew up with me, and from my mother's womb it came forth with me.

Job's declaration of innate compassion, applied by Lapide to Van der Burch's lifelong charity toward the poor. The quotation is explicitly attributed to "holy Job" and used to characterise Van der Burch's almsgiving as instinctive rather than acquired.
