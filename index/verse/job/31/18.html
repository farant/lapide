    Job 31:18

    Vulgate
    Ab infantia mea crevit mecum miseratio, et de utero matris meae egressa est mecum.

    Preliminaries

    • TO THE MOST REVEREND AND MOST ILLUSTRIOUS LORD HENRY FRANCIS VAN DER BURCH, ARCHBISHOP AND DUKE OF CAMBRAI, PRINCE OF THE HOLY ROMAN EMPIRE, COUNT OF CAMBRAI. — Quoted as expressing Van der Burch's innate charity
      "From my infancy compassion grew up with me, and from my mother's womb it came forth with me."
