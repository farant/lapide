    Job 19:25-27 (Vulgate)

    Description
    I know that my redeemer lives, and on the last day I shall rise from the earth

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P15 — Jerome quotes this as Job's clear and cautious prophecy of the resurrection of the body
      "I know, he says, that my redeemer lives, and on the last day I shall rise from the earth; and again I shall be clothed with my skin, and in my flesh I shall see God, whom I myself shall see, and my eyes shall behold, and not another. This hope of mine is laid up in my bosom."
