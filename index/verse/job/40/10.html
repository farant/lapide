    Job 40:10

    Description
    The elephant is called Behemoth — reference to the Hebrew plural as honorific.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — God Elohim P24 — Example of Hebrew honorific plural: elephant called Behemoth (beasts) because of its size
      "Thus in Job XL, 10, the elephant is called Behemoth, that is "beasts," because on account of the greatness of its body and strength, it is the equivalent of many beasts"
