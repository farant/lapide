    Luke 2:29-32

    Description
    The Nunc Dimittis — the canticle of Simeon upon seeing the infant Christ in the temple

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • ON THE WORSHIP OF JESUS CHRIST IN THE SCRIPTURES. — Lacordaire quotes Simeon's canticle as the supreme word spoken when the Son of God came to the temple
      "Now, Lord, you will let your servant depart in peace, according to your promise, because my eyes have seen your salvation, the salvation that you have prepared before the face of all peoples, to be a light of revelation to them and the glory of your people Israel"
