Acts 8:30-31

Description
Philip asks the Ethiopian eunuch if he understands Isaiah; the eunuch replies 'How can I, unless someone teaches me?'

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Jerome cites the Ethiopian eunuch as proof that Scripture requires a guide to be understood
    "'Do you think you understand what you are reading?' answered: 'How can I, unless someone teaches me?'"
