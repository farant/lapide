    Acts 7:22

    Description
    St. Stephen's speech praising Moses: Moses was mighty in his words and in his deeds

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Praises of Moses from Scripture and the Fathers — Quoted at length from Stephen's speech in Acts 7
      "Moses was mighty in his words and in his deeds; there appeared to him in the desert of Mount Sinai an angel, in a flame of fire in a bush; this man God sent as ruler and redeemer, with the hand of the angel who appeared to him; this man led them out, performing wonders and signs in the land of Egypt; this is he who was in the assembly in the wilderness with the angel who spoke to him on Mount Sinai, who received the words of life to give to us."
