    Genesis 9:3

    Description
    Reference to God's explicit grant of meat-eating after the flood.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 29: Behold I Have Given You Every Herb for Food — Men abstained from meat before the flood because God had not yet expressly granted it, as clear from Genesis 9:3
      "God had not yet expressly and explicitly granted the use of meat and wine, as is clear from Genesis 9, verses 3 and 21."
