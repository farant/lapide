    Genesis 1:22-23

    Description
    And He blessed them, saying: Increase and multiply, and fill the waters of the sea.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Vulgate Text: Genesis 1:1-31 — Full Vulgate text quoted
      "22. And He blessed them, saying: Increase and multiply, and fill the waters of the sea; and let the birds be multiplied upon the earth. 23. And the evening and the morning were the fifth day."
    • Verse 22: And He Blessed Them, Saying: Increase and Multiply — God's blessing gives appetite, power, and capacity to generate; fecundity of fish greater than birds
      "22. AND HE BLESSED THEM, SAYING: INCREASE AND MULTIPLY. For God to bless is to do good"
