    Genesis 1:21

    Description
    And God created great whales, and every living and moving creature, which the waters brought forth.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Vulgate Text: Genesis 1:1-31 — Full Vulgate text quoted
      "21. And God created great whales, and every living and moving creature, which the waters brought forth according to their kinds, and every winged fowl according to its kind. And God saw that it was good."
    • Verse 21: And God Created the Great Sea Creatures — Cete (tanninim) means dragons and enormous animals, both terrestrial and aquatic
      "21. AND GOD CREATED THE GREAT SEA CREATURES. "Cete" (sea creatures) are called in Hebrew tanninim"
