Genesis 14:15

Description
Reference to the city of Laish being called Dan — a later editorial insertion into the Mosaic text

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses Most Ancient P6 — Lapide notes that at Genesis 14:15 the city of Laish is called Dan, though the name postdates Moses
    "at Genesis 14:15, the city of Laish is called Dan, although it was called Dan long after the times of Moses"
