    Genesis 14:15

    Description
    Reference to the city of Laish being called Dan — a later editorial insertion into the Mosaic text

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Moses as the Most Ancient Theologian, Philosopher, Poet, and Historian — Lapide notes that at Genesis 14:15 the city of Laish is called Dan, though the name postdates Moses
      "at Genesis 14:15, the city of Laish is called Dan, although it was called Dan long after the times of Moses"

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Argumentum — Cited as an example of a post-Mosaic editorial substitution (Laish called Dan)
      "in Genesis 14:15, the city Laish is called Dan, although it was called Dan long after Moses' time"
