    • Genesis 1 — Heaven And Earth P18 — Moses tacitly implies the angels in the "ornament" of the heavens
      "he tacitly implies them in chapter II, 1, when he says: "Thus the heavens were finished, and all their ornament:" for the ornament of the heavens consists of stars and angels."
