    Wisdom 1:4-5

    Description
    Into a malevolent soul wisdom will not enter, nor will it dwell in a body subject to sins

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P8 — Quoted on the necessity of purity of heart for receiving wisdom
      "into a malevolent soul wisdom will not enter, nor will it dwell in a body subject to sins. For the Holy Spirit of discipline will flee from the deceitful, and will withdraw from thoughts that are without understanding, and will be rebuked by the coming of iniquity"
