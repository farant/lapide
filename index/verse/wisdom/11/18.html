    Wisdom 11:18

    Description
    God created the world from invisible matter — reference to formless matter.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 2: And the earth was without form and void — Cited on the formless, unordered state of original matter
      "God "created the world from invisible matter," in Greek amorpho, that is, formless, unadorned, unordered."
