    4 Ezra 6:40

    Description
    From His treasures He brought forth a luminous light, that His work might appear.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 3: And God said: Let there be light — Light brought forth from God's treasures so His work might be visible
      ""From His treasures," says Esdras, Book IV, ch. 6, v. 40, "He brought forth a luminous light, that His work might appear.""
