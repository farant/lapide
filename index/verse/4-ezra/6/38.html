    4 Ezra 6:38

    Description
    Reference to God creating heaven and earth by commanding 'Let there be.'

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • He Created — God created by commanding, as expressly stated in 4 Esdras
      "God created heaven and earth by commanding and saying: Let there be heaven and earth, as is expressly stated in IV Esdras, vi, 38"
