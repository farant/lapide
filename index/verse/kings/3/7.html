3 Kings 3:7

Vulgate
Ego autem sum puer parvulus et ignorans egressum et introitum meum.
English
I am a little child who does not know his own going out or coming in.

Solomon's prayer at the beginning of his reign, acknowledging his inadequacy before God. Lapide weaves this into his own devotional self-assessment, combining it with Proverbs 30:2-3 to express radical humility despite his vast learning. Note: 3 Kings 3:7 in the Vulgate corresponds to 1 Kings 3:7 in modern numbering.
