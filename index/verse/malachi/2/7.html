Malachi 2:7

Description
The lips of the priest shall keep knowledge, and they shall seek the law from his mouth

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P5 — Cited as the reason Moses directed doubtful questions to the priests
    "The lips of the priest shall keep knowledge, and they shall seek the law (that is, the doubtful points of the law about which there is question, says St. Bernard) from his mouth."
  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Arguments For The Ot P3 — Cited as a prophetic argument for the necessity of the Old Testament
