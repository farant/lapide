Deuteronomy 34:8

Vulgate
Fleveruntque eum filii Israel in campestribus Moab triginta diebus.
English
And the children of Israel wept for him in the plains of Moab thirty days.

The mourning for Moses at his death, applied by Lapide to the grief of the people of Ghent when Van der Burch departed their city to become Archbishop of Cambrai. The typology underscores that a beloved pastor leaves behind immense longing.
