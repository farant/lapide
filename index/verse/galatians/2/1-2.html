    Galatians 2:1-2

    Description
    Paul's second visit to Jerusalem after fourteen years, with Barnabas and Titus, to present his Gospel to the apostles

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome cites Paul's return to verify his teaching as evidence for the necessity of authoritative guidance
      "again after fourteen years, having taken Barnabas and Titus, he set forth the Gospel to the Apostles, lest perhaps he was running or had run in vain"
