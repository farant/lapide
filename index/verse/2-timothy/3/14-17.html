    2 Timothy 3:14-17

    Description
    From infancy you have learned the Sacred Letters... All Scripture divinely inspired is profitable for teaching, for reproving, for correcting

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Arguments for the Old Testament — Quoted as Paul's commendation of Timothy's knowledge of the Old Testament Scriptures
      "which can," he says, "instruct you unto salvation, through the faith which is in Christ Jesus. All Scripture divinely inspired is profitable for teaching, for reproving, for correcting, for instructing in justice, that the man of God may be perfect, equipped for every good work."
