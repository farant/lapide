    2 Corinthians 13:3

    Description
    Do you seek proof of him who speaks in me, Christ?

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome quotes Paul as an example of how great men sought learning from others, introducing Paul as a vessel of election
