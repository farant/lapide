Exodus 32:32

Vulgate
Aut dimitte eis hanc noxam; aut si non facis, dele me de libro tuo quem scripsisti.
English
Either forgive them this offence, or if you will not, blot me out of the book which you have written.

Moses' supreme act of intercessory charity, offering his own eternal destiny for the sake of his people after the sin of the golden calf. Lapide cites this as the pinnacle of Moses' motherly love for Israel, establishing charity as the essential pastoral virtue.
