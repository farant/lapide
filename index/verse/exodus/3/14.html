Exodus 3:14

Description
God's self-revelation to Moses as 'ehyeh' — 'I am who I am'

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Praises Of Moses P10 — Cited in the discussion of Plato learning from Moses; God's name "ehyeh" = "who is" or "I am who I am," which Plato rendered as "to on"
    "just as Moses calls Him "ehyeh," that is, "who is," or "I am who I am.""
