Numbers 12:7-8

Description
My servant Moses is the most faithful in all My house: for I speak to him mouth to mouth — God's defense of Moses

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses Virtues P3 — Quoted to show Moses's unique intimacy with God
    "My servant Moses," says the Lord, Numbers 12:7, "is the most faithful in all My house: for I speak to him mouth to mouth, and openly, and not through enigmas and figures does he see the Lord."
  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses And Christ P7 — Cited by Basil in his praise of Moses
    "Mouth to mouth I shall speak to him in a vision, and not through enigmas."
