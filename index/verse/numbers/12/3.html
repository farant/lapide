Numbers 12:3

Vulgate
Erat enim Moyses vir mitissimus super omnes homines qui morabantur in terra.
English
He was the meekest of all mortals.

The famous description of Moses as the meekest man on earth, cited by Lapide as the defining praise Holy Scripture bestows upon Moses. This virtue is then applied typologically to both Van der Burch and St. Basil, who "vanquished his adversaries by his steady kindness."
