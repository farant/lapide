Numbers 11:11-12

Description
Moses's complaint to God about the burden of leading the people — 'Did I conceive this whole multitude?'

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses Virtues P4 — Quoted to show Moses's charity and care for his people, and the immense burden of leadership
    "Why, Lord, have You laid the burden of this entire people upon me? Did I conceive this whole multitude, or beget them, that You should say to me: Carry them in your bosom, as a nurse is accustomed to carry an infant, and bring them to the land which You swore to their fathers?"
