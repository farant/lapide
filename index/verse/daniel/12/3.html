Daniel 12:3

Vulgate
Qui autem docti fuerint fulgebunt quasi splendor firmamenti, et qui ad iustitiam erudiunt multos quasi stellae in perpetuas aeternitates.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P52 — Quoted as the eschatological promise closing the dedicatory letter
    "They that are learned shall shine as the brightness of the firmament, and they that instruct many unto justice, as stars for all eternity."
  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Jerome quotes this to distinguish learned justice from mere rusticity; the learned shine like the firmament, the just like stars
    "But those who are learned shall shine like the brightness of the firmament; and those who instruct many unto justice, like stars for perpetual eternities."
