Daniel 12:3

Vulgate
Qui docti fuerint, fulgebunt quasi splendor firmamenti, et qui ad justitiam erudiunt multos, quasi stellae in perpetuas aeternitates.
English
They that are learned shall shine as the brightness of the firmament, and they that instruct many unto justice, as stars for all eternity.

The closing verse of the dedicatory letter, explicitly cited as "that which was promised by Daniel." Lapide invokes this eschatological promise as the ultimate reward for both his own scholarly labour and Van der Burch's pastoral ministry -- that teachers and pastors will shine eternally.
