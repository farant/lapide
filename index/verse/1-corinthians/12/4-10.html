    1 Corinthians 12:4-10

    Description
    Paul's enumeration of spiritual gifts — word of wisdom, knowledge, faith, healing, prophecy, discernment, tongues, interpretation

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P15 — Quoted as Paul attributing the ability to understand Scripture not to natural intellect but to distributions of the Holy Spirit's graces
      "to one is given through the Spirit the word of wisdom, to another the word of knowledge, to another faith, to another the grace of healing, to another the working of miracles, to another prophecy, to another the discernment of spirits, to another kinds of tongues, to another finally the interpretation of speeches"
