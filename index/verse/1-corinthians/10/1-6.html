    1 Corinthians 10:1-6

    Description
    Our fathers were all under the cloud, and all passed through the sea... these things happened as figures of us

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • IV. The Old Testament Surpasses the New in Allegorical Richness — Quoted at length as Paul's teaching that the Old Testament events are types for Christians
      "Our fathers," says the Apostle, 1 Corinthians 10:1, "were all under the cloud, and all passed through the sea, and all were baptized in Moses, in the cloud and in the sea, and all ate the same spiritual food, etc. Now these things happened as figures of us: and they were written for our sake, upon whom the ends of the ages have come."
    • V. Figures, Examples, and Maxims from the Old Testament — Cited as Paul's statement that Old Testament events are examples
      "All things happened to them as figures, that is as examples for us; that we should not be covetous of evil things, nor idolaters"
