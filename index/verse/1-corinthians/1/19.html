    1 Corinthians 1:19

    Description
    I will destroy the wisdom of the wise, and the prudence of the prudent I will reject

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Jerome quotes this after discussing John 1:1 to contrast divine wisdom with the ignorance of Plato and Demosthenes
      "I will destroy, he says, the wisdom of the wise, and the prudence of the prudent I will reject."
