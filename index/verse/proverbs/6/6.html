    Proverbs 6:6

    Description
    Go to the ant, O sluggard, and consider her ways, and learn wisdom.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 20: Let the Waters Bring Forth — The Wise Man sends the idle man to the ants as examples of providence
      "Go, he says, to the ant, O sluggard, and consider her ways, and learn wisdom, which though she has no leader, nor instructor, nor prince, prepares food for herself in summer, and gathers at harvest what she may eat."
