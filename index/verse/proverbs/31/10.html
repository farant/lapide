    Proverbs 31:10

    Description
    Who shall find a valiant woman? — the acrostic poem at the end of Proverbs

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • I. THE HELMETED PROLOGUE. — Jerome cites the alphabetic acrostic at the end of Proverbs as evidence for the twenty-two Hebrew letters
      "the Proverbs of Solomon at the end, from the place where he says, 'Who shall find a valiant woman?' are reckoned by the same alphabets or divisions."
