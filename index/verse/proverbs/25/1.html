    Proverbs 25:1

    Description
    Reference to the fact that Solomon's proverbs were collected and arranged by others

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Moses as the Most Ancient Theologian, Philosopher, Poet, and Historian — Cited as evidence that the proverbs of Solomon were not collected by him but by others
      "the proverbs of Solomon were not collected and arranged by him, but by others from his writings, as is clear from Proverbs 25:1"

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Argumentum — Cited as proof that Solomon's proverbs were arranged by others
      "the proverbs of Solomon were not gathered and arranged by him, but by others from his writings, as is clear from Proverbs 25:1"
