Jeremiah 1:5-6

Description
God's call to Jeremiah: Before I formed you in the womb, I knew you — and Jeremiah's response: I am a child

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P7 — Quoted as an example of a prophet humbled before the divine word despite being sanctified from the womb
    "Before I formed you in the womb, I knew you, and before you came forth from the womb, I sanctified you, and I made you a prophet to the nations... Ah, ah, ah, Lord God, behold I do not know how to speak, for I am a child."
