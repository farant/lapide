Psalm 38:2-3 (Vulgate)

Description
I said, I will guard my ways, that I may not sin with my tongue. I set a guard over my mouth

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Helmeted Prologue P29 — Jerome quotes this as his own resolve against detractors, asking for the prayers of the faithful
    "I said, I will guard my ways, that I may not sin with my tongue. I set a guard over my mouth, when the sinner stood against me. I was silent and was humbled, and kept silence even from good things."
