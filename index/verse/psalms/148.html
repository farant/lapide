    Psalm 148 (Vulgate)

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 24: Let the Earth Bring Forth the Living Creature — Augustine marvels at the design of the flea and gnat as evidence of God's greatness
      "who arranged the limbs of the flea and the gnat, so that they have their own order, their own life, their own movement?"
