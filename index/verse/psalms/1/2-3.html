    Psalm 1:2-3

    Description
    Blessed is the man who meditates on the law day and night; he is like a tree planted beside running waters

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter I P7 — Alluded to: the Royal Psalmist calls blessed the one who meditates on God's law day and night
      "The Royal Psalmist calls blessed not the one who pours forth the words of God to others, but the one who meditates on his law day and night; such a one, he says, is like a tree planted beside running waters"
