    Haggai 2:12

    Description
    Ask the priests the law

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome attributes this to "the prophet Malachi, or rather the Lord through Haggai" to show the priestly duty of teaching the law
      "The prophet Malachi, or rather the Lord through Haggai, says: 'Ask the priests the law.'"
