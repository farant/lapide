Greek Translation of Jerome's Psalter and Prophets

Author
Sophronius of Jerusalem

Sophronius's translation of Jerome's Latin Psalter and Prophets into elegant Greek, demonstrating that Jerome's work was esteemed not only among Latins but also among Greek-speaking Christians.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Preface Reader P6 — Sophronius described as translating Jerome's Psalter and Prophets into elegant Greek
    "Sophronius also, a most erudite man, observing that St. Jerome's translation was highly approved not only by Latins but also by Greeks, valued it so greatly that he translated the Psalter and the Prophets from Jerome's version into elegant Greek."
