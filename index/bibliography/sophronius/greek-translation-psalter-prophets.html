Greek Translation of Jerome's Psalter and Prophets

Description
Greek translation of Jerome's Psalter and Prophets, showing acceptance by both Latins and Greeks
Author
Sophronius

Sophronius translated Jerome's Latin Psalter and Prophets into Greek, demonstrating that Jerome's translation was approved and valued not only by the Latin-speaking West but also by the Greek-speaking East.
