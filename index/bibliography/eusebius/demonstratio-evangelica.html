Demonstratio Evangelica

Also known as
Demonstration of the Gospel
Author
Eusebius of Caesarea

Eusebius's continuation of the Praeparatio, demonstrating that Jesus fulfilled Old Testament prophecy, including a famous antithesis of Moses and Christ.
