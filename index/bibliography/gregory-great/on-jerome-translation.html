On Jerome's Translation

Description
Gregory's praise of Jerome's translation as drawn more truly from the Hebrew
Author
St. Gregory the Great

Gregory the Great's endorsement of Jerome's translation as "more truly from the Hebrew speech," adding papal authority to the growing preference for the Hieronymian version over the Old Latin.
