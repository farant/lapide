Lives of Saints Theodoric and John of Cambrai

Author
Hincmar of Reims

Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims, wrote or transmitted accounts extolling the virtues of Saints Theodoric and John, bishops of Cambrai, cited by Lapide as exemplars of episcopal holiness.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P44 — Hincmar cited as extolling the virtues of St. Theodoric of Cambrai
    "Saint Theodoric was his near equal, whose virtues Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims, extols."
  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P45 — Hincmar cited as celebrating St. John, successor of Theodoric
    "Likewise Saint John, his successor, celebrated by the same Hincmar."
