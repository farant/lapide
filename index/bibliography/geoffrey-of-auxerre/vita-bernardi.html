Life of Saint Bernard

Also known as
Vita Prima Sancti Bernardi
Author
Geoffrey of Auxerre / William of Saint-Thierry

The primary biography of Bernard, describing his meditative method of learning Scripture among beeches and oaks rather than from books.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P23 — Book III, chapter 3 and Book I, chapter 4 cited: Bernard's masters were beeches and oaks
    "he himself used to say repeatedly that in the study of Sacred Scripture he had no other masters than beeches and oaks"
