Anecdote about Alfonso of Aragon

Author
Panormitanus (Niccolo de' Tudeschi)

Panormitanus's account of Alfonso, King of Aragon, reading the entire Bible with glosses and commentaries fourteen times.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Utility Iv P8 — Panormitanus cited: Alfonso of Aragon read the Bible with commentaries fourteen times
    "Panormitanus relates that Alfonso, King of Aragon, used to boast that, even in the midst of his kingdom's business, he had read through the entire Bible with glosses and commentaries fourteen times."
