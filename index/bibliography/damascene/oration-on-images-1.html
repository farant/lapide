First Oration on Holy Images

Description
Defense of holy images, containing the account of Basil's prayer before an image of Our Lady
Author
St. John Damascene

The first of John Damascene's three orations defending the veneration of holy images against iconoclasm. Contains the account from Helladius of Basil's prayer before an image of the Blessed Virgin, after which St. Mercury appeared in vision and slew Julian the Apostate.
