    Homilies on Numbers

    Also known as
    Homiliae in Numeros
    Author
    Origen

    Origen's homiletical commentary on the Book of Numbers, cited for the tradition that Moses received both the law and its explanation on Sinai.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P3 — Homily 5 cited: tradition that Moses received the explanation of the law on Sinai and transmitted it secretly
      "Origen in Homily 5 on Numbers lend their support, that Moses received on Mount Sinai from God not only the law but also the explanation of the law"
