    Homily That Man Is Set Over Every Creature

    Also known as
    Quod homo sit constitutus super omnem creaturam
    Author
    St. John Chrysostom

    A homily on man's lordship over creation, with exhortations to gratitude.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 5: And He called the light Day — Quoted at length on creation serving man: sky, sun, moon, forests, fields, springs
      "St. Chrysostom, in his Homily That Man Is Set Over Every Creature, applies keen incentives to man from the day, light, and other creatures for serving God."
