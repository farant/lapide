Homilies on 1 Timothy

Also known as
Homiliae in Epistulam I ad Timotheum
Author
St. John Chrysostom

Chrysostom's homiletical commentary on Paul's First Epistle to Timothy, containing the demanding standard that a bishop must be like an angel.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses Virtues P4 — Homily 40 quoted: a bishop must be an angel, subject to no human disturbance
    "Truly St. Chrysostom said, homily 40 on the First Epistle to Timothy: "A bishop must be an angel, subject to no human disturbance or vice;""
