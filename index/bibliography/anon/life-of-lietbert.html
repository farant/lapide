Life of St. Lietbert

Description
Hagiographical account of St. Lietbert, Bishop of Cambrai
Author
Anonymous

Anonymous hagiographical account of St. Lietbert, Bishop of Cambrai, who undertook a pilgrimage to Jerusalem. The text also records a posthumous miracle: the discovery that his hair had turned grey, a sign of sanctity.
