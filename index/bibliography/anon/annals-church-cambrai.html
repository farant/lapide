Annals of the Church of Cambrai

Description
Historical chronicle of bishops of the Archdiocese of Cambrai
Author
Anonymous

Historical chronicle recording the succession of bishops of the Archdiocese of Cambrai, many of whom were enrolled in the catalogue of saints for their holiness and pastoral virtues.
