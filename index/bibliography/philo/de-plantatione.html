    De Plantatione

    Also known as
    On the Planting of Noah, De Plantatione Noe
    Author
    Philo of Alexandria

    Philo's allegorical treatise on Noah as a planter, with reflections on creation and the world.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 31: And God Saw All Things That He Had Made, and They Were Very Good — Quoted at length on nothing lacking to God's works except a just praiser
      "Philo, in his book On the Planting of Noah, near the end, teaches that nothing is lacking to God's works except a just evaluator and eulogist."
