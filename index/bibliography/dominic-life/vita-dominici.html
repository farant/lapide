    Life of Saint Dominic

    Author
    Jordan of Saxony (attributed)

    Early vita of Saint Dominic recording his urging the Brothers to study the Old and New Testaments as essential for preaching.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Saintly Examples P5 — Book IV, chapter IV quoted: Dominic urged the Brothers to study the Old and New Testaments
      "Because," he says, "without knowledge of the Scriptures no one can be a perfect preacher, he urged the Brothers always to study the Old and New Testaments"
