Farewell Discourse (John 13-17)

Also known as
The Last Discourse, the High-Priestly Prayer
Author
St. John the Apostle

The extended discourse of Jesus to his apostles at the Last Supper, comprising chapters 13-17 of the Gospel of John. Lacordaire urges the reader to read this discourse in its entirety.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Du Culte P46 — Lacordaire directs the reader to John 13-17 as Christ's final instruction to his apostles before the Passion
    "the discourse recorded in chapters 13, 14, 15, 16, and 17 of the Gospel of Saint John (the reader, for God's sake, must not fail to read it)"
