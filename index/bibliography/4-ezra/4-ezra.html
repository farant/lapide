    4 Ezra

    Also known as
    II Esdras, Apocalypse of Ezra, IV Esdras
    Author
    Anonymous (attributed to Ezra)

    A Jewish apocalyptic text, cited by Lapide as a supplementary witness to creation theology though not canonical.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • He Created — Cited (ch. VI, v. 38) on God creating by commanding
      "as is expressly stated in IV Esdras, vi, 38"
    • Verse 3: And God said: Let there be light — Cited (Book IV, ch. 6, v. 40) on God bringing forth luminous light from His treasures
      ""From His treasures," says Esdras, Book IV, ch. 6, v. 40, "He brought forth a luminous light, that His work might appear.""
    • Verse 21: And God Created the Great Sea Creatures — Cited (ch. 6) as source of the Jewish fable about two great whales
      "They took this fable from Book IV of Esdras, chapter 6"
