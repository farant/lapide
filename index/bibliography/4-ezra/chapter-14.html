    4 Ezra, Chapter 14

    Also known as
    2 Esdras 14, Apocalypse of Ezra
    Author
    Anonymous (attributed to Ezra)

    Apocryphal text in which Ezra dictates 204 books by divine inspiration, with 70 to be kept secret for the wise, cited on the tradition of hidden wisdom transmitted alongside public Scripture.
