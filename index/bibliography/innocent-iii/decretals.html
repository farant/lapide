    Decretals (Solitae)

    Also known as
    Solitae benignitatis, Decretals, Book I, title 33
    Author
    Pope Innocent III

    Pope Innocent III's decretal letter to the Emperor of Constantinople on the relationship between papal and royal authority.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 14: Let There Be Lights in the Firmament — Quoted on the two great luminaries as Pontifical authority and royal power
