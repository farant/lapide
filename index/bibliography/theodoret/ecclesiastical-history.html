Ecclesiastical History

Also known as
Historia Ecclesiastica
Author
Theodoret of Cyrrhus

Theodoret's history of the Church, covering the Arian controversy and its aftermath, preserving important testimony about the Cappadocian Fathers' resistance to imperial Arianism.
