Philotheos (Religious History)

Also known as
Historia Religiosa, Religious History
Author
Theodoret of Cyrrhus

Theodoret's collection of biographies of Syrian ascetics and monks, including St. Marcian's teaching on hearing God through creatures and Scripture.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter I P9 — St. Marcian quoted (via Theodoret's Philotheos): God speaks daily through creatures and Scripture yet we derive little benefit
    "that saying of St. Marcian in Theodoret's Philotheos, who, when asked by the Bishops to speak a word of salvation, said: God speaks to us daily through his creatures and through Sacred Scripture"
