Controversies

Also known as
Disputationes de Controversiis Christianae Fidei
Author
St. Robert Bellarmine

Bellarmine's massive systematic refutation of Protestant theology, described by Lapide as impenetrable and incomparable, the wall and bulwark of Catholic truth.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iv P33 — Described as impenetrable and incomparable, the wall and bulwark of Catholic truth
    "This the most illustrious Bellarmine, champion of the faith and overthrower of heresies, did solidly and exactly in his Controversies — a work therefore impenetrable and incomparable, nor has the Church from the time of Christ until now seen its like in this genre"
