    Acts of Saint Saturninus

    Author
    Anonymous

    Hagiographic text recording the miracle at Alutina where rain from a clear sky extinguished fire threatening sacred books, demonstrating God's protection of Scripture.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses Most Ancient P9 — Acts of St. Saturninus cited (Surius, February 11): Bishop Fundanus surrendered books, fire miraculously extinguished
      "when Fundanus, formerly Bishop of Alutina, out of fear of death had surrendered the sacred books, and the sacrilegious magistrate was consigning them to the fire, suddenly a shower poured down from a clear sky"
