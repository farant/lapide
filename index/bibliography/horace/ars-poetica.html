Ars Poetica

Description
Verse epistle on poetry and literary craft
Author
Horace

Horace's verse epistle on the art of poetry. Cited for the maxim "He wins all votes who blends the useful with the sweet" (line 343), applied in the preface to Lapide's method of combining doctrinal substance with readability.
