Commentary on Galatians

Also known as
Commentarii in Epistulam ad Galatas
Author
St. Jerome

Jerome's commentary on Paul's Epistle to the Galatians, cited for the teaching that Moses was not only a Prophet but also an Apostle.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses And Christ P9 — Beginning of commentary cited: Moses was not only a Prophet but also an Apostle (from Hebrew tradition)
    "Saint Jerome, who at the beginning of his Commentary on the Epistle to the Galatians teaches that Moses was not only a Prophet but also an Apostle"
  • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Canons P17 — Cited (on chapter 3) for the view that divine apparitions were made through angels
    "St. Jerome on chapter 3 of Galatians"
