    Commentary on Obadiah

    Also known as
    Commentarii in Abdiam
    Author
    St. Jerome

    Jerome's commentary on the prophet Obadiah; in the prologue he rebukes himself for having once explained the prophet allegorically without grasping the historical meaning.
