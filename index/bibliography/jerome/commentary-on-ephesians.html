    Commentary on Ephesians

    Also known as
    Commentarii in Epistulam ad Ephesios
    Author
    St. Jerome

    Jerome's commentary on Paul's Epistle to the Ephesians; in the preface he describes his lifelong devotion to reading and consulting learned men.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — The Fathers P4 — Preface quoted: Jerome never ceased reading or questioning; went to Alexandria to consult Didymus
      "Never," he says, "from my youth did I cease to read, or to question learned men about what I did not know; never did I make myself (as most do) my own teacher. Finally, very recently, for this above all other reasons, I went to Alexandria, to see Didymus and to consult him about all the doubts I had in the Scriptures."
