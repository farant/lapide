Annotations on Lapide's Commentaries

Also known as
Notes by Aug. Crampon
Author
Augustinus Crampon

Crampon's editorial annotations enriching Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch, adding scholarly notes and cross-references from nineteenth-century biblical scholarship.
