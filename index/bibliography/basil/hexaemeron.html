Hexaemeron

Description
Homilies on the six days of creation (Genesis 1)
Author
St. Basil the Great

Nine homilies by St. Basil the Great on the six days of creation in Genesis 1. The work became foundational for patristic cosmology and was translated into Latin by St. Ambrose, who "gave to Latin ears not so much his own work as that of Saint Basil."
