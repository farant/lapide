Epistle 164

Description
Letter describing fleeing the bishopric of Neocaesarea
Author
St. Basil the Great

Letter in which Basil recounts fleeing the bishopric of Neocaesarea, feigning sickness to avoid being made bishop, until he was reluctantly consecrated.
