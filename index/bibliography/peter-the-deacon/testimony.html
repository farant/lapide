    Testimony on Gregory the Great

    Author
    Peter the Deacon

    Peter the Deacon's eyewitness testimony that the Holy Spirit in the form of a dove sat upon Gregory the Great as he commented and wrote.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P18 — Peter the Deacon attested: Holy Spirit in form of a dove sat upon Gregory as he commented and wrote
      "That the Holy Spirit sat upon St. Gregory the Great in the form of a dove... as he was commenting and writing, the eyewitness Peter the Deacon attests."
