    Alethia

    Also known as
    De Perversis Suae Aetatis Moribus
    Author
    Claudius Marius Victor

    A biblical epic poem by the 5th-century Gallic poet Claudius Marius Victor, versifying the Genesis creation account.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 2: And the earth was without form and void — Quoted on the Holy Spirit hovering over the waters and animating them
      "And the sacred Spirit, hovering over the outstretched waves, Animated the nurturing waters, giving the seeds of things."
