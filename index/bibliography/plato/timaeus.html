Timaeus

Author
Plato

Plato's cosmological dialogue, cited via Justin for the story of Solon being told by an Egyptian priest: "O Solon, you Greeks are always children."

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Praises Of Moses P7 — Cited via Justin: Solon told Critias what an Egyptian priest said: "O Solon, you Greeks are always children"
