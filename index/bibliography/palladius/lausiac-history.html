Lausiac History

Also known as
Historia Lausiaca
Author
Palladius

Palladius's collection of biographies of Egyptian and Eastern monks, cited for John the Anchorite's teaching on purity and spiritual knowledge.

References in Commentary

    "The same thing that great St. John the Anchorite taught by his word and example, as reported by Palladius in the Lausiac History, chapter 40."
