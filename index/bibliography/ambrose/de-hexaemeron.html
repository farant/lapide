De Hexaemeron

Description
Latin treatise on the six days of creation, based on Basil's Hexaemeron
Author
St. Ambrose

St. Ambrose's Latin adaptation of Basil's Hexaemeron. The dedicatory letter notes that Ambrose "gave to Latin ears not so much his own work as that of Saint Basil," making the Eastern patristic cosmology accessible to the Latin-speaking West.
