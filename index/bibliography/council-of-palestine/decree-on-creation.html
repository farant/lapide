    Decree on the creation at the vernal equinox

    Also known as
    Council on the Paschal Controversy
    Author
    Council of Palestine (under Pope Victor)

    A council held under Pope Victor defining that the world was created at the vernal equinox and that the first day was March 25.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 12 P12 — Cited as defining that the world was created at the vernal equinox, on March 25
      "because the Council of Palestine, held under Pope Victor in the year of Christ 198, defined this very thing."
