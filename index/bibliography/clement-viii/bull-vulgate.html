Cum Sacrorum (Bull on the Vulgate)

Description
Papal bull establishing the corrected Vatican Vulgate (9 November 1592)
Author
Pope Clement VIII

Papal bull "Cum Sacrorum" issued by Clement VIII on 9 November 1592, establishing the corrected Vatican edition of the Vulgate and forbidding unauthorized printing for ten years. This Clementine edition superseded the earlier Sixtine edition (1590) and became the standard Catholic Bible text until the Nova Vulgata of 1979. The full text is quoted in the Preliminares.
