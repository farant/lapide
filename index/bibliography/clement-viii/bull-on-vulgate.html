Bull on the Vulgate (Cum Sacrorum)

Also known as
Cum Sacrorum Bibliorum
Author
Pope Clement VIII

Clement VIII's 1592 bull regulating the printing and preservation of the corrected Vulgate edition, forbidding unauthorized printing for ten years and requiring conformity to the Vatican copy thereafter.
