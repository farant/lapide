Commentary on the Epistles of Saint Paul

Also known as
Commentaria in omnes Divi Pauli Epistolas
Author
Cornelius a Lapide

Lapide's commentary on all fourteen Pauline epistles, published before the Pentateuch commentary and establishing his exegetical method.

References in Commentary
