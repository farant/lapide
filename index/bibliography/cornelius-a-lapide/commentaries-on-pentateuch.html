Commentaries on the Pentateuch of Moses

Also known as
Commentaria in Pentateuchum Mosis
Author
Cornelius a Lapide

Lapide's commentary on the five books of Moses (Genesis and Exodus in Volume One). First published at Antwerp in 1616, with a second edition in 1623. This is the work within which all the Preliminares material appears.
