    Sayings

    Also known as
    Sententiae Secundi, Sentences of Secundus, Life and Sentences of Secundus
    Author
    Secundus the Philosopher

    A collection of definitions and sayings attributed to the philosopher Secundus, in dialogue with the Emperor Hadrian.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Heaven And Earth P19 — Quoted on what is the world, God, the ocean, and the earth
      "the philosopher Secundus, when questioned by the emperor Hadrian: "What is the world?" He responded: "An unceasing circuit, an eternal course. "
    • Genesis 1 — Verse 16 P2 — Quoted on the sun and moon: "The eye of heaven, a splendor without setting"
      "The philosopher Secundus, cleverly questioned by the Emperor Hadrian, "What is the sun?" replied"
