On the Celestial Hierarchy

Also known as
De Coelesti Hierarchia
Author
Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite

Treatise on the orders of angels and heavenly intelligences, opening with an exhortation to proceed from Sacred Scripture received from the Fathers.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Ii P19 — Dionysius quoted from the opening of the Celestial Hierarchy on proceeding from Sacred Scriptures received from the Fathers
    "Let us proceed with all our strength to understand the Sacred Scriptures, as we have received them from the Fathers, to be contemplated, and let us speculate, insofar as we can, on the distinctions and orders of the heavenly spirits"
