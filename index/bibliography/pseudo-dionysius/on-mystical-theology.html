    On Mystical Theology

    Also known as
    De Mystica Theologia
    Author
    Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite

    Short treatise on apophatic theology and the ascent to God through negation, contrasted with the breadth of symbolic theology.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter I: On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture — Cited on mystical theology reaching God by negation, ending in silence
      "And again in the book On Mystical Theology, he teaches that spiritual and mystical Theology, which reaches the super-substantial hidden mystery and the darkness of God by transcending all created things through negation, without symbols, is narrow and so compressed as to end in silence"
