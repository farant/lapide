    De Coelesti Hierarchia

    Also known as
    Celestial Hierarchy, On the Celestial Hierarchy
    Author
    Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite

    The Pseudo-Dionysian treatise on the angelic orders and their mediating role between God and humanity.

    References in Commentary

    • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Canons P17 — Cited (chapter 4) for the view that divine apparitions were made through angels
