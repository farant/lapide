    On Wisdom According to the Egyptians

    Also known as
    De Sapientia secundum Aegyptios
    Author
    Pseudo-Aristotle

    A pseudonymous treatise attributed to Aristotle on Egyptian wisdom and the nature of God as the center of all things.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — God Elohim P12 — Quoted (Book XII, ch. XIX) on God as the center from whom all natures depend
      ""God is He from whom come perpetuity, place, and time, and by whose benefit all things endure; and just as the center of a circle exists in itself,"
