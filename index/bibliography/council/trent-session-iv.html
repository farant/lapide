Council of Trent, Session IV

Description
Two decrees on Scripture — On Canonical Scriptures and On the Edition and Use of Sacred Books (8 April 1546)

The fourth session of the Council of Trent (8 April 1546) issued two landmark decrees. The first, "On Canonical Scriptures," defined the canon of Old and New Testament books and declared the Vulgate authentic. The second, "On the Edition and Use of Sacred Books," regulated biblical interpretation and printing. Both decrees are quoted in full in the Preliminares.
