The Lake

Also known as
Le Lac
Author
Alphonse de Lamartine

Lamartine's celebrated lyric poem, cited by Lacordaire among the greatest works of human poetic genius, surpassed by the psalms and prophecies.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Du Culte P15 — Lacordaire lists "Lamartine's The Lake with its waters, its shores, and his beloved" among the pinnacles of human poetry
