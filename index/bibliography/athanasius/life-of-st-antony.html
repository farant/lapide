    Life of Saint Antony

    Also known as
    Vita Antonii
    Author
    St. Athanasius of Alexandria

    Athanasius's biography of Antony the Great, foundational text of Christian monasticism, describing Antony's conversion by Scripture and his teaching on purity of heart.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter I P9 — Athanasius cited as source for Antony's conversion by the Gospel text "If you wish to be perfect, go, sell all"
      "So St. Athanasius writes in his Life."
    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P9 — Antony quoted (via Athanasius) on purity of heart and foreknowledge
      "St. Anthony, as reported by Athanasius: If anyone, he says, is held by a desire of knowing even future things, let him have a pure heart"
