    De Decretis

    Also known as
    Epistle on the Decrees of the Council of Nicaea, De Decretis Nicaenae Synodi
    Author
    St. Athanasius of Alexandria

    Athanasius's treatise defending the decrees of the Council of Nicaea, explaining the meaning of "to create" in Scripture.
