    Sermon 1 On the Annunciation

    Also known as
    Sermo 1 in Annuntiatione
    Author
    St. Bernard of Clairvaux

    Bernard's sermon on the Annunciation, reflecting on the original state of man.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Quoted on what the first man possessed: mercy, truth, justice, and peace
      "Rightly therefore St. Bernard, Sermon 1 On the Annunciation: "What, he says, was lacking to the first man, whom mercy guarded, truth taught, justice governed, and peace cherished?""
    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited on the image being burned but not burned up in hell; likeness lost through sin
      "St. Bernard, Sermon 1 On the Annunciation"
