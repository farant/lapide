    Life of Saint Ambrose

    Also known as
    Vita Sancti Ambrosii
    Author
    Paulinus of Milan

    The deacon Paulinus's biography of Ambrose of Milan, recording that Ambrose was seen assisted by an angel while treating Scripture.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — Paulinus cited: Ambrose was seen assisted by an angel while treating Scripture
      "Ambrose, if we believe St. Paulinus in his account of his deeds, when he was treating the Scriptures in a sermon, was seen assisted by an angel."
