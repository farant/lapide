Generic reference to Homer's characterization of kings as shepherds of the peoples, a classical topos used in the dedicatory letter to frame the bishop's pastoral role.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P6 — Homer cited as calling a king "the shepherd of the peoples"
