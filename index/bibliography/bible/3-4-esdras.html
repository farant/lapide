3-4 Esdras

Description
Apocryphal books placed outside the canon by the Council of Trent

The Third and Fourth Books of Esdras (3 Ezra and 4 Ezra), apocryphal texts placed outside the canonical Scriptures by the Council of Trent. Printed as appendices in some Vulgate editions but not received as canonical.
