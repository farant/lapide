Prayer of Manasseh

Description
Apocryphal prayer placed outside the canon by the Council of Trent

The Prayer of Manasseh, an apocryphal penitential prayer attributed to King Manasseh of Judah. Placed outside the canonical Scriptures by the Council of Trent, though printed as an appendix in some Vulgate editions.
