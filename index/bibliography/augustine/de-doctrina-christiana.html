De Doctrina Christiana

Description
Treatise on biblical interpretation and Christian teaching
Author
St. Augustine

Augustine's foundational treatise on how to interpret Scripture and communicate Christian doctrine. In Book II, Augustine praises the Italic (Old Latin) translation as preferable among Latin versions for its fidelity and clarity, a judgment later cited in discussions of the Vulgate's authority.
