On the Lord's Sermon on the Mount

Also known as
De Sermone Domini in Monte
Author
St. Augustine of Hippo

Augustine's commentary on the Sermon on the Mount (Matthew 5-7), containing his teaching on the beatitude of peacemakers and wisdom.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P7 — Quoted on the beatitude of peacemakers and wisdom
    "befits the peaceful, in whom all things are now ordered, and no motion is rebellious against reason, but all things obey the spirit of man, since he himself obeys God."
