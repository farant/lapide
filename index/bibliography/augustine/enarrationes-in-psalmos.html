    Enarrationes in Psalmos

    Also known as
    Expositions of the Psalms, Commentaries on the Psalms, Exposition on the Psalms
    Author
    St. Augustine of Hippo

    Augustine's extensive homiletic commentary on all 150 Psalms.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 16 P20 — Cited (on Psalm 94) on stars teaching patience: Saints endure insults as luminaries endure mockery
      "Hence the stars teach us patience, says St. Augustine on Psalm 94."
    • Genesis 1 — Verse 16 P21 — Cited (same Psalm 94 passage) on Saints fixed in heaven like stars, undisturbed by evils on earth
      "Third, the stars teach loftiness and immovability of mind in the midst of so many adversities and injuries, so that like stars they look down upon all things, both evil and good, that happen in the world."
    • Genesis 1 — Verse 24 P12 — Cited (on Psalm 148) on God's wisdom in arranging even the smallest creatures
      "St. Augustine, in the Exposition on Psalm 148"
