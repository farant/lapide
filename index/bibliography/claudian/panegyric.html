    Panegyric on Theodosius

    Also known as
    De Tertio Consulatu Honorii
    Author
    Claudian

    Claudian's verses praising Theodosius, applied by Lapide to Joshua and Judas Maccabeus as examples of divine military aid.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • II. The Richness of the Old Testament — Verse quoted: "O too much beloved of God, for whom from his caves Aeolus marshals his armed storms"
      "O too much beloved of God, for whom from his caves Aeolus marshals his armed storms, for whom the sky fights, and the conspiring winds come at the trumpet's call."
