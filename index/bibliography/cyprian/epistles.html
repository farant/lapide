Epistles of St. Cyprian

Also known as
Epistulae, Letters of St. Cyprian
Author
St. Cyprian of Carthage

Cyprian's collection of letters, including his letter to Antonianus (Book IV, Letter 2) praising the gradual ecclesial ascent of Pope Cornelius through all Church offices before accepting the episcopate.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P14 — Cyprian quoted praising Bishop Cornelius of Rome for ascending through all Church offices before accepting the episcopate
