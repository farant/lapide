Letter 2 to Antonianus

Description
Letter praising Pope St. Cornelius for his humble ascent to the episcopate
Author
St. Cyprian

Letter in which Cyprian praises Pope Cornelius for ascending to the episcopate through all ecclesiastical grades rather than seeking the office directly, illustrating the patristic ideal of reluctant episcopal promotion.
