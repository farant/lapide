    Adversus Judaeos

    Also known as
    Against the Jews, Testimonia ad Quirinum
    Author
    St. Cyprian

    Cyprian's apologetic work against Jewish objections to Christianity, collecting Old Testament testimonies pointing to Christ.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Cited (Book II, chapter 5) for the view that God appeared in bodily form to Abraham and Moses
