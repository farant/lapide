Verses on Modestus

Description
Mock verses ridiculing Prefect Modestus after his humiliation by Basil
Author
Cyrus Theodorus

Satirical verses composed after Prefect Modestus's failed attempt to intimidate Basil the Great. "You are Prefect over all men else, Modestus, / but under Basil the Great you take your place."
