Verses on Modestus

Also known as
Epigram on Modestus and Basil
Author
Cyrus Theodorus

Satirical verses mocking the Prefect Modestus for his subordination to Basil the Great, illustrating Basil's moral authority over imperial power.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P32 — Quoted in full: four lines mocking Modestus for being subject to Basil
    "You are Prefect over all men else, Modestus, but under Basil the Great you take your place."
