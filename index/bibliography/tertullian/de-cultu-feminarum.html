On the Apparel of Women

Also known as
De Cultu Feminarum
Author
Tertullian

Tertullian's treatise on women's dress and adornment, cited for the principle that no pronouncement of the Holy Spirit is directed only for the present occasion.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Utility Iv P3 — Chapter 22 cited: no pronouncement of the Holy Spirit is directed only for the present occasion
    "Tertullian in his book On the Apparel of Women, chapter 22, holds that there is no pronouncement of the Holy Spirit which can be directed and received only for the present matter"
