    Against Marcion

    Also known as
    Adversus Marcionem
    Author
    Tertullian

    Tertullian's five-book refutation of Marcion's rejection of the Old Testament, demonstrating the agreement of both Testaments as testimony to Christ and truth.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — The Fathers P2 — Cited: Tertullian wrote four books Against Marcion
      "Tertullian wrote for the same cause four books Against Marcion."
    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Utility Vi P3 — Tertullian cited: agreement of both Testaments bears testimony to Christ and to truth
      "the agreement of both Testaments — that is, the agreement of Moses and Christ, of the Prophets and the Apostles, of the Synagogue and the Church — bears great testimony to Christ and to the truth, as Tertullian teaches everywhere against Marcion"
