    Oration 2 (Apologeticus)

    Also known as
    Oration 2, Apologeticus, On Easter
    Author
    St. Gregory Nazianzen

    Gregory Nazianzen's oration defending his flight from ordination, cited on the hermeneutical middle way between literalism and excessive allegory.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — The Authors Method P2 — Nazianzen cited: one must proceed by a middle way between literalists and excessive allegorists
      "following Nazianzen's lead (Oration 2, On Easter), that one must proceed by a middle way between those who, with a coarser intellect, dwell on the letter, and those who delight excessively in allegorical speculation alone"
