    Commentary on Genesis

    Also known as
    Commentarii in Genesim, Instructiones
    Author
    Eucherius of Lyon

    Eucherius's exegetical work on Genesis, treating both literal and allegorical senses.

    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited (Book I on Genesis) on distinguishing image and likeness

    • Verse 24: Let the Earth Bring Forth the Living Creature — Cited on the tropological meaning of the six days
