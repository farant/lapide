    Thesaurus

    Also known as
    Thesaurus de Sancta et Consubstantiali Trinitate, Thesaurus de Sancta Trinitate
    Author
    St. Cyril of Alexandria

    Cyril's systematic treatise on the Trinity, refuting Arian interpretations of Scripture.
