    Glaphyra

    Also known as
    Glaphyra in Pentateuchum, Elegant Commentaries on the Pentateuch, Polished Gems on the Pentateuch
    Author
    St. Cyril of Alexandria

    Cyril's allegorical commentary on select passages of the Pentateuch, pursuing the mystical sense.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Introduction — Cited as pursuing the mystical sense of Genesis; Lapide used manuscript copies later published by Andreas Schottus
      "Blessed Cyril wrote five books, to which add his Glaphyra, that is, "polished gems," as if to say, a few things selected from many, in which he pursues not the literal but mostly the mystical sense."
