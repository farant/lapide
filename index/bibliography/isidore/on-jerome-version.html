On Jerome's Version

Description
Isidore's preference for the Hieronymian version of Scripture
Author
St. Isidore of Seville

Isidore's endorsement of Jerome's translation of Scripture as "clearer in words and truer in meaning," contributing to the gradual triumph of the Hieronymian version over the older Italic (Old Latin) translations.
