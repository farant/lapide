Works on Basil

Description
Testimonies about St. Basil including the dove vision and giving away his inheritance
Author
St. Gregory of Nyssa

Gregory of Nyssa's writings containing testimony about his brother Basil, including the vision of St. Ephrem seeing a dove whispering Basil's sermon in his ear, and Basil's distribution of his personal inheritance to the poor.
