Commentaries on Ezra through Maccabees

Description
Commentary on Ezra, Nehemiah, Tobit, Judith, Esther, and Maccabees
Author
Cornelius a Lapide

Lapide's commentary on the later historical books: Ezra, Nehemiah, Tobit, Judith, Esther, and Maccabees. Published posthumously at Antwerp in 1644.
