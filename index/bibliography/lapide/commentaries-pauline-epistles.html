Commentaries on the Pauline Epistles

Description
Commentary on all fourteen Pauline epistles
Author
Cornelius a Lapide

Lapide's commentary on the fourteen Pauline epistles (Romans through Hebrews). Referenced in the preface to the reader as the model for the Pentateuch commentaries: "My method here is the same as it was in the Pauline commentaries."
