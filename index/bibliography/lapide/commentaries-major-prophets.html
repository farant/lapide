Commentaries on the Major Prophets

Description
Commentary on Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, and Daniel
Author
Cornelius a Lapide

Lapide's commentary on the four major prophets: Isaiah, Jeremiah (with Baruch and Lamentations), Ezekiel, and Daniel. Contains Lapide's personal prayer for martyrdom addressed to the four prophets.
