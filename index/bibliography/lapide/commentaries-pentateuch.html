Commentaries on the Pentateuch

Description
Commentaries on the five books of Moses (Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy)
Author
Cornelius a Lapide

Lapide's commentaries on the five books of Moses, first published at Antwerp in 1616 and again in 1623. This is the work being dedicated in the dedicatory letter and the primary text of the site. Lapide describes his method as the same used in his earlier Pauline commentaries: giving each sense of the text its due, treating the literal first.
