Ecclesiastical History

Description
Continuation of Eusebius's Church History covering the 4th century
Author
Rufinus of Aquileia

Rufinus's continuation of Eusebius's Ecclesiastical History, covering events from the Council of Nicaea to the death of Theodosius I. Provides key testimony about Basil and Gregory's thirteen years of Scripture study and about Basil's pastoral zeal in Pontus.
