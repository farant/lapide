Life of Saint Basil

Also known as
Vita Sancti Basilii
Author
St. Gregory of Nyssa

Gregory of Nyssa's biographical testimony about his brother Basil, including the miracle of the dove seen by Ephrem while Basil was preaching, and Basil's distribution of his personal inheritance to the poor.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P23 — Ephrem saw a dove whispering into Basil's ear while preaching, as Gregory of Nyssa testifies
    "a dove, I say, which is the sign and hieroglyph of the Holy Spirit, as Gregory of Nyssa testifies"
  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P23 — Basil gave away his personal inheritance to the poor, per Gregory of Nyssa
    "Basil's own brother Gregory of Nyssa adds that at that time Saint Basil also gave away his own personal inheritance to the poor."
