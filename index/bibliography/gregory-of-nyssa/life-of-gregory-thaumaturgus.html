    Life of Gregory Thaumaturgus

    Also known as
    Vita Gregorii Thaumaturgi
    Author
    St. Gregory of Nyssa

    Gregory of Nyssa's biography of Gregory Thaumaturgus, including the vision of John the Evangelist delivering a creed at the Virgin's command, and a teaching on purity of spiritual vision.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — Gregory Thaumaturgus received from St. John (at the Virgin's command) an explanation of the beginning of his Gospel
      "Gregory, surnamed Thaumaturgus, a client of the Blessed Virgin, at her admonition and command in a dream, received from St. John an explanation of the beginning of his Gospel"
    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — Nyssen cited: no one can behold the divine with a free sense when gazing at lowly things
      "as Nyssen beautifully says, no one can behold the divine and that kindred light which is discerned by the mind itself, with a free and unoccupied sense, when one directs one's gaze... at lowly and muddy things"
