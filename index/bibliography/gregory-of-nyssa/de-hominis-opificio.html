    De Hominis Opificio

    Also known as
    On the Making of Man, De Opificio Hominis
    Author
    St. Gregory of Nyssa

    Gregory of Nyssa's treatise on the creation of man, completing Basil's unfinished Hexaemeron commentary.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 24: Let the Earth Bring Forth the Living Creature — Cited (Oration 2) as holding all creatures were originally peaceful
      "Gregory of Nyssa seems to hold the same view, Oration 2 On the Creation of Man."
    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited on God furnishing the world like a dining hall before creating man
      "as Nyssen says"
