    Platonic School

    Also known as
    Platonists
    Description
    Philosophical school of Plato; Justin went to a Platonist but was deceived with a vain hope of wisdom, before finding truth in the Prophets

    Philosophical school of Plato. Justin slipped from the Pythagorean to a Platonist, deceived by all of them with a vain and fleeting hope of wisdom, before finding truth in the Prophets.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter I: On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture — Justin slipped from the Pythagorean to a Platonist, deceived by vain hope
      "from this one he slipped to a Platonist, deceived by all of them with a vain and fleeting hope of wisdom"
