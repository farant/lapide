    Anthropomorphites

    Also known as
    Audaeans
    Description
    Heretical sect whose originator was Audaeus; held that man is the image of God according to the body and therefore that God is corporeal

    Heretical sect whose originator was Audaeus (hence they are also called Audaeans). They held that man is the image of God according to the body, and therefore that God is corporeal. This is condemned as heresy.

    References in Commentary

      "The Anthropomorphites, whose originator was Audaeus (hence they are called Audaeans), thought that man is the image of God according to the body, and therefore that God is corporeal; but this is heresy."
