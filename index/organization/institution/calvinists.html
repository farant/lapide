Calvinists

Also known as
followers of Calvin
Description
Protestant movement following John Calvin, who used Old Testament passages to argue for predestination and God as author of evil

Protestant movement following John Calvin, who used Old Testament passages about Pharaoh's hardened heart and Shimei cursing David to argue for predestination and God as the author of evil.
