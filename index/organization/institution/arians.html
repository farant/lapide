    Arians

    Also known as
    Arian heretics
    Description
    Followers of the heresy of Arius who denied the full divinity of Christ

    Followers of the heresy of Arius who denied the full divinity of Christ. The Council of Sirmium is described by Lapide as an assembly of Arians.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — The Council of Sirmium was an assembly of Arians
      "for it is established that this Council was an assembly of Arians"

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • He Created — Athanasius's epistle with the decrees of the Council of Nicaea was written against the Arians
      "St. Athanasius, in the epistle inscribed with the decrees of the Council of Nicaea against the Arians"
    • Verse 3: And God said: Let there be light — Athanasius argued against the Arians that God's willing is His doing
      "God's willing is His doing, says St. Athanasius, Sermon 3 Against the Arians"
