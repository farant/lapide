    School at Nisibis

    Also known as
    schools of sacred Scripture at Nisibis
    Description
    Schools of sacred Scripture established at Nisibis among the Syrian Hebrews in the time of Emperor Justinian

    Schools of sacred Scripture established at Nisibis in the time of Emperor Justinian, as attested by Junilius Africanus. Cassiodorus notes these schools were diligently practiced among the Syrian Hebrews.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — Cassiodorus notes these schools were diligently practiced at Nisibis
