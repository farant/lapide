Anabaptists

Description
Radical Reformation sect that proscribed the Old Testament, boasting of being moved and taught by the enthusiasm of the spirit

Radical Reformation sect that proscribed the Old Testament together with Moses, boasting that they were moved and taught by the enthusiasm of the spirit.
