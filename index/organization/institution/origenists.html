    Origenists

    Description
    Followers of Origen's theological errors; Gregory Thaumaturgus received a creed from St. John to set against them

    Followers of Origen's theological errors. Gregory Thaumaturgus received from St. John an explanation of the beginning of his Gospel, in a divinely issued creed which he might set against the Origenists.
