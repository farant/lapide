    Philosophical school of Aristotle. Justin chose a Peripatetic master but despised him for hawking wisdom for a price.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter I: On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture — Justin chose a Peripatetic master but despised him
      "he chose a Peripatetic master, whom he despised for hawking wisdom for a price"

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • He Created — The Peripatetics asserted that God created the world from necessity of nature
      "the error of the Peripatetics, who asserted that God created the world not by will, nor freely, but from the necessity of nature from eternity"
