    Priscillianists

    Also known as
    followers of Priscillian
    Description
    Heretical sect that, like the Manichaeans, taught there are two principles of things or two gods

    Heretical sect that, like the Manichaeans, taught there are two principles of things or two gods: one good, the creator of spirits; the second evil, the creator of bodies.
