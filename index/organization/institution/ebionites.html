Ebionites

Description
Early Jewish-Christian heretical sect; Symmachus, the fourth translator of Hebrew Scripture into Greek, was first an Ebionite

Early Jewish-Christian heretical sect. Symmachus, the fourth translator of Hebrew Scripture into Greek, was first an Ebionite, then a Jew.
