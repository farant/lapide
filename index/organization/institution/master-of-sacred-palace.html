Master of the Sacred Palace

Description
Office in the papal household; St. Dominic was created the first holder, and from that time the dignity adhered to the Order of Preachers

Office in the papal household. St. Dominic was created the first Master of the Sacred Palace, and from that time the dignity adhered to the Order of Preachers.
