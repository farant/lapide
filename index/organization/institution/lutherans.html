    Lutherans

    Description
    Protestant movement; referenced through Matthias Flacius Illyricus whose teaching on original sin is condemned

    Protestant movement. Referenced through Matthias Flacius Illyricus, whose teaching that original sin substantially transformed man into an image of the devil is condemned as impious and foolish.

    References in Commentary
