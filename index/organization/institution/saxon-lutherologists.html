    Saxon Lutherologists

    Also known as
    Saxon Lutherans, babblers
    Description
    Lutheran theologians from Saxony who argued at the Regensburg disputation for proscribing traditions and establishing Scripture alone as the judge of faith controversies

    Lutheran theologians from Saxony who, in a disputation at Regensburg, argued from Deuteronomy for proscribing traditions and establishing the sole word of God as the ultimate judge of controversies of faith.
