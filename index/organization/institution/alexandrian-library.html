    Alexandrian Library

    Also known as
    the library, Philadelphus's library
    Description
    The great library at Alexandria; Ptolemy Philadelphus had the Septuagint and Hebrew manuscripts deposited there through Demetrius

    The great library at Alexandria. Ptolemy Philadelphus arranged for the Septuagint version and Hebrew manuscripts to be deposited there through Demetrius, the library's prefect. Tertullian testifies it was preserved there down to his own times.
