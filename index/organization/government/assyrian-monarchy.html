    Assyrian Monarchy

    Also known as
    Assyrians
    Description
    One of the ancient monarchies that must be known to understand the Old Testament prophets and historical books

    One of the four ancient monarchies (Assyrians, Medes, Persians, Greeks, Romans) that must be thoroughly learned to understand the Old Testament prophets and historical books.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Iii P19 — How many monarchies of the Assyrians must be thoroughly learned
      "How many monarchies — of the Assyrians, Medes, Persians, Greeks, and Romans — must be thoroughly learned!"
    • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Sacred Chronology P4 — Ninus and Semiramis founded the first monarchy of the Assyrians
      "Ninus and Semiramis, who founded the first monarchy of the Assyrians, in whose time Abraham lived"
