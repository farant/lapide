    Privy Council

    Description
    Advisory council to the sovereign of the Spanish Netherlands; Van der Burch's father served as President

    The advisory council to the sovereign of the Spanish Netherlands. Van der Burch's father served as President of the Privy Council after his tenure at the Parliament of Mechlin.
