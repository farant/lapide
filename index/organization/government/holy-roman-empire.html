    Holy Roman Empire

    Also known as
    the Empire
    Description
    Supranational political entity in central Europe; Van der Burch held the title of Prince of the Holy Roman Empire

    Supranational political entity in central Europe. Van der Burch held the title of Prince of the Holy Roman Empire as Archbishop of Cambrai.
