Kingdom of Aragon

Description
Kingdom ruled by Alfonso, who boasted of having read the entire Bible with glosses and commentaries fourteen times

Kingdom ruled by Alfonso, who boasted of having read the entire Bible with glosses and commentaries fourteen times while managing his kingdom's business.

References in Commentary

    "Alfonso, King of Aragon, used to boast that, even in the midst of his kingdom's business, he had read through the entire Bible with glosses and commentaries fourteen times"
