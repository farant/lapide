    Seleucid Dynasty

    Also known as
    Greek era, Seleucid era
    Description
    Dynasty founded after the death of Alexander the Great; its dating system is used by the books of the Maccabees

    The Seleucid dynasty, whose dating system began after the death of Alexander the Great when Seleucus assumed the royal title. The books of the Maccabees compute and record their histories from the Seleucid era.
