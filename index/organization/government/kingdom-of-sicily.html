    Kingdom of Sicily

    Description
    Kingdom ruled by Robert, who told Petrarch that letters were dearer to him than his kingdom

    Kingdom ruled by Robert, who told Petrarch under oath that letters, especially sacred ones, were far dearer to him than his kingdom, and that he would more calmly part with the crown than with letters.
