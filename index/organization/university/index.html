Universities

  • University of Cologne — University descended from the Parisian academy; described as the daughter of Paris and mother of Louvain
  • University of Louvain — Catholic university in Louvain; where Lapide held the chair of Sacred Scripture for over twenty years
  • University of Paris — University established by Charlemagne at Paris; described as the mother of Cologne and the grandmother of Louvain
