Diocese of Rochester

Also known as
Bishopric of Rochester
Description
English diocese; Bl. John Fisher served as its bishop and refused transfer to any wealthier see

English diocese. Bl. John Fisher was raised to the bishopric of Rochester on account of his incomparable learning and innocence of life, and refused to leave it for any wealthier or grander see.
