Diocese of Ghent

Also known as
Bishopric of Ghent
Description
Catholic diocese in Flanders; Van der Burch served as its Bishop before transfer to Cambrai

Catholic diocese in Flanders. Van der Burch served as its Bishop, restoring discipline and splendour, before being transferred to the archbishopric of Cambrai by the Archduke.
