Diocese of Ghent

Description
Suffragan diocese in the Low Countries; Van der Burch called to its bishopric by Archduke Albert

Van der Burch was called to the bishopric of Ghent by Archduke Albert before his later elevation to the archbishopric of Cambrai and then Mechlin.
