    Archdiocese of Cambrai

    Also known as
    Church of Cambrai
    Description
    Ancient archdiocese in the southern Low Countries/northern France; Van der Burch was inaugurated as its Archbishop

    Ancient archdiocese in the southern Low Countries (now northern France). Van der Burch was inaugurated as its Archbishop. Described as containing nearly 800 parishes and as one of the foremost churches in Belgium, with many of its bishops enrolled in the catalogue of saints.
