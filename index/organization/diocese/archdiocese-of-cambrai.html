Archdiocese of Cambrai

Also known as
Church of Cambrai, Archidioecesis Cameracensis
Description
Ancient archdiocese in the Low Countries, praised for its many sainted bishops; Van der Burch inaugurated as Archbishop

Described in the dedicatory letter as "among the foremost in Belgium" and celebrated for its long succession of sainted bishops: Vindicianus, Lietbert, Authbert, and Gaugericus among those named. Lapide hails Van der Burch's inauguration as Archbishop of this "most ancient" church, calling the see a fitting culmination of his ecclesiastical career.
