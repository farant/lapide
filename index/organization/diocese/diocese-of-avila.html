Diocese of Avila

Description
Spanish diocese; Blessed Alfonso Tostado served as Bishop of Avila and wrote extensively on the Old Testament

Spanish diocese. Blessed Alfonso Tostado served as Bishop of Avila and composed extensive volumes on the Decateuch and the individual books of the Old Testament.
