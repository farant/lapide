Diocese of Amiens

Description
Catholic diocese in northern France; diocese of Augustin Crampon, annotator of this edition

Catholic diocese in northern France. Augustin Crampon, the annotator of this edition of Lapide's commentaries, was a priest of this diocese. His annotations were approved by the Bishop of Amiens in 1852.
