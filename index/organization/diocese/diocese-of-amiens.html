Diocese of Amiens

Description
French diocese where Crampon served as priest; Bishop Jacobus Antonius approved his annotations

The diocese in which Augustin Crampon served as a priest and honorary canon. Bishop Jacobus Antonius of Amiens granted the imprimatur for Crampon's annotated edition of Lapide's commentary.
