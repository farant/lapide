Diocese of Tinnin

Description
Hungarian diocese (modern Croatia); seat of Bishop Andreas Dudecius, who appeared as legate at the Council of Trent but later apostatized

Historical diocese in Hungary (modern Croatia). Andreas Dudecius, Bishop of Tinnin, served as legate of the Hungarian clergy at the Council of Trent, but later apostatized to Calvinism.
