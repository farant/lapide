Archdiocese of Canterbury

Description
English archdiocese; St. Edmund served as its Archbishop, spending days and nights in sacred Letters

English archdiocese. St. Edmund served as its Archbishop in 1247, spending his days and nights in sacred Letters with such reverence that he kissed the Bible each time he opened it.
