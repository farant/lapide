    Archdiocese of Mechlin

    Description
    Catholic archdiocese in the Spanish Netherlands with its see at Mechlin; Van der Burch served as Dean and Vicar-General here

    Catholic archdiocese in the Spanish Netherlands with its see at Mechlin (Mechelen). Van der Burch served as Dean of the Metropolitan Church and Vicar-General of the archdiocese before becoming Bishop of Ghent.
