Archdiocese of Mechlin

Description
Metropolitan see of the Low Countries; Van der Burch served as its Archbishop

The metropolitan archdiocese of the southern Low Countries. Van der Burch served as its Archbishop, governing "the whole archdiocese of Mechlin."
