Diocese of Alutina

Description
Diocese whose bishop Fundanus surrendered the sacred books to persecutors out of fear, after which a miraculous storm saved the books from burning

Ancient diocese whose bishop Fundanus, out of fear of death, surrendered the sacred books to the persecutors. A miraculous shower extinguished the fire, saving the books from burning.
