Dioceses

  • Archdiocese of Cambrai — Ancient archdiocese in the southern Low Countries/northern France; Van der Burch was inaugurated as its Archbishop
  • Archdiocese of Canterbury — English archdiocese; St. Edmund served as its Archbishop, spending days and nights in sacred Letters
  • Archdiocese of Mechlin — Catholic archdiocese in the Spanish Netherlands with its see at Mechlin; Van der Burch served as Dean and Vicar-General here
  • Diocese of Alutina — Diocese whose bishop Fundanus surrendered the sacred books to persecutors out of fear, after which a miraculous storm saved the books from burning
  • Diocese of Amiens — Catholic diocese in northern France; diocese of Augustin Crampon, annotator of this edition
  • Diocese of Avila — Spanish diocese; Blessed Alfonso Tostado served as Bishop of Avila and wrote extensively on the Old Testament
  • Diocese of Capua — Italian diocese; St. Benedict saw the soul of Germanus, Bishop of Capua, being carried to heaven by angels
  • Diocese of Ghent — Catholic diocese in Flanders; Van der Burch served as its Bishop before transfer to Cambrai
  • Diocese of Rochester — English diocese; Bl. John Fisher served as its bishop and refused transfer to any wealthier see
  • Diocese of Tinnin — Hungarian diocese (modern Croatia); seat of Bishop Andreas Dudecius, who appeared as legate at the Council of Trent but later apostatized
