Apostolic See

Also known as
Holy See, Sedes Apostolica
Description
The episcopal jurisdiction of the Bishop of Rome, referenced in conciliar decrees, papal bulls, and permissions

The supreme episcopal authority of the Catholic Church. Referenced in the Council of Trent's decree on Scripture (reserving interpretation to the Church and Holy See), in Clement VIII's bull on the Vulgate, and in the preface's discussion of textual authority.
