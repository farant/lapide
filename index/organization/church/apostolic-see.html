Apostolic See

Also known as
Holy See, See of Rome
Description
Episcopal jurisdiction of the Pope as Bishop of Rome; supreme authority on Scripture and its preservation

The episcopal jurisdiction of the Pope as Bishop of Rome. Referenced as the supreme authority on Scripture and its preservation. The Council of Trent convened with three legates of the Apostolic See presiding. Multiple popes entrusted the correction and publication of the Vulgate to its care.
