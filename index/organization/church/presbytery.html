The Presbytery

Also known as
Council of Elders, presbyterium
Description
The body of ordained priests/elders; Paul writes to Timothy about the grace given by the laying on of hands of the presbytery

The body of ordained priests/elders. Paul exhorts Timothy not to neglect the grace given by the laying on of hands of the presbytery.
