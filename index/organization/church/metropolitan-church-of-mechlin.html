Metropolitan Church of Mechlin

Description
Cathedral church of the Archdiocese of Mechlin; Van der Burch presided as Dean

The metropolitan (cathedral) church of the Archdiocese of Mechlin. Van der Burch presided over it as Dean, and Lapide was summoned there by Van der Burch. Its choir and clergy were described as a mirror of virtue and religion for all the Low Countries.
