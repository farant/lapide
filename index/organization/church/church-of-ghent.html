Church of Ghent

Description
The local Church (diocese) of Ghent; its renewed discipline under Van der Burch described as a new radiance for all Belgium

The local Church of Ghent, whose renewed discipline under Van der Burch's episcopate was described as a new splendour and radiance shining out upon all Belgium.
