Metropolitan Church of Mechlin

Description
Principal church of the Archdiocese of Mechlin; Van der Burch served as its Dean

The metropolitan (cathedral) church of the Archdiocese of Mechlin. Van der Burch served as Dean of this church before his episcopal appointments.
