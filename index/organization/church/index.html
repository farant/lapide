Church Institutions

  • Apostolic See — The episcopal jurisdiction of the Bishop of Rome, referenced in conciliar decrees, papal bulls, and permissions
  • Metropolitan Church of Mechlin — Principal church of the Archdiocese of Mechlin; Van der Burch served as its Dean
  • St. Peter's Basilica — Papal basilica in Rome; location given in the dating clause of Clement VIII's bull on the Vulgate
  • Vatican Press — Official printing press of the Vatican that produced the Clementine Vulgate
