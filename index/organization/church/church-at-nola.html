    Church at Nola

    Description
    Church built by St. Paulinus at Nola, with two shrines — one for the Sacred Eucharist and one for the sacred volumes — with inscribed verses

    Church built by St. Paulinus at Nola, which had two shrines: one for the Sacred Eucharist and one for the sacred volumes, with inscribed verses.
