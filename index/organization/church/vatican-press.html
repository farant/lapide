Vatican Press

Also known as
Vatican Typography, our Vatican Press
Description
Printing press of the Holy See; used for the authoritative edition of the corrected Vulgate under Clement VIII

The printing press of the Holy See, used for the authoritative edition of the corrected Vulgate Bible under Clement VIII. Clement's bull forbade printing the Vulgate anywhere other than the Vatican Press for ten years, and after that decade printers were required to obtain a copy from it as their model.
