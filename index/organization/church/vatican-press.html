Vatican Press

Also known as
Typographia Vaticana
Description
Official printing press of the Vatican that produced the Clementine Vulgate

The official printing press of the Holy See. Produced the authoritative Clementine Vulgate edition. Clement VIII's bull commands that the corrected text be sent forth "from our Vatican Press into the light" and forbids other printers from reproducing it without express permission.
