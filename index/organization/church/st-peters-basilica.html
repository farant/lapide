St. Peter's Basilica

Description
Papal basilica in Rome; location given in the dating clause of Clement VIII's bull on the Vulgate

Referenced in the formal dating clause of Clement VIII's bull on the Vulgate: "Given at Rome, at St. Peter's, under the Fisherman's Ring."
