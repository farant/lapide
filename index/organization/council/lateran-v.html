Fifth Lateran Council

Dates
1512–1517
Description
Ecumenical council referenced for its decree imposing penalties on unauthorized printers

Referenced once for the penalty it imposed on unauthorized printing of books. The Clement VIII bull on the Vulgate invokes "the fine imposed in the canon of the most recent Lateran Council" against anyone who would print the corrected Vulgate text without permission.
