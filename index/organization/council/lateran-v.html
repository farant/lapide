    Fifth Lateran Council

    Also known as
    Fifth Lateran Council, most recent Lateran Council
    Description
    Ecumenical council (1512-1517); referenced by the Council of Trent regarding penalties for unauthorized printing

    Ecumenical council of the Catholic Church (1512-1517). Referenced by the Council of Trent with regard to the penalty of fine imposed for unauthorized printing of sacred books.

    Preliminaries

    • DECREES OF THE COUNCIL OF TRENT (SESSION IV). — Trent invokes the fine from the Lateran Council
      "penalty of anathema and the fine imposed in the canon of the most recent Lateran Council"
