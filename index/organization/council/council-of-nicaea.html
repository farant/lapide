    Council of Nicaea

    Also known as
    Nicene Council, First Council of Nicaea
    Description
    Ecumenical council (325) whose decrees against the Arians are cited by Athanasius regarding the meaning of creation

    Ecumenical council held in 325. Referenced indirectly through St. Athanasius's epistle inscribed with the decrees of the Council against the Arians, in which he discusses the meaning of "to create" in Sacred Scripture as making something from nothing.
