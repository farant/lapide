    Order of Preachers

    Also known as
    Dominicans, O.P.
    Description
    Catholic religious order founded by St. Dominic in 1216

    Catholic religious order founded by St. Dominic in 1216. Lacordaire was a member, identified as "Fr. H. D. Lacordaire, O.P."

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • St. Jerome / Fr. H. D. Lacordaire, O.P. — Lacordaire identified as a member of the Order of Preachers
      "Fr. H. D. Lacordaire, O.P."

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Saintly Examples of Scripture Study — St. Dominic created the first Master of the Sacred Palace; the dignity adhered to the Order of Preachers
      "from this he was created the first Master of the Sacred Palace; and from that time this dignity adhered to the Order of Preachers"
    • Saintly Examples of Scripture Study — Dominic urged Brothers to study Old and New Testaments; they carried only the Bible when sent to preach
      "he urged the Brothers always to study the Old and New Testaments... the Brothers sent to preach carried only the Bible with them"
    • Saintly Examples of Scripture Study — St. Jordan, the second Master General of his Order after St. Dominic
      "St. Jordan, indeed, a doctor, the second Master General of his Order after St. Dominic"
