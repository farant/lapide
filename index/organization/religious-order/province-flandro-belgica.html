Province of Flandro-Belgica

Also known as
Jesuit Province of Flanders-Belgium
Description
Jesuit administrative province covering the southern Low Countries; Charles Scribani served as Provincial Superior

The Jesuit administrative province covering the southern Low Countries (Flanders and Belgium). Charles Scribani served as its Provincial Superior, granting printing permissions for Lapide's commentaries.
