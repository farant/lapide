Louvain College

Also known as
Collegium Lovaniense S.J.
Description
Jesuit college at Louvain; mentioned by Lapide as bearing esteem toward Van der Burch

The Jesuit college at Louvain, part of the Society of Jesus. Lapide invoked the love and esteem of the Louvain College toward Van der Burch in his dedicatory letter.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P52 — Lapide invokes the love of the Louvain College
    "this token and pledge of the love and esteem which I, the Louvain College, and our whole Society bear toward you"
  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P3 — The Society established a College in Mechlin
    "until our Society established both a Novitiate and a College in that city"
