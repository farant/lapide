Ludwig Vives (Publisher)

Also known as
Ludwig Vives, Ludovicus Vives
Description
Bookseller and publisher in Paris; published the 1891 Crampon edition of Lapide's commentaries

Bookseller and publisher in Paris, located at 13 Rue Delambre. Published the 1891 Crampon edition of Cornelius a Lapide's commentaries.
