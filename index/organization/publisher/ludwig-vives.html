Ludwig Vives (publisher)

Description
Parisian publishing house at 13 Rue Delambre that published the 1891 Crampon-annotated Lapide edition

Publisher and bookseller in Paris. Published the 1891 Crampon-annotated edition of Lapide's commentary, Volume One covering Genesis and Exodus. Not to be confused with the Renaissance humanist Juan Luis Vives (1493–1540).
