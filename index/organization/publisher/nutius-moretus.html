Nutius & Moretus

Also known as
Martin Nutius, Jan Moretus, Plantin-Moretus Press
Description
Antwerp printers granted permission to print Lapide's Pentateuch commentary

"The heirs of Martin Nutius and Jan Moretus, printers of Antwerp." Granted permission to print by Provincial Superior Scribani. The Moretus firm was the successor to the celebrated Plantin Press.
