Nutius/Moretus

Also known as
Heirs of Martin Nutius, Jan Moretus, Officina Plantiniana
Description
Printers of Antwerp; granted permission to print Lapide's Pentateuch commentaries in 1616

Printers of Antwerp. The heirs of Martin Nutius and Jan Moretus were granted permission by Charles Scribani, Jesuit Provincial Superior, to print Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch of Moses in 1616.
