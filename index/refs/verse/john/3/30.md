John the Baptist's declaration about Christ, applied by Lapide to his own relationship with Archbishop Van der Burch. As Van der Burch rose through ecclesiastical offices, Lapide foresaw his own decrease -- a self-effacing tribute that frames the entire dedicatory letter.
