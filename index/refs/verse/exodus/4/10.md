Moses' reluctance to accept God's call to lead Israel out of Egypt, declining the commission so persistently that he provoked God's anger. Lapide cites this passage explicitly ("In Exodus iv he says") as a parallel to Van der Burch's own reluctance to accept the archbishopric of Cambrai, establishing the pattern that the truly worthy candidate resists the honour.
