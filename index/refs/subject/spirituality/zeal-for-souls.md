Burning pastoral zeal for the conversion of sinners and the salvation of souls — a central apostolic motivation. Basil "in a short time transformed the face of the whole province"; Authbert of Cambrai "burned with such zeal to convert a single sinner that he nearly consumed himself in tears and penances."
