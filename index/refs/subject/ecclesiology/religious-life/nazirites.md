Moses is said to have "instituted the Nazirites and dictated laws for them in Numbers v" (likely a reference to Numbers 6:1-21, the Nazirite vow legislation). Lapide draws a direct line from these Old Testament consecrated persons to the cenobitic monks of Basil's day, establishing a biblical foundation for the religious life.
