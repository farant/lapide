The penalty of major excommunication imposed automatically (ipso facto) for unauthorized printing or alteration of the Vulgate text. Absolvable only by the Roman Pontiff, except in danger of death.

## References in Commentary

- `01_Preliminares.html#clement-viii-p3-s-29f9918` — Clement VIII decrees automatic excommunication, absolvable only by the Roman Pontiff, for printers or booksellers who print or sell unauthorized editions of the Vulgate.
  text: "he shall incur... also the sentence of major excommunication ipso facto; from which he cannot be absolved except by the Roman Pontiff, except when in danger of death."
