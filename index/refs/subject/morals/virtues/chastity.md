Chastity appears briefly in the dedicatory letter in connection with episcopal character. St. Cornelius is praised for his "virginal conscience," and John Fisher's fidelity to his small diocese of Rochester is described in spousal imagery: he refused to "abandon his own bride -- modest she was indeed, but first by God's calling."
