The misuse of sacred Scripture for profane purposes — divination, incantations, satire, defamation — condemned by the Council of Trent, which empowers bishops to restrain violators with penalties.

## References in Commentary

- `01_Preliminares.html#trent-decrees-p12-s-d3169f2` — Trent condemns turning Scripture to profane purposes and empowers bishops to restrain violators with penalties.
