The life, character, offices, and greatness of Moses as lawgiver, pontiff, prophet, general, wonder-worker, and type of Christ, who for thousands of years was the greatest man under the sun. His three periods of forty years encompassed royal education, contemplation in Midian, and leadership of the people.
