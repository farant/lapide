St. Lietbert of Cambrai's pilgrimage to Jerusalem is noted as an example of episcopal piety: "On departing for Jerusalem he drew with him three thousand men, who accompanied him on pilgrimage." The shrine of Our Lady of Aspromont, where Lapide nearly attained martyrdom, is also described as a pilgrimage site "renowned for miracles" where crowds gathered for religious purposes.
