Van der Burch sent the relics of St. Macarius, once Archbishop of Antioch in Armenia, to the city of Mons during a grievous plague, "and as soon as it was brought into the city, the pestilence... began to recede." A silver reliquary was subsequently erected. Authbert of Cambrai also "adorned the relics of the saints with the greatest seemliness."
