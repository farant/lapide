Theodoret's Church History, covering the period from the Arian controversy to the mid-fifth century. Book IV, chapter 17 preserves the anecdote of Demosthenes, the imperial cook who served Emperor Valens, being rebuked by Basil for a grammatical error and told to "go back to your sauces."
