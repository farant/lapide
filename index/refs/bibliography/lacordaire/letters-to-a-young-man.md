A collection of letters by Fr. Henri-Dominique Lacordaire, O.P., addressed to a young man named Emmanuel, on the foundations of the Christian life. The section reproduced in the commentary is the letter "Du Culte de Jesus-Christ dans les Ecritures" (On the Worship of Jesus Christ in the Scriptures).
