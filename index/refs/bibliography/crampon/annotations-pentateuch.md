Augustin Crampon (1826-1894) added editorial footnotes and annotations to Cornelius a Lapide's Pentateuch commentaries for the Vives edition (Paris, 1891). These additions cite post-Lapide scholars such as Rosenmuller, Gesenius, and Meignan, and have been identified and removed from the Genesis commentary on this site to restore Lapide's original text.
