The Greek word Logos, rich with multiple meanings. Jerome explains that it signifies word, reason, reckoning, and the cause through which all things subsist -- all of which are rightly understood in Christ.

## References in Commentary

- `02_Clemens_Hieronymi_Du_Culte.html#jerome-to-paulinus-p2-s-af4504d` — Jerome discusses the richness of the Greek word Logos, explaining it means word, reason, reckoning, and the cause through which all things subsist
  text: "For the Word (Logos) in Greek means many things: it is both word, and reason, and reckoning, and the cause of each thing through which all individual things that exist subsist -- all of which we rightly understand in Christ."
