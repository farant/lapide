The Greek word for "Scribes," the equivalent of the Hebrew sopherim. Used to designate the ancient rabbis who devoted themselves to Sacred Scripture.

## References in Commentary

- `12_Proemium_Et_Encomium_Sacrae_Scripturae.html#chapter-iv-p12-s-28a40c1` — Lapide gives grammateis as the Greek title alongside the Hebrew sopherim for the ancient rabbis
  text: "The ancient Rabbis of the Jews were wholly devoted to Sacred Scripture; and from this they were called sopherim, grammateis, and Scribes."
