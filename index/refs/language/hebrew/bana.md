Hebrew root verb meaning "he built." Lapide cites it as the root from which *banim* ("sons") derives, explaining the metaphor that families are built from sons as buildings from stones.

## References in Commentary

- `14_Commentaria_In_Pentateuchum_Mosis_Canones.html#canons-p11-s-f3975f5` — Root verb from which banim derives
  text: "from the root bana, that is, "he built"; for from sons as from stones the houses and families of parents are built"
