The political entity encompassing Mechlin, Ghent, Cambrai, and Antwerp — all places featured in the Preliminares. The religious wars between Catholic loyalists and Protestant rebels form the backdrop of Van der Burch's family history: heretics seizing Mechlin in 1572 and 1580, the Duke of Alba's rescue, and Archduke Albert's role as co-sovereign calling Van der Burch to the bishopric of Ghent.
