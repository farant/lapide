The religious order to which Cornelius a Lapide belonged. He entered the Society on 8 July 1592, and its institutional structures — novitiates, colleges, superiors general, and provincial superiors — form the backdrop of the Preliminares. The Jesuit college and novitiate at Mechlin are noted, as is Lapide's long service teaching Holy Scripture at Louvain before being called to Rome.
