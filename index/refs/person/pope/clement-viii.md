Pope who brought to completion the corrected Vulgate edition that his predecessors Sixtus V, Gregory XIV, and others had labored over. His bull of 9 November 1592 forbade unauthorized printing for ten years under penalty of excommunication and established the Vatican Press edition as the standard text.
