Prefect of the imperial kitchen under Valens who rebuked Basil "in a thoroughly barbarian fashion." Basil's retort is a famous piece of patristic wit: "We have seen an illiterate Demosthenes" -- playing on the name of the great Athenian orator. When the man threatened further, Basil added: "Your business is to look after the seasoning of broths." Not the classical orator; the name-play is the point.
