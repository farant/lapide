Bishop of Rochester, cardinal, and martyr under Henry VIII. Canonized in 1935; the text (written c. 1616) calls him "that illustrious martyr." Lapide praises him for refusing promotion to larger sees, preferring his modest flock and declaring he could render account for them more easily before God.
