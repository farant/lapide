The Mother of God and greatest of all saints. In the Preliminares she appears in three contexts: Basil prayed before her image in his confrontation with Julian the Apostate; the shrine of Our Lady of Aspromont is noted as "renowned for miracles"; and Lapide credits her intercession with preserving his life, calling her "the most blessed Mother of God, to whom he owed himself and all he had."
