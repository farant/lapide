Last of the Greek Fathers of the Church and Doctor of the Church, known for his defense of sacred images and his systematic theological work, the Fount of Knowledge. In the Preliminares he is cited as the source (via Helladius) for the account of Basil's prayer against Julian the Apostate and the vision of St. Mercury with a bloodied spear.
