Mother of Sts. Basil the Great, Gregory of Nyssa, and Macrina, venerated as a saint in the Eastern tradition. In the Preliminares she is named among Basil's "parents illustrious for piety — Basil and Emmelia," and is noted for following her son into the wilderness.
