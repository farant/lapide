Roman soldier-saint martyred under the Emperor Decius. In the Preliminares he appears in the famous vision associated with Basil's prayer against Julian the Apostate: Basil prayed before an image of Our Lady on which Mercury was also depicted, and Mercury appeared with a bloodied spear, signifying Julian's death.
