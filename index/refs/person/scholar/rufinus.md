Historian, translator, and monk. Attests that Basil and Gregory Nazianzen devoted thirteen years to reading and meditating upon Moses and Sacred Scripture after studying at Athens. Also recounts at length Basil's pastoral zeal in transforming the province of Pontus through preaching, monasteries, and care for the poor (Ecclesiastical History, Book II, ch. ix).
