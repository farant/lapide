The central figure of Christian faith, referenced in the dedicatory letter, Trent decrees, and Lapide's autobiography. Mentions here are relatively brief and contextual rather than exegetical, serving to frame other themes: humility (John the Baptist's "He must increase"), service (washing the disciples' feet), and the purpose of Lapide's life work.
