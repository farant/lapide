Prophet, lawgiver, and leader of the Hebrews who brought his people out of Egypt and through the desert to the promised land. Central figure of the dedicatory letter, where Lapide's entire commentary on the Pentateuch is framed as "this Moses of mine," and Van der Burch is urged to be "our Moses of the Low Countries." Basil the Great is likewise called "the Moses of his age."
