City in the Duchy of Brabant (modern Belgium), seat of the metropolitan archbishopric of the Low Countries. Van der Burch served as Dean and then Vicar-General of its Metropolitan Church. Lapide was summoned there by Van der Burch as confessor. The city was twice seized by heretics (1572 and 1580), during which Van der Burch's father suffered imprisonment and plunder.
