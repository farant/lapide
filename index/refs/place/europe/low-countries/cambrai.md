City in the Low Countries (modern northern France), seat of an archbishopric and duchy within the Holy Roman Empire. Van der Burch was inaugurated there as Archbishop and Prince. The Annals of its church record many sainted bishops, and the text describes it as "most ancient, and among the foremost in Belgium."
