County in the Low Countries (modern Belgium). St. Authbert evangelized Hainaut and under him it began to flourish in the Christian faith, aided by companions including St. Vincent, Count of Hainaut. The people of Hainaut and Mons were afflicted by a grievous plague, relieved by relics of St. Macarius sent by Van der Burch.
