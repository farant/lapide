    Titus Livius (Livy)

    Also known as
    Titus Livius, Livy
    Dates
    59 BC–17 AD
    Role
    Roman historian

    Roman historian. Nobles from Spain and Gaul came to Rome solely for his fame. Called "flowing with the milky fountain of eloquence."
