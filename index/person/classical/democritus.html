    Democritus

    Dates
    c. 460–c. 370 BC
    Role
    Greek philosopher, atomist

    Greek philosopher and proponent of atomism. With Empedocles, confessed that nothing can truly be known by human reason alone.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter II: On the Object and Breadth of Sacred Scripture — With Empedocles, confessed nothing can truly be known
      "Democritus and Empedocles straightforwardly confessed that nothing can truly be known by us."
