    Atlas

    Dates
    mythological
    Role
    Titan, astrologer

    Titan of Greek mythology, brother of Prometheus. In the Proemium identified as an astrologer and the maternal grandfather of Mercury Trismegistus.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Praises of Moses from Scripture and the Fathers — Brother of Prometheus; born in 7th year of Moses
      "Atlas, the brother of Prometheus"
    • Moses as the Most Ancient Theologian, Philosopher, Poet, and Historian — Maternal grandfather of Mercury Trismegistus; an astrologer
      "whose maternal grandfather was Atlas the astrologer"

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Argumentum — Maternal grandfather of Mercury Trismegistus; contemporary of Prometheus; flourished when Moses lived
      "whose maternal grandfather Atlas the astrologer, a contemporary of Prometheus, flourished at the time when Moses lived"
