    Cadmus

    Dates
    mythological/legendary
    Role
    Legendary founder of Thebes; brought the alphabet from Phoenicia to Greece

    Legendary founder of Thebes and bringer of the Phoenician alphabet to Greece. In Cyril's chronology, reigned at Thebes 160 years after Moses.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Praises of Moses from Scripture and the Fathers — First to bring Greek letters from Phoenicia
      "Cadmus was the first to bring these later from Phoenicia"
    • Praises of Moses from Scripture and the Fathers — In 160th year after Moses, reigned at Thebes; his daughter was Semele; Linus and Amphion were musicians at that time
      "Cadmus reigned at Thebes, whose daughter was Semele"
