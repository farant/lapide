    Hermagoras

    Also known as
    Hermagoras of Temnos
    Dates
    2nd century BC
    Role
    Greek rhetorician

    Greek rhetorician. Jerome jokingly refers to his pomposity when offering to study together with Paulinus.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome jokingly refers to his pomposity: "to babble something foolish, after the pomposity of Hermagoras"
