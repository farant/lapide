    Iamblichus

    Dates
    c. 245–c. 325
    Role
    Neoplatonic philosopher

    Neoplatonic philosopher from Syria. Wrote on the life and doctrines of Pythagoras, claiming his teaching was divinely handed down.

    Works

    • On the Pythagorean Life

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture
