Libanius

Also known as
Libanius Sophista
Dates
c. 314 – c. 393
Role
Greek rhetorician

Greek rhetorician known as "the Sophist." Recipient of Basil's letter 140, in which Basil describes conversing with Moses, Elijah, and other blessed men.
