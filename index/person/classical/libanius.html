Libanius

Also known as
Libanius Sophista, Libanius the Sophist
Dates
c. 314–c. 393
Role
Greek rhetorician and teacher at Antioch

Greek rhetorician and teacher at Antioch. Recipient of Basil's letter 140 in which Basil tells him he converses with Moses and Elijah.
