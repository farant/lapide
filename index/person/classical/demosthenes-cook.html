Demosthenes (the cook)

Also known as
Demosthenes, prefect of the imperial kitchen
Dates
fl. 370s
Role
Prefect of Valens's imperial kitchen

Prefect of Valens's imperial kitchen who rebuked Basil in barbarian fashion. Basil replied: "We have seen an illiterate Demosthenes" and told him to look after seasoning broths.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P33 — Present at the confrontation with Basil; rebuked Basil in barbarian fashion; Basil replied "We have seen an illiterate Demosthenes" and told him to look after seasoning broths
    "a certain man named Demosthenes, prefect of the imperial kitchen, who in a thoroughly barbarian fashion rebuked Basil, master of the whole world. But Saint Basil, smiling, said: 'We have seen an illiterate Demosthenes.'"
