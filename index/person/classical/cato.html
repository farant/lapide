    Cato the Elder

    Also known as
    Cato, Marcus Porcius Cato
    Dates
    234–149 BC
    Role
    Roman statesman, orator

    Roman statesman and orator, known for learning Greek in old age. His late study of Greek is compared to Jerome's learning of Hebrew.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — Jerome learned Hebrew as Cato learned Greek letters in old age
