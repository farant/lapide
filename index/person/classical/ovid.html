    Ovid

    Also known as
    Ovidius, Publius Ovidius Naso
    Dates
    43 BC–17/18 AD
    Role
    Roman poet

    Roman poet, author of the Metamorphoses. His account of primordial chaos and the Golden Age parallels the Genesis narrative.
