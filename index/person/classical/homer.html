Homer

Also known as
Homerus
Dates
c. 8th century BC
Role
Greek poet

Greek poet. Quoted on the pastoral nature of kingship: "Homer calls a king the shepherd of the peoples, because he ought to feed them, as a shepherd feeds sheep, and not fleece them."
