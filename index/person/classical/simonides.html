    Simonides

    Also known as
    Simonides of Ceos
    Dates
    c. 556–c. 468 BC
    Role
    Greek lyric poet

    Greek lyric poet. Jerome compares David to Simonides, along with Pindar, Alcaeus, Horace, Catullus, and Serenus.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Jerome compares David to Simonides, along with Pindar, Alcaeus, Horace, Catullus, and Serenus
      "David, our Simonides, our Pindar and Alcaeus"
