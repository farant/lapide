    Apollonius of Tyana

    Also known as
    Apollonius
    Dates
    c. 15–c. 100
    Role
    Neo-Pythagorean philosopher and traveler

    Neo-Pythagorean philosopher and traveler. Whether magician or philosopher, he entered Persia, crossed the Caucasus, penetrated India, and reached the Brahmans.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Whether magician or philosopher; entered Persia, crossed the Caucasus, penetrated India, reached the Brahmans to hear Hiarchas; returned through many lands to Ethiopia to see the gymnosophists
      "Apollonius, whether he was a magician, as the common people say, or a philosopher, as the Pythagoreans hold"
