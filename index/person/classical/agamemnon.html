Agamemnon

Dates
mythological/legendary
Role
King of Mycenae, leader of the Greeks at Troy

Legendary king of Mycenae and leader of the Greek forces in the Trojan War. In Cyril's chronology, ruled among the Argives at the fall of Troy, 410 years after Moses.
