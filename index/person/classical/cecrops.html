    Cecrops

    Also known as
    Cecrops Diphyes
    Dates
    mythological/legendary
    Role
    Legendary first king of Athens

    Legendary first king of Athens, surnamed Diphyes. In Cyril's chronology, first reigned at Athens in the 35th year of Moses, first sacrificed an ox, and named Jupiter supreme god among the Greeks.
