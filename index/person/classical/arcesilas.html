Arcesilaus

Also known as
Arcesilas, Arcesilaus
Dates
c. 316–c. 241 BC
Role
Greek philosopher, head of the New Academy

Greek philosopher who headed the New (Skeptical) Academy at Athens. Went beyond Socrates's profession of ignorance, claiming not even that confession could be known.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter Ii P13 — Said not even Socrates's confession of ignorance could be known
