Aeschines

Also known as
Aeschines of Athens
Dates
c. 389–c. 314 BC
Role
Athenian orator, rival of Demosthenes

Athenian orator and rival of Demosthenes. In exile at Rhodes, heard the oration of Demosthenes read against him and sighed, "What if you had heard the beast itself?"

Works

  • Against Ctesiphon
