    Hercules

    Also known as
    Heracles
    Dates
    mythological
    Role
    Legendary hero of Greek and Roman mythology

    Legendary hero of Greek and Roman mythology, famed for his twelve labors. In Cyril's chronology, freed Theseus from the underworld.
