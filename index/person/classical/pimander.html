    Pimander

    Also known as
    Poimandres
    Dates
    legendary/Hermetic
    Role
    Figure in Hermetic texts

    Figure from the Hermetic corpus, sometimes identified as a divine mind or teacher who instructs Hermes Trismegistus. The name gives its title to the first treatise of the Corpus Hermeticum.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter I: On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture — Listed among figures who are "children" before Scripture
