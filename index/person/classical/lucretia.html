    Lucretia

    Dates
    legendary (c. 508 BC)
    Role
    Roman noblewoman, exemplar of chastity

    Legendary Roman noblewoman whose violation and subsequent suicide led to the overthrow of the Roman monarchy. Contrasted unfavorably with the biblical Susanna.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • II. The Richness of the Old Testament — Called "chaste after the violation, yet the late avenger of the crime — and a self-slayer"; contrasted with Susanna
      "They praise Lucretia, chaste after the violation"
