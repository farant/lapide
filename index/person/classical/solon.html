    Solon

    Dates
    c. 630–c. 560 BC
    Role
    Athenian lawgiver, one of the Seven Sages

    Athenian lawgiver and one of the Seven Sages of Greece. In Plato's Timaeus, an Egyptian priest tells Solon: "O Solon, you Greeks are always children."
