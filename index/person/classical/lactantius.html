    Lactantius

    Also known as
    Lucius Caecilius Firmianus Lactantius
    Dates
    c. 250–c. 325
    Role
    Christian author, apologist

    Early Christian apologist, sometimes called "the Christian Cicero." Author of the Divine Institutes and On the Wrath of God.
