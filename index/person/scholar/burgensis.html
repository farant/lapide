    Paul of Burgos

    Also known as
    Burgensis
    Dates
    c. 1351–1435
    Role
    Bishop of Burgos, Jewish convert, biblical commentator

    Bishop of Burgos and Jewish convert who wrote Additions to the Postillae of Nicholas of Lyra.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • God (Elohim): Thirteen Definitions — Cited for the plural Elohim implying plurality of persons
      "Lyranus, Burgensis"
    • Verse 9: Let the waters be gathered together — Cited for the sea being made higher
      "Basil, Burgensis, Catharinus"
    • Verse 11: Let the earth bring forth — Cited alongside Cajetan for the earth actively producing
      "Cajetan and Burgensis hold"
