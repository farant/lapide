    Sextus Pompeius Festus

    Also known as
    Festus
    Dates
    2nd century AD
    Role
    Roman grammarian

    Roman grammarian who epitomized the lexicon of Verrius Flaccus. Cited on the etymology of the name Ancus from his crooked elbow.
