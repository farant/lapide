    Averroes

    Also known as
    Ibn Rushd
    Dates
    1126–1198
    Role
    Arab philosopher, commentator on Aristotle

    Arab philosopher and the most influential medieval commentator on Aristotle. Known in the Latin West simply as "the Commentator."
