Einhard

Also known as
Einhardus
Dates
c. 775–840
Role
Frankish scholar, biographer of Charlemagne

Frankish scholar and biographer of Charlemagne. In his Life of Charlemagne, records the emperor's diligent correction of reading and chanting discipline.
