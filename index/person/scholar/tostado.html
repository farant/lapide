Alfonso Tostado

Also known as
Blessed Alfonso Tostado, Bishop of Avila, Tostado
Dates
c. 1400–1455
Role
Bishop of Avila, biblical scholar

Bishop of Avila and prolific biblical scholar. Composed individual volumes on the Decateuch and individual Old Testament history books with keen judgment.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Saintly Examples P2 — On the Decateuch and individual OT history books, composed individual volumes with keen judgment; Lapide once pored over him and rereads him
