    Diodorus Siculus

    Also known as
    Diodorus
    Dates
    c. 90–c. 30 BC
    Role
    Greek historian

    Greek historian, author of the Bibliotheca Historica. Called "the most esteemed of all historians." Lists six ancient legislators beginning with Moses.
