    Duns Scotus

    Also known as
    Scotus
    Dates
    c. 1266–1308
    Role
    Franciscan friar, Scholastic theologian

    Franciscan friar and Scholastic theologian known as the Subtle Doctor. Maintained eccentrics and epicycles against Aristotle.
