    Agostino Steuco

    Also known as
    Eugubinus
    Dates
    1497–1548
    Role
    Bishop of Kisamos, biblical scholar

    Bishop of Kisamos and biblical scholar who wrote the Cosmopoeia on the creation narrative.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • God (Elohim): Thirteen Definitions — Cited for the plural Elohim implying plurality of persons
      "Eugubinus, Catharinus"
    • Verse 6: Let there be a firmament — Cited for the view that the firmament is the middle region of air
      "Rupert, Eugubinus"
    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited alongside Oleaster for thinking God assumed human form
      "Oleaster and Eugubinus in the Cosmopoeia"
