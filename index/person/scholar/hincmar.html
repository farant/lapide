Hincmar

Also known as
Hincmarus Remensis
Dates
c. 806–882
Role
Archbishop of Reims

Archbishop of Reims who extolled the virtues of two successive bishops of Cambrai, St. Theodoric and St. John. Cited as a witness to the long tradition of saintly bishops at Cambrai.
