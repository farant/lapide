Hugh of Saint Victor

Also known as
Hugo a Sancto Victore
Dates
c. 1096–1141
Role
Augustinian canon, theologian, prior of the Abbey of Saint Victor

Augustinian canon and theologian of the Abbey of Saint Victor in Paris. Listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate version.
