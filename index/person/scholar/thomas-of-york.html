    Thomas of York

    Also known as
    Thomas the Doctor, the English Doctor, Doctor of York
    Dates
    c. 1400
    Role
    English theologian

    English theologian whose works on Genesis were commonly but erroneously cited under the name of St. Thomas Aquinas.
