Richard of Saint Victor

Also known as
Richardus a Sancto Victore
Dates
d. 1173
Role
Augustinian canon, mystic, prior of the Abbey of Saint Victor

Augustinian canon and mystic of the Abbey of Saint Victor in Paris. Listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate version.
