    Eupolemus

    Also known as
    Eupolemus
    Dates
    fl. 2nd century BC
    Role
    Jewish historian

    Jewish historian of the 2nd century BC. Cited by Eusebius as reporting that Moses transmitted letters to the Egyptians and was called Mercury.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Moses as the Most Ancient Theologian, Philosopher, Poet, and Historian — Cited by Eusebius: says Moses transmitted letters to the Egyptians and was called Mercury
