Theodoret of Cyrrhus

Also known as
Theodoretus Cyrrhensis
Dates
c. 393 – c. 458/466
Role
Bishop of Cyrrhus, Church historian

Bishop of Cyrrhus and Church historian who records the Demosthenes anecdote: a prefect of Valens's imperial kitchen rebuked Basil "in a thoroughly barbarian fashion," to which Basil retorted: "We have seen an illiterate Demosthenes."
