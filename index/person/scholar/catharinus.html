    Ambrosius Catharinus

    Also known as
    Catharinus
    Dates
    1484–1553
    Role
    Dominican theologian

    Dominican theologian who participated in the Council of Trent and wrote extensively on Genesis.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — God Elohim P26 — Cited for Elohim implying plurality of persons

      "Catharinus answers first that it was the most brilliant sun"

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 6 P14 — Cited for the reason God did not say "it was good" on the second day
