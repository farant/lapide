    Christopher Clavius

    Also known as
    Fr. Clavius
    Dates
    1538–1612
    Role
    Jesuit mathematician and astronomer

    Jesuit mathematician and astronomer who reformed the calendar under Pope Gregory XIII and wrote the influential Commentarius in Sphaeram.
