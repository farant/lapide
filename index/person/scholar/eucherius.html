    Eucherius of Lyon

    Also known as
    Eucherius of Lyon
    Dates
    d. c. 449
    Role
    Bishop of Lyon, theological writer

    Bishop of Lyon and theological writer. Author of On Spiritual Forms, in which he imitated Augustine's collection of Scripture phrases.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — Imitated Augustine's collection of Scripture phrases in his book On Spiritual Forms
      "This was later imitated by Eucherius of Lyon in his book On Spiritual Forms"

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Introduction — Listed among those who wrote on Genesis
      "Eucherius, Rupert, and others wrote on Genesis."
    • Verse 24: Let the Earth Bring Forth the Living Creature — Cited for the tropological application of the six days
      "so from Eucherius, Origen, and Hugh, says Pererius."
    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited from Book I on Genesis on image and likeness
      "Eucherius, Book I on Genesis"
    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited for the tropological interpretation of man's dominion
      "So say Origen, Chrysostom, and Eucherius."
