Rabanus Maurus

Dates
c. 780–856
Role
Archbishop of Mainz, Benedictine monk, theologian

Archbishop of Mainz and Benedictine theologian. Listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate version. Venerated locally but not formally canonized.
