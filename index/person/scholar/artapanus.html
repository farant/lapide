Artapanus

Also known as
Artapanus
Dates
fl. 2nd century BC
Role
Egyptian Jewish historian

Egyptian Jewish historian of the 2nd century BC. With Eupolemus, cited by Eusebius as reporting that Moses transmitted letters to the Egyptians, was called Mercury, and was worshipped as a god.
