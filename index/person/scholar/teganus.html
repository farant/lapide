Teganus

Also known as
Thegan
Dates
d. c. 849
Role
Frankish chronicler

Frankish chronicler. In his Life of Louis, attests that Charlemagne near death gave himself to prayers, almsgiving, and sacred Letters, and corrected the four Gospels against Greek and Syriac.
