    Henricus Samerius

    Dates
    d. before 1637
    Role
    Reverend Father; elaborated the chronology table used by Lapide

    Reverend Father, described as "of blessed memory." Elaborated with precision the sacred chronology table that Lapide received and corrected for his commentary.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Sacred Chronology — Elaborated the chronology with precision; had some errors that Lapide purged; omits Cainan; gives Saul 40 years after Samuel
      "I received it from the Reverend Father Henricus Samerius of blessed memory, who elaborated it with precision"
