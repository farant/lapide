Peter Lombard

Also known as
Petrus Lombardus
Dates
c. 1096–1160
Role
Bishop of Paris, theologian, author of the Sentences

Bishop of Paris and theologian whose Sentences became the standard theological textbook of the medieval West. Listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate version over the preceding nine hundred years.
