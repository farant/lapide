Peter Lombard

Also known as
Petrus Lombardus
Dates
c. 1096–1160
Role
Bishop of Paris, theologian, author of the Sentences

Bishop of Paris and theologian. Author of the Sentences, the standard theological textbook of the Middle Ages. Among the scholars who used Jerome's version of Scripture exclusively.
