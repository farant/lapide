    Augustin Crampon

    Also known as
    Augustinus Crampon, Aug. Crampon
    Dates
    1826–1894
    Role
    French biblical scholar, priest of the Diocese of Amiens; annotator of Cornelius a Lapide's commentaries

    French biblical scholar and priest of the Diocese of Amiens. Revised and annotated Cornelius a Lapide's commentaries with notes.

    Works

    • Annotations on Lapide's Commentaries

    Preliminaries

    • Title Page — Named as the one who revised and illustrated the commentaries with notes
      "Carefully revised and illustrated with notes by Augustinus Crampon, Priest of the Diocese of Amiens."
    • Permissions P4 — His annotations to the Commentaries on the Pentateuch approved for printing by the Bishop of Amiens in 1852
      "Annotations by which Aug. Crampon, priest of the diocese of Amiens, has illustrated and enriched Father Cornelius a Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch."
