    Procopius of Gaza

    Also known as
    Procopius
    Dates
    c. 465–c. 528
    Role
    Christian rhetorician, biblical commentator

    Christian rhetorician and biblical commentator from Gaza. Published questions and phrases on the Pentateuch.
