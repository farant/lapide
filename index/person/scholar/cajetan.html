    Thomas de Vio Cajetan

    Also known as
    Cajetan, Cardinal Cajetan
    Dates
    1469–1534
    Role
    Dominican theologian, cardinal

    Dominican theologian and cardinal, best known for his commentaries on the Summa Theologica of Thomas Aquinas.
