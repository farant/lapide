    Diodorus of Tarsus

    Also known as
    Diodorus
    Dates
    d. c. 390
    Role
    Bishop of Tarsus, theologian

    Bishop of Tarsus and teacher of John Chrysostom and Theodore of Mopsuestia. Exegete who favored literal interpretation.
