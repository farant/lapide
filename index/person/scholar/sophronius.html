Sophronius

Dates
fl. 4th–5th century
Role
Scholar who translated Jerome's Psalter and Prophets into Greek

Scholar who, observing Jerome's translation was highly approved, translated the Psalter and Prophets from Jerome's version into elegant Greek. Not to be confused with Sophronius, Patriarch of Jerusalem.

Works

  • Greek Translation of Jerome's Psalter and Prophets

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Preface Reader P6 — A most erudite man who translated the Psalter and the Prophets from Jerome's version into elegant Greek
    "Sophronius also, a most erudite man, observing that St. Jerome's translation was highly approved not only by Latins but also by Greeks, valued it so greatly that he translated the Psalter and the Prophets from Jerome's version into elegant Greek."
