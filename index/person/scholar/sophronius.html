Sophronius

Dates
fl. late 4th century
Role
Scholar and translator

Scholar and translator who valued Jerome's translation so highly that he rendered the Psalter and the Prophets from Jerome's Latin version into elegant Greek, demonstrating the Vulgate's acceptance among Greek-speaking scholars as well as Latins.
