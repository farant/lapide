Jacob

Also known as
Israel, Iacob
Dates
biblical patriarch
Role
Patriarch, son of Isaac, father of the twelve tribes

Patriarch, son of Isaac, father of the twelve tribes of Israel. Listed among the ancient patriarchs who held both supreme powers (prince and priest) in conjunction.
