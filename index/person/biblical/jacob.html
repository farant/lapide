Jacob

Also known as
Israel
Dates
c. 2nd millennium BC
Role
Patriarch; son of Isaac, father of the twelve tribes

Patriarch, son of Isaac. Named among those ancient patriarchs in whom both supreme powers were joined, and in Moses's genealogy as father of Levi.
