Paul the Apostle

Also known as
Paulus, the Apostle Saint Paul
Dates
c. 5–c. 64/67 AD
Role
Apostle to the Gentiles

Apostle to the Gentiles. Fourteen epistles attributed to him in the Tridentine canon.
