Paul the Apostle

Also known as
Paulus Apostolus, St. Paul
Dates
c. 5 – c. 64/67
Role
Apostle to the Gentiles

Apostle to the Gentiles. Referenced in the Trent canon list (fourteen epistles), in the catalogue of Lapide's works, and in the dedicatory letter where Lapide mentions "the Pauline commentaries" as establishing his method.
