Titus

Also known as
St. Titus
Dates
d. c. 96–107
Role
Bishop of Crete, companion of Paul

Bishop of Crete and companion of Paul. Paul commanded Titus that a bishop should hold fast the faithful word and choose knowledge of the Scriptures.
