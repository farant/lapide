    Noah

    Role
    Patriarch who survived the flood

    Patriarch who survived the flood through the ark. The flood through the ark was for the faithful a type of baptism.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Listed among those given meaningful names
      "That this is so is clear in the names Adam, Eve, Seth, Cain, Noah, Abraham"
    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — The flood through the ark was for the faithful a type of baptism
      "the flood, insofar as Noah survived it through the ark, was for the faithful a type of baptism"
    • Sacred Chronology — Chronological entry: year of the world 1056
      "Years from the beginning of the world to Noah: 1056"
    • Sacred Chronology — Lamech begets Noah
      "Lamech, 182 years old, begets Noah."
    • Sacred Chronology — Noah, 500 years old, begets Shem, Ham, and Japheth
      "Noah, 500 years old, begets Shem, Ham, and Japheth."
    • Sacred Chronology — Noah in his 600th year of life; the flood ceases
      "with Noah in his 600th year of life"

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Introduction — Named as a Patriarch whose deeds are narrated in Genesis
      "especially of the Patriarchs Noah, Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, and Joseph."
    • Introduction — Named as beginning the second part of Genesis
      "The second contains the deeds from Noah and the flood up to Abraham"
    • Verse 26: Let Us Make Man in Our Image and Likeness — Cited for his dominion over all the animals of the ark
      "such as Noah over all the animals of the ark"
