Gamaliel

Also known as
Rabban Gamaliel, Gamaliel the Elder
Dates
d. c. 50
Role
Pharisee, teacher of the Law, Paul's instructor

Pharisee and teacher of the Law. Paul boasts of learning the law of Moses and the Prophets at his feet.
