    Habakkuk

    Also known as
    Habakkuk the Prophet
    Role
    Minor prophet

    Minor prophet. Called "the strong and unyielding wrestler"; stands upon his watch and contemplates Christ on the cross.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Called "the strong and unyielding wrestler"; stands upon his watch; contemplates Christ on the cross; quotes "His glory covered the heavens"
      "Habakkuk, the strong and unyielding wrestler, stands upon his watch and sets his foot upon the fortress"
