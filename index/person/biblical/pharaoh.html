Pharaoh

Also known as
Pharao
Dates
Exodus era
Role
King of Egypt, oppressor of the Hebrews

King of Egypt who oppressed the Hebrews. Moses was educated at his court, then rescued his people from Pharaoh's power, and withstood him and his magicians with constancy.
