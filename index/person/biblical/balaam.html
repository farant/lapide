    Balaam

    Role
    Non-Israelite prophet

    Non-Israelite prophet. His prophecy in Numbers contains mysteries.

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P13 — Numbers contains the mysteries of Balaam's prophecy
      "Do not Numbers contain the mysteries of all arithmetic, and of Balaam's prophecy"
    • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Canons P40 — Says Israel will devastate Moab, Edom, and the sons of Seth; by Israel understands both David and Christ
      "So Balaam, saying that Israel will devastate Moab, Edom, and the sons of Seth (Numbers 24:17)"
    • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Canons P47 — Sings of Christ the Savior: "He shall strike the leaders of Moab"
      "Balaam sings in Numbers 24:17 of Christ the Savior"
