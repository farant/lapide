Martha

Dates
1st century
Role
Disciple of Christ, sister of Mary and Lazarus

Disciple of Christ, emblematic of the active life. Lapide identifies with her in his younger years: "Once in my younger days I played Martha," before turning in old age to the contemplative life of Mary Magdalene.
