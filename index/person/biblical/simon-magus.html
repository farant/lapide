    Simon Magus

    Also known as
    Simon
    Dates
    1st century
    Role
    Heretic, magician

    First-century magician and heresiarch whose heresy proscribed the Old Testament, teaching it was produced by a sinister power and evil angels.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Heresies Proscribing the Old Testament — His heresy proscribed the Old Testament; taught it was produced by a sinister power and evil angels
      "It was the heresy of Simon Magus and his followers"

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • God (Elohim): Thirteen Definitions — Listed as erring about God creating the Son
      "Simon Magus, Arius, and others err, who say that God created the Son"
