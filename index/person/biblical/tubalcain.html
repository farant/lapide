    Tubalcain

    Role
    Biblical figure; inventor of instruments

    Descendant of Cain, called the "father" of those who play instruments, meaning the first and inventor of the instrument.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Called the "father" of those who play instruments, meaning inventor
      "Thus Tubalcain is called the "father" of those who play instruments: father, that is, the first and inventor of the instrument."
