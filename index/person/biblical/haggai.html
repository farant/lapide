    Haggai

    Also known as
    Haggai the Prophet
    Dates
    6th century BC
    Role
    Minor prophet

    Minor prophet. Festive and joyful, he sowed in tears to reap in joy; builds the destroyed temple and introduces God speaking about the Desired of all nations.
