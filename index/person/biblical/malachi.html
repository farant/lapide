Malachi

Also known as
Malachi the Prophet
Dates
5th century BC
Role
Last of the twelve minor prophets

Last of the twelve minor prophets. Openly prophesies the rejection of Israel and calling of the nations.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Cited as speaking (or rather the Lord through Haggai): "Ask the priests the law"
    "The prophet Malachi, or rather the Lord through Haggai, says: 'Ask the priests the law.'"
  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P33 — Openly prophesies the rejection of Israel and calling of the nations; quotes "from the rising of the sun to its setting, my name is great among the nations"
    "Malachi, openly, and at the end of all the Prophets, concerning the rejection of Israel and the calling of the nations"
  • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Canons P23 — Calls John the Baptist "Elijah"
    "John the Baptist is called Elijah by Malachi"
