Melchizedek

Also known as
Melchisedech
Dates
patriarchal era
Role
King of Salem, priest of God Most High

King of Salem and priest of God Most High. Named first among those ancient figures in whom both supreme powers -- that of the prince and of the priest -- were joined, establishing the typological pattern applied to Moses and Van der Burch.
