    Oholibamah

    Also known as
    Basemath
    Role
    Wife of Esau; daughter of Anah

    Wife of Esau, called Oholibamah in Genesis 36:2 but Basemath in Genesis 26:34.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Called Oholibamah in Genesis 36:2 but Basemath in Genesis 26:34
      "his other wife who in Genesis 36:2 is called Oholibamah, daughter of Anah, in Genesis 26:34 is called Basemath"
