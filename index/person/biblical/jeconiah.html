    Jeconiah

    Also known as
    Jehoiachin, Coniah
    Dates
    6th century BC
    Role
    King of Judah, deported to Babylon

    King of Judah, deported to Babylon. The books of Kings describe the kingdom of Judah from Solomon to Jeconiah.
