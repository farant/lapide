Biblical Figures

  • Aaron (c. 13th century BC) — High Priest, brother of Moses
  • Abraham (c. 2nd millennium BC) — Patriarch; ancestor of Moses
  • Daniel (6th century BC) — Prophet
  • Elijah (9th century BC) — Prophet
  • Isaac (c. 2nd millennium BC) — Patriarch; son of Abraham, father of Jacob
  • Jacob (c. 2nd millennium BC) — Patriarch; son of Isaac, father of the twelve tribes
  • Jesus Christ (c. 4 BC – c. AD 33) — Son of God, Messiah, Redeemer
  • Job (ancient) — Righteous sufferer
  • John the Apostle (1st century) — Apostle, Evangelist
  • Joseph (patriarchal era) — Patriarch, son of Jacob; steward of Egypt during famine
  • Martha (1st century) — Disciple of Christ, sister of Mary and Lazarus
  • Mary Magdalene (1st century) — Disciple of Christ, penitent
  • Melchizedek (patriarchal era) — King of Salem, priest of God Most High
  • Moses (c. 13th century BC) — Prophet, lawgiver, leader of the Hebrews; author of the Pentateuch
  • Paul the Apostle (c. 5 – c. 64/67) — Apostle to the Gentiles
  • Pharaoh (Exodus era) — King of Egypt, oppressor of the Hebrews
