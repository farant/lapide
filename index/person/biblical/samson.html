    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • II. The Richness of the Old Testament — Listed among great military leaders endowed with heavenly strength

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Sacred Chronology — The war and destruction of Troy occurred in the time of Samson and Eli
      "which occurred in the time of Samson and the high priest Eli"
