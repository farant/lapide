    Amos

    Also known as
    Amos the Prophet
    Dates
    8th century BC
    Role
    Minor prophet, shepherd

    Minor prophet and shepherd. A rustic plucking mulberries who speaks to the fat cows on the mountain of Samaria.

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P24 — A shepherd and rustic, plucking mulberries; speaks to the fat cows on the mountain of Samaria; sees a hook of fruit and a famine of hearing the word of God
      "Amos, a shepherd and a rustic, plucking mulberries from brambles, cannot be explained in a few words."
