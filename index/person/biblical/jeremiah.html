    Jeremiah

    Also known as
    Jeremiah the Prophet
    Dates
    c. 650–c. 570 BC
    Role
    Major prophet

    Major prophet. The second of the great prophets; intertwines an almond rod, a boiling pot, a leopard, and a fourfold alphabet.
