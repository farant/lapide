Lazarus

Also known as
Lazarus of Bethany
Role
Friend of Christ, raised from the dead

Friend of Christ, raised from the dead. Christ loves Lazarus, the man of mature years, and the tears of his friendship flow upon him.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Du Culte P8 — Christ loves Lazarus, the man of mature years

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Du Culte P47 — The reader will see the tears of Christ's friendship flow upon Lazarus
