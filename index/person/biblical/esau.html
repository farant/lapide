    Esau

    Also known as
    Edom
    Role
    Son of Isaac, twin of Jacob; ancestor of the Edomites

    Son of Isaac and twin brother of Jacob. Ancestor of the Edomites. His wives have different names in different chapters of Genesis.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — The mountains were called Seir long after Moses, named by Esau
      "he calls the mountains Seir, which were called Seir only long after, by Esau"
    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Dominion over Esau (the Edomites) promised to Jacob
      "dominion over Esau, that is, the Edomites, is promised"
    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Esau's wives have different names in different chapters
      "the wife of Esau who in Genesis 36:2 is called Adah"
    • Sacred Chronology — Isaac begets Esau and Jacob
      "Isaac, 60 years old, begets Esau and Jacob."
    • Sacred Chronology — Esau at forty marries the daughter of Beeri the Hittite
      "Esau at forty marries the daughter of Beeri the Hittite."
