    Heroine of the Book of Daniel, champion of chastity

    Heroine of the Book of Daniel who chose death over sin; celebrated as a far braver champion of chastity than the pagan Lucretia.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • II. The Richness of the Old Testament — Celebrated as far braver champion of chastity and life than Lucretia
      "we celebrate Susanna, a far braver champion of chastity as well as of life and reputation."
