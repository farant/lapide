    Philip the Deacon

    Also known as
    Philip, the Evangelist Philip
    Dates
    1st century
    Role
    One of the Seven Deacons, evangelist

    One of the Seven Deacons and an evangelist. Came to the Ethiopian eunuch reading Isaiah and showed him Jesus hidden in the letter.

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Came to the Ethiopian eunuch reading Isaiah and showed him Jesus hidden in the letter; the eunuch was baptized in the same hour
      "Philip came and showed him Jesus, who lay hidden, shut up in the letter."
