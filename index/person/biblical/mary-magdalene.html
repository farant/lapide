Mary Magdalene

Also known as
Maria Magdalena
Dates
1st century
Role
Disciple of Christ, penitent

Disciple of Christ, emblematic of the contemplative life. Lapide identifies with her in his old age: "now in the declining slope of age I play and love the part of Mary Magdalene more," contrasting with his earlier active years as Martha.
