Mary Magdalene

Dates
1st century
Role
Disciple of Christ

Disciple of Christ. Lapide says he now plays "the part of Mary Magdalene" in contemplation.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Life P2 — Lapide says he now plays "the part of Mary Magdalene" in contemplation, contrasting with his earlier role as Martha
    "Once in my younger days I played Martha; now in the declining slope of age I play and love the part of Mary Magdalene more"
  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Du Culte P47 — Named as one of those with Christ at the end; Jerome calls her "Magdalene"
    "it will be in the hands of John, of Mary, and of Magdalene that he will find the last farewell of earth"
  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses And Christ P5 — Christ washed Mary Magdalene with heavenly grace (parallel 14 with Moses/Miriam)
    "Christ washed Mary Magdalene, overwhelmed with the stains of sins, with heavenly grace."
