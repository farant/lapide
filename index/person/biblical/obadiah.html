    Obadiah

    Also known as
    Obadiah the Prophet
    Role
    Minor prophet

    Minor prophet. His name means "servant of God." Thunders against Edom, the earthly man, and strikes the rival of Jacob.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — His name means "servant of God"; thunders against Edom, the earthly man; strikes the rival of Jacob
      "Obadiah, whose name means servant of God, thunders against Edom, the bloody and earthly man"
