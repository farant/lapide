Simeon

Also known as
the old man Simeon, Simeon the Elder
Role
Devout man in Jerusalem who received the infant Jesus in the temple

Devout man in Jerusalem who received the infant Jesus in the temple. Speaks the supreme word at the Presentation: "Now, Lord, you will let your servant depart in peace."
