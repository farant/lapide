    Nehemiah

    Also known as
    Nehemiah, consoler from the Lord
    Dates
    5th century BC
    Role
    Governor of Judah, rebuilder of the walls of Jerusalem

    Governor of Judah and rebuilder of the walls of Jerusalem. With Ezra, compressed into one volume; restores the temple and builds the walls of the city.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — With Ezra, compressed into one volume; restores the temple, builds walls of the city
      "Ezra and Nehemiah -- that is, helper and consoler from the Lord -- are compressed into one volume"
