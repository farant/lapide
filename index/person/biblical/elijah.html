Elijah

Also known as
Elias
Dates
9th century BC
Role
Prophet

Prophet of Israel. Invoked alongside Moses in Basil's letter 140 to Libanius, where Basil describes conversing with Moses, Elijah, and other blessed men who impart their teaching.
