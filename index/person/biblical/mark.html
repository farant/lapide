    Mark

    Also known as
    Mark the Evangelist, Marcus
    Dates
    1st century
    Role
    Evangelist

    Evangelist. His Gospel is listed second in the Tridentine canon.

    Preliminaries

    • DECREES OF THE COUNCIL OF TRENT (SESSION IV). — His Gospel listed second
      "according to Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John"

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Named as one of the four evangelists
      "Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John -- the four-horse chariot of the Lord and the true Cherubim"

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — Instituted Christian study of sacred Letters at Alexandria in the apostolic age
      "St. Mark instituted this Christian study of the sacred Letters at Alexandria."
