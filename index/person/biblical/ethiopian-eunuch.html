Ethiopian Eunuch

Also known as
the holy Eunuch, the Candace's treasurer
Dates
1st century
Role
Official of the Ethiopian queen, converted by Philip

Official of the Ethiopian queen, converted by Philip. Was reading Isaiah in his chariot and asked Philip for help understanding the Scripture.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P2 — Was reading Isaiah in his chariot; asked Philip "How can I, unless someone teaches me?"; believed and was baptized in the same hour; Jerome says he is neither holier nor more studious than this eunuch
    "the holy Eunuch -- or rather man (for thus Scripture names him) -- when he was reading Isaiah the prophet, asked by Philip: 'Do you think you understand what you are reading?' answered: 'How can I, unless someone teaches me?'"
