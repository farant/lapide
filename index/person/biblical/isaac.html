Isaac

Dates
c. 2nd millennium BC
Role
Patriarch; son of Abraham, father of Jacob

Patriarch, son of Abraham and father of Jacob. Named among those ancient patriarchs in whom both supreme powers were joined, and in Moses's genealogy.
