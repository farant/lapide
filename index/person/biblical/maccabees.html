The Maccabees

Also known as
Judas Maccabeus and his brothers
Dates
2nd century BC
Role
Jewish liberators and leaders of the revolt against Seleucid rule

Jewish liberators and leaders of the revolt against Seleucid rule. Purified the temple that had been defiled by the nations, and delivered their homeland from the yoke of the foreigner.
