    St. John of Cambrai

    Dates
    fl. 7th century
    Role
    Bishop of Cambrai

    Bishop of Cambrai, successor of St. Theodoric. Celebrated by Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims.

    Preliminaries

    • TO THE MOST REVEREND AND MOST ILLUSTRIOUS LORD HENRY FRANCIS VAN DER BURCH, ARCHBISHOP AND DUKE OF CAMBRAI, PRINCE OF THE HOLY ROMAN EMPIRE, COUNT OF CAMBRAI. — Successor of Theodoric; celebrated by Hincmar
      "Likewise Saint John, his successor, celebrated by the same Hincmar."
