St. Gregory Nazianzen

Also known as
Gregory of Nazianzus, Gregorius Nazianzenus, Gregory the Theologian, Blessed Gregory Nazianzen
Dates
c. 329–390
Role
Archbishop of Constantinople, Doctor of the Church, Church Father

One of the three Cappadocian Fathers and Archbishop of Constantinople. Known as "the Theologian" for the depth of his doctrinal orations. Close friend and peer of Basil the Great.

Works

  • De Fide
  • Oration 2 (Apologeticus)
  • Oration 22
  • Oration in Praise of Saint Basil
