St. Ephrem the Syrian

Also known as
Ephraem Syrus, Ephraem
Dates
c. 306–373
Role
Deacon, hymnographer, Doctor of the Church

Syrian deacon, hymnographer, and Doctor of the Church. Known as "the Harp of the Spirit."

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P23 — Saw a dove (sign of the Holy Spirit) whispering a sermon into Basil's ear while Basil was preaching
    "While Saint Basil was preaching, Saint Ephrem saw a dove whispering the sermon into his ear — a dove, I say, which is the sign and hieroglyph of the Holy Spirit, as Gregory of Nyssa testifies."
