Pope St. Cornelius

Also known as
Cornelius Papa
Dates
d. 253
Role
Pope, martyr

Pope and martyr, praised by St. Cyprian for having ascended through every office of the Church before reaching the episcopate. Lapide cites Cyprian's testimony to illustrate the ideal of gradual, humble advancement to ecclesiastical office.
