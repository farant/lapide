St. Theodoric of Cambrai

Also known as
Theodoricus, Theodericus
Dates
fl. 7th century
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai whose virtues were extolled by Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims. Near equal of St. Gaugericus.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P44 — Near equal of Gaugericus; his virtues extolled by Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims
    "Saint Theodoric was his near equal, whose virtues Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims, extols."
