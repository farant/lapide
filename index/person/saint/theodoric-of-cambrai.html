St. Theodoric of Cambrai

Also known as
Theodoricus Cameracensis
Dates
fl. 7th century
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai whose virtues were extolled by Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims. Lapide describes him as the near equal of his predecessor Gaugericus.
