St. Vindicianus

Also known as
Vindicianus Cameracensis
Dates
d. c. 712
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai noted for his devotion to building churches and monasteries. Lapide cites him among the saintly bishops of Cambrai in the dedicatory letter to Van der Burch.

References in Commentary
