    St. Benedict of Nursia

    Also known as
    Benedictus, Blessed Benedict
    Dates
    c. 480–547
    Role
    Founder of Western monasticism, Patriarch of Monks

    Founder of Western monasticism and Patriarch of Monks. His vision of the whole world gathered in a single ray of light is recorded by Gregory the Great.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — Praying at a window, saw a light greater than day in which the whole world was gathered; saw the soul of Germanus of Capua carried to heaven
      "the Blessed Benedict, praying one evening at a window, saw a light so great"
