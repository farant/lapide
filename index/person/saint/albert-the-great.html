St. Albert the Great

Also known as
Albertus Magnus
Dates
c. 1200–1280
Role
Dominican friar, Bishop of Regensburg, Doctor of the Church

Dominican friar, Bishop of Regensburg, and Doctor of the Church, known as the "Universal Doctor" for the breadth of his learning. Teacher of St. Thomas Aquinas. In the Preliminares he is listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate translation.
