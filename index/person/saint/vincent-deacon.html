    St. Vincent

    Also known as
    Vincentius
    Dates
    d. 304
    Role
    Deacon, martyr at Valencia

    Deacon and martyr at Valencia. Conquered and laughed at the torments of the Roman governor Dacian by raising his mind to heaven.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 16 P16 — Described as conquering and laughing at the torments of Dacian; quoted his replies while on the rack
      "So St. Vincent, raising his mind to heaven, conquered, nay laughed at all the torments of Dacian"
