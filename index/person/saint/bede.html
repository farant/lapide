St. Bede the Venerable

Also known as
Beda Venerabilis
Dates
c. 673–735
Role
Monk, Doctor of the Church, historian

Benedictine monk, Doctor of the Church, and father of English history. In the Preliminares he is listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate translation.
