St. John the Baptist

Also known as
Ioannes Baptista
Dates
1st century
Role
Prophet, Forerunner of Christ, martyr

The Forerunner and Baptizer of Christ, greatest of those born of women. In the Preliminares his words "He must increase, but I must decrease" (John 3:30) are quoted as a model of humility in the context of episcopal self-effacement.
