St. Peter Damian

Also known as
Petrus Damiani
Dates
c. 1007–1072
Role
Cardinal-Bishop of Ostia, Doctor of the Church, reformer

Cardinal-Bishop of Ostia, Doctor of the Church, and leading figure of the eleventh-century Gregorian reform. In the Preliminares he is listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate translation.
