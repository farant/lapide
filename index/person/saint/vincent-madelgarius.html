St. Vincent Madelgarius

Also known as
Vincentius Comes Hannoniae
Dates
c. 615–c. 677
Role
Count of Hainaut, monk

Count of Hainaut who renounced his secular life to become a monk. Listed among the companions who assisted St. Authbert in the evangelization of Hainault. Husband of St. Waldetrudis.
