    St. Bartholomew

    Also known as
    Bartholomaeus
    Dates
    1st century
    Role
    Apostle

    One of the twelve Apostles; cited by Dionysius the Areopagite as saying that Theology is both very great and very small, the Gospel both broad and concise.
