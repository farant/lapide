St. Athanasius of Alexandria

Also known as
Athanasius
Dates
c. 296–373
Role
Bishop of Alexandria, Doctor of the Church

Bishop of Alexandria and Doctor of the Church. Defender of Nicene orthodoxy against Arianism. Author of the Life of Antony.
