    St. Symphorian

    Also known as
    Symphorianus
    Dates
    d. c. 178
    Role
    Christian martyr

    Early Christian martyr who was executed under Emperor Aurelian. His mother encouraged him to look to heaven as he was being dragged to martyrdom.

    References in Commentary
