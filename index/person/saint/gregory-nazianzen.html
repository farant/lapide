St. Gregory Nazianzen

Also known as
Gregorius Nazianzenus, Gregory the Theologian
Dates
c. 329–390
Role
Church Father, Bishop of Constantinople, Doctor of the Church

One of the three Cappadocian Fathers and peer of St. Basil, with whom he studied at Athens. Known as "the Theologian" for the depth of his doctrinal thought. His Oration in Praise of Saint Basil is the primary biographical source for Basil's life and is quoted extensively in Lapide's dedicatory letter.
