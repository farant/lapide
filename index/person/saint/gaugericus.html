St. Gaugericus

Also known as
Gaugericus Cameracensis
Dates
c. 550–c. 625
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai for thirty-nine years, noted for his miracles and his devotion to freeing prisoners. Lapide relates that even as a boy he was "most strongly inclined toward sacred things," and that he miraculously freed prisoners from dungeons and chains.
