    St. Gaugericus of Cambrai

    Also known as
    Géry, Géry of Cambrai
    Dates
    c. 550–c. 625
    Role
    Bishop of Cambrai

    Bishop of Cambrai for thirty-nine years. Even as a boy was inclined toward sacred things; miraculously freed prisoners from dungeons and chains; built many churches.
