St. Vincent Madelgarius

Also known as
Vincentius Madelgarius, Madelgarius, Vincent Madelgaire, Vincent Count of Hainault
Dates
c. 607–c. 677
Role
Count of Hainault, later monk and saint

Count of Hainault who later became a monk. Husband of St. Waldetrudis. Listed among companions who assisted St. Authbert in evangelizing Hainault.
