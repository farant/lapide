St. Cyprian of Carthage

Also known as
Cyprianus Carthaginensis, Thascius Caecilius Cyprianus
Dates
c. 210–258
Role
Bishop of Carthage, Church Father, martyr

Bishop of Carthage and early Church Father, martyred during the Valerian persecution. In the Preliminares he is quoted at length from his letter to Antonianus, praising Pope St. Cornelius for his gradual ascent through all offices of the Church to the episcopate.
