St. Macarius of Antioch

Also known as
Macarius Antiochenus
Dates
d. c. 1012
Role
Archbishop of Antioch in Armenia

Archbishop of Antioch in Armenia whose relics were venerated in the Low Countries. Van der Burch sent his body to the city of Mons during a plague; the pestilence receded upon the relics' arrival, and the people of Mons erected a silver reliquary in thanksgiving.
