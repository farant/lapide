    St. Gregory Thaumaturgus

    Also known as
    Gregory the Wonder-worker
    Dates
    c. 213–c. 270
    Role
    Bishop of Neocaesarea, wonder-worker

    Bishop of Neocaesarea and wonder-worker. At the Virgin's command, received from St. John an explanation of the beginning of his Gospel.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — A client of the Blessed Virgin; at her admonition in a dream, received from St. John an explanation of the beginning of his Gospel
      "Gregory, surnamed Thaumaturgus, a client of the Blessed Virgin, at her admonition and command in a dream, received from St. John an explanation of the beginning of his Gospel"
