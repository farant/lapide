    St. Lucian

    Also known as
    Lucian
    Dates
    d. 312
    Role
    Priest, martyr, biblical scholar who made a new Greek edition of the Old Testament from Hebrew

    Priest and martyr under Diocletian; undertook a new edition of the Old Testament from Hebrew into Greek, making him the seventh translator after the Septuagint tradition.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture
