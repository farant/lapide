St. Gregory of Nyssa

Also known as
Gregorius Nyssenus, Gregory Nyssenus
Dates
c. 335–c. 395
Role
Bishop of Nyssa, Church Father, brother of Basil the Great

Bishop of Nyssa and one of the three Cappadocian Fathers. Brother of Basil the Great.
