St. Gregory of Nyssa

Also known as
Gregorius Nyssenus
Dates
c. 335–c. 395
Role
Church Father, Bishop of Nyssa

One of the three Cappadocian Fathers and younger brother of St. Basil the Great. In the Preliminares he appears as a witness to Basil's holiness: he testified about the dove whispering to Basil during his preaching, and reported that Basil gave away his entire inheritance to feed the poor.
