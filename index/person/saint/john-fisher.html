St. John Fisher

Also known as
Ioannes Fischerus, Bishop of Rochester, John Fisher the Martyr
Dates
1469–1535
Role
Bishop of Rochester, Cardinal, martyr; canonized 1935

Bishop of Rochester, Cardinal, and martyr. Raised to the bishopric for his learning and innocence of life. Refused Henry VIII's offers of grander sees, saying he would not abandon his first bride (Rochester). Canonized in 1935.
