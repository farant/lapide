St. Isidore of Seville

Also known as
Isidorus, Isidorus Hispalensis
Dates
c. 560–636
Role
Archbishop of Seville, Doctor of the Church

Archbishop of Seville and Doctor of the Church. Preferred Jerome's version of Scripture to all others and affirmed that it is commonly received and approved by Christian churches.
