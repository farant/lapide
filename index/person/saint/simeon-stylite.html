    St. Simeon the Stylite

    Also known as
    Simeon Stylites
    Dates
    c. 388–459
    Role
    Ascetic, pillar-saint

    Ascetic and pillar-saint. Converted immediately by the Gospel beatitudes; stood on a pillar for eighty continuous years, praying day and night.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter I: On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture — Converted immediately by the Gospel beatitudes; stood on one foot atop a pillar for eighty continuous years
      "A single reading of the Gospel was able... to convert Simeon the Stylite immediately"
