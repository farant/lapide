    St. Ignatius of Loyola

    Also known as
    Ignatius
    Dates
    1491–1556
    Role
    Founder of the Society of Jesus

    Founder of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits). Known for his Spiritual Exercises and his exclamation upon looking at the heavens.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 14: Let There Be Lights in the Firmament — Quoted: "How the earth seems vile to me when I look upon heaven!"
