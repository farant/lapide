    St. John the Anchorite

    Also known as
    Ioannes Anachoreta
    Dates
    fl. 4th century
    Role
    Desert hermit

    Desert hermit who taught by word and example about purity of heart as prerequisite for knowledge. Reported by Palladius in the Lausiac History.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — Taught by word and example about purity of heart as prerequisite for knowledge; reported by Palladius in Lausiac History, ch. 40
