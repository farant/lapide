    St. Vincent Ferrer

    Also known as
    Vincentius Ferrerius
    Dates
    c. 1350–1419
    Role
    Dominican friar, itinerant preacher

    Dominican friar and celebrated itinerant preacher who converted tens of thousands across Europe, carrying only a Breviary and the Bible.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Saintly Examples of Scripture Study — Traveled through Italy, France, Germany, England, and Spain; converted at least a hundred thousand people; carried only a Breviary and the Bible
      "That St. Vincent Ferrer, who in the memory of our great-grandfathers, traveling through Italy, France, Germany, England, and Spain, converted at least a hundred thousand people"
