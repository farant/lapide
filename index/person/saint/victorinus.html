Victorinus

Also known as
Victorinus Rhetor
Dates
fl. 4th century
Role
Rhetorician of Rome, convert from paganism

Rhetorician of Rome who was turned from pagan superstition and pride to Christian faith and humility by divine Scripture.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter I P9 — Turned from pagan superstition and pride to Christian faith and humility by divine Scripture
    "Divine Scripture was able to turn Victorinus, then a puffed-up Rhetorician of the city, from pagan superstition and pride to Christian faith and humility."
