St. Remigius

Also known as
Remigius Autissiodorensis, Remigius of Auxerre, Remigius of Rheims
Dates
fl. 9th century (if Remigius of Auxerre) or c. 437–533 (if Remigius of Rheims)
Role
Biblical commentator / Bishop of Rheims

Listed first among scholars who used Jerome's version of Scripture so exclusively that other versions became obsolete.
