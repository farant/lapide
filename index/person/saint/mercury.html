    St. Mercury

    Also known as
    Mercurius, Mercury the Martyr
    Dates
    d. c. 250
    Role
    Roman soldier, Christian martyr

    Roman soldier and Christian martyr. Depicted on an image of Our Lady before which Basil prayed. In a vision, he appeared first dim then holding a bloodied spear, portending Julian the Apostate's death.
