    St. Boniface

    Also known as
    Bonifatius
    Dates
    c. 675–754
    Role
    Apostle of the Germans, Archbishop of Mainz, martyr

    Apostle of the Germans, Archbishop of Mainz, and martyr. At his martyrdom held up a codex of the Gospel as a shield; the book was miraculously preserved.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — Carried a codex of the Gospel continually; at his martyrdom held up the codex as a shield; by miracle the book was cut through but no letter destroyed
      "Among the Belgians, St. Boniface together with his companions, spreading the law of Christ, continually carried with him a codex of the holy Gospel"
