St. Edmund of Canterbury

Also known as
Edmundus Cantuariensis, Edmund Rich
Dates
c. 1175–1240
Role
Archbishop of Canterbury

Archbishop of Canterbury who spent his days and nights in sacred Letters; honored the Bible with a kiss before opening it.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Saintly Examples P3 — In 1247, spent days and nights in sacred Letters; honored Bible with a kiss before opening; a candle fell on the book during sleep but the book was found intact and unharmed
    "St. Edmund, Archbishop of Canterbury, in the year of salvation 1247, spent his days and nights in sacred Letters"
