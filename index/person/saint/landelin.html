St. Landelin

Also known as
Landelinus
Dates
c. 625–c. 686
Role
Monk, founder of Lobbes, Aulne, and Crespin

Monk who founded the abbeys of Lobbes, Aulne, and Crespin. Listed among the companions who assisted St. Authbert in the evangelization of Hainault.
