St. Lietbert

Also known as
Lietbertus Cameracensis
Dates
c. 1010–1076
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai who led a pilgrimage of three thousand men to Jerusalem. Lapide praises his equanimity, his contempt for the love of money as "the surest poison," and the posthumous miracle of his grey hairs returning to a youthful colour.
