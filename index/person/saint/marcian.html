    St. Marcian

    Also known as
    Marcianus
    Dates
    d. c. 387
    Role
    Holy monk, cited in Theodoret's Philotheos

    Holy monk cited in Theodoret's Philotheos. When asked by Bishops to speak a word of salvation, reminded them that God speaks daily through creatures and Scripture.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter I: On the Excellence, Necessity, and Fruit of Sacred Scripture — When asked by Bishops to speak a word of salvation, said God speaks daily through creatures and Scripture yet we derive little benefit
      "that saying of St. Marcian in Theodoret's Philotheos"
