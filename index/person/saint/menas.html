    St. Menas

    Also known as
    Menas Aegyptius, the Egyptian Martyr
    Dates
    d. c. 309
    Role
    Egyptian soldier, Christian martyr

    Egyptian soldier and Christian martyr. When subjected to atrocious tortures, declared that nothing can be compared with the kingdom of heaven.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 16: And God Made Two Great Lights — Quoted saying nothing can be compared with the kingdom of heaven
      "So St. Menas, the Egyptian Martyr, when subjected to atrocious tortures, said: "There is nothing that can be compared with the kingdom of heaven;"
