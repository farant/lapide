St. Odo of Cambrai

Also known as
Odo Cameracensis, Odo of Tournai
Dates
c. 1050–1113
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai driven from his see by Emperor Henry IV during the Investiture Controversy for refusing to receive the episcopal staff and ring as an imperial gift. He spent the rest of his life in exile at the abbey of Anchin. Lapide praises his faith and constancy toward God and the Church.
