    St. Authbert of Cambrai

    Also known as
    Authbertus, Autbertus, Autbertus Cameracensis, Authert
    Dates
    d. c. 674
    Role
    Bishop of Cambrai and Arras

    Bishop of Cambrai and Arras who evangelized Hainault. Under his episcopate, Hainault began to flourish in Christian faith. King Dagobert came frequently for his counsel.

    References in Commentary

    • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P42 — Shone with humility and holiness among the people of Cambrai and Hainault; under him Hainault began to flourish in Christian faith; King Dagobert came for his counsel; burned with zeal to convert sinners; adorned relics of the saints
      "Authert shone among the people of Cambrai and Hainault with marvellous humility and holiness."
