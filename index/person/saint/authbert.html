St. Authbert

Also known as
Autbertus Cameracensis
Dates
d. c. 669
Role
Bishop of Cambrai

Bishop of Cambrai who evangelized Hainault with the help of companions including Sts. Landelin, Ghislain, Vincent Madelgarius, and Waldetrudis. King Dagobert sought his counsel. Lapide praises his humility, holiness, and zeal to convert sinners.
