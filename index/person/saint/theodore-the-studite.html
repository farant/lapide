    St. Theodore the Studite

    Also known as
    Theodorus Studita
    Dates
    759–826
    Role
    Monastic reformer, defender of icons

    Monastic reformer and defender of icons. Presided over Studios Monastery after St. Plato and contended against Iconoclast Emperors.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — After St. Plato, presided over Studios Monastery around 800; contended against Iconoclast Emperors Constantine Copronymus and Leo the Isaurian
      "after him Theodore the Studite, around the year of the Lord 800, left so many monuments of his genius"
