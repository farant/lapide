St. Bernard of Clairvaux

Also known as
Bernardus Claraevallensis
Dates
1090–1153
Role
Abbot of Clairvaux, Doctor of the Church

Cistercian abbot, Doctor of the Church, and one of the most influential figures of twelfth-century Christendom. In the Preliminares he is listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate translation.
