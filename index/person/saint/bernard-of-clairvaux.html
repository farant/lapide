St. Bernard of Clairvaux

Also known as
Bernardus Claraevallensis, Bernard
Dates
1090–1153
Role
Abbot of Clairvaux, Doctor of the Church, Cistercian reformer

Abbot of Clairvaux, Doctor of the Church, and Cistercian reformer. Among the scholars who used Jerome's version of Scripture exclusively.

Works

  • Letter to the Brothers of Mont-Dieu
  • Sermons on the Song of Songs
