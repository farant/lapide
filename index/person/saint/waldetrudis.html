    St. Waldetrudis

    Also known as
    Waudru, Waltrudis
    Dates
    c. 612–c. 688
    Role
    Wife of Vincent of Hainault, later foundress of a convent at Mons

    Wife of St. Vincent of Hainault who later founded a convent at Mons. Listed among companions who assisted St. Authbert in evangelizing Hainault.
