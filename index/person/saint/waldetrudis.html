St. Waldetrudis

Dates
d. c. 688
Role
Wife of St. Vincent Madelgarius, foundress of Mons convent

Wife of St. Vincent Madelgarius and foundress of the convent at Mons. Listed among the companions who assisted St. Authbert in the evangelization of Hainault.
