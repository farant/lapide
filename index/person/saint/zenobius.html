St. Zenobius

Also known as
Zenobius
Dates
c. 337–c. 417
Role
Bishop of Florence (traditionally)

Bishop of Florence, known for the purity of soul that granted prophetic insight; his Life testifies that the minds of the Saints gather truth from the smallest indications.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P14 — His Life quoted: the minds of the Saints are strong, and the purity of the soul gathers outcomes from the smallest indications
    "For what is said in the Life of St. Zenobius is most true"
