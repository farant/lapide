St. Anthony of Padua

Also known as
Antonius Patavinus, Anthony of Padua
Dates
1195–1231
Role
Franciscan friar, Doctor of the Church, preacher

Franciscan friar and Doctor of the Church; so versed in Scripture that when he preached before the Pope, he was greeted as the Ark of the Testament.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Saintly Examples P2 — While Francis was still living, taught sacred Letters; so versed in Scripture old and new that when he preached before the Pontiff, he was greeted as the Ark of the Testament
    "the first from his family, St. Anthony of Padua, while St. Francis himself was still living and watching, taught these letters"
