    St. Dominic

    Also known as
    Dominicus, Dominic de Guzmán
    Dates
    c. 1170–1221
    Role
    Founder of the Order of Preachers, Doctor of Sacred Theology

    Founder of the Dominican Order (Order of Preachers), devoted to preaching and the study of Sacred Scripture.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Saintly Examples of Scripture Study — Studied both Old and New Testaments; publicly taught many books at Rome; created first Master of the Sacred Palace; urged Brothers to always carry and study the Bible when preaching
      "St. Dominic, Doctor of Sacred Theology, frequently studied both the Old and New Testaments"
