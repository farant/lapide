St. Ghislain

Also known as
Gislenus
Dates
d. c. 681
Role
Monk, founder of the abbey of Saint-Ghislain

Monk who founded the abbey later named after him (Saint-Ghislain). Listed among the companions who assisted St. Authbert in the evangelization of Hainault.
