    St. Ghislain

    Also known as
    Gislenus
    Dates
    d. c. 681
    Role
    Hermit, founder of a monastery in Hainault

    Hermit and founder of a monastery in Hainault. Listed among companions who assisted St. Authbert in evangelizing the region.

    Preliminaries

    • TO THE MOST REVEREND AND MOST ILLUSTRIOUS LORD HENRY FRANCIS VAN DER BURCH, ARCHBISHOP AND DUKE OF CAMBRAI, PRINCE OF THE HOLY ROMAN EMPIRE, COUNT OF CAMBRAI. — Listed among companions who assisted Authbert in Hainault
      "such as Saint Landelin, Saint Ghislain, Saint Vincent Count of Hainault, and Saint Waldetrudis"
