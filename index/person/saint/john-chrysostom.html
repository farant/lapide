St. John Chrysostom

Also known as
Chrysostom, Ioannes Chrysostomus
Dates
c. 347–407
Role
Archbishop of Constantinople, Doctor of the Church

Archbishop of Constantinople and Doctor of the Church. Known as "the Golden-Mouthed" for the power of his preaching. Wrote extensively on Genesis and Paul's Epistles.

Works

  • Homilies on 1 Timothy
  • Homilies on Genesis
