St. Thomas Aquinas

Also known as
Thomas Aquinas, Doctor Angelicus
Dates
1225–1274
Role
Dominican friar, Doctor of the Church, theologian

Dominican friar, Doctor of the Church, and the preeminent theologian of the Scholastic tradition. In the Preliminares he is listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate translation.
