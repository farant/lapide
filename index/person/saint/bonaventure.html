St. Bonaventure

Also known as
Bonaventura, Doctor Seraphicus
Dates
1221–1274
Role
Franciscan friar, Cardinal-Bishop of Albano, Doctor of the Church

Franciscan friar, Cardinal-Bishop of Albano, and Doctor of the Church, known as the "Seraphic Doctor." In the Preliminares he is listed among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate translation.
