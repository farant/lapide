St. Ambrose of Milan

Also known as
Ambrosius, Ambrosius Mediolanensis
Dates
c. 340–397
Role
Bishop of Milan, Doctor of the Church, Church Father

One of the four great Latin Doctors of the Church. Bishop of Milan who translated Basil's Hexaemeron into Latin.

Works

  • De Hexaemeron
  • Epistles
  • On Cain and Abel
