    Audaeus

    Dates
    4th century
    Role
    Originator of the Anthropomorphite heresy

    Originator of the Anthropomorphite heresy that God is corporeal, reasoning from man being made in God's image.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 26 P10 — Named as the originator of the Anthropomorphite heresy that God is corporeal
      "The Anthropomorphites, whose originator was Audaeus (hence they are called Audaeans), thought that man is the image of God according to the body"
