    Photinus

    Dates
    d. 376
    Role
    Bishop of Sirmium; heresiarch who denied the pre-existence of Christ

    Bishop of Sirmium and heresiarch who denied the pre-existence of Christ. His heresies were condemned by the Council of Sirmium, which was itself an assembly of Arians.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — His heresies were condemned by the Council of Sirmium
      "except insofar as they condemn the heresies of Photinus; for it is established that this Council was an assembly of Arians"
