    Arius

    Dates
    c. 256–336
    Role
    Originator of Arianism

    Alexandrian priest whose teaching that the Son was created by the Father was condemned at the Council of Nicaea (325).

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — God Elohim P3 — Named alongside Simon Magus for the error that God created the Son
