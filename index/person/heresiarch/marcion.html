    Marcion

    Also known as
    Marcion
    Dates
    c. 85–c. 160
    Role
    Heresiarch, founder of Marcionism

    Founder of Marcionism, a heresy that proscribed the Old Testament and taught it was produced by a sinister power. Marcionites held the God of the Old Testament was ignorant and wrathful.
