Mani

Also known as
Curbicus the Persian, Manes, Manichaeus
Dates
c. 216–c. 274
Role
Founder of Manichaeism

Called Curbicus the Persian, whom his own people called Manes and Manichaeus ("as if a pourer-out of manna, by way of honor"). Founded Manichaeism, a heresy that, together with Simon Magus and Marcion, taught the Old Testament was produced by evil angels.
