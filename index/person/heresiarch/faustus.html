    Faustus the Manichaean

    Also known as
    Faustus
    Dates
    fl. late 4th century
    Role
    Manichaean bishop, opponent of Augustine

    Manichaean bishop and opponent of St. Augustine. Augustine defends Moses against his slanders: "far be it from us to judge such and so great a man by the slanderous mouth of Faustus."
