    Fr. H. D. Lacordaire, O.P.

    Also known as
    Henri-Dominique Lacordaire, Jean-Baptiste-Henri Lacordaire
    Dates
    1802–1861
    Role
    French Dominican preacher, restorer of the Dominican Order in France; author of the du-culte section

    French Dominican preacher and restorer of the Dominican Order in France. Author of the du-culte section, from his work "Letters to a Young Man on the Christian Life" (Paris, 1858).
