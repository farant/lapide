Eusebius of Caesarea (Bishop)

Also known as
Eusebius, Eusebius, Bishop of Caesarea
Dates
d. before Basil became bishop (fl. 360s)
Role
Bishop of Caesarea (the bishop Basil supported, not the Church historian)

Bishop of Caesarea whom Basil faithfully stood by through his illness until death. Not to be confused with the Church historian Eusebius of Caesarea (c. 260–340).

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P16 — Basil faithfully stood by Eusebius through his illness until death; once Eusebius died, Basil hid himself
    "after he had faithfully stood by his friend Eusebius, Bishop of Caesarea, through his illness until death, once Eusebius had died Basil immediately hid himself away"
