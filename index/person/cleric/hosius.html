    Stanislaus Hosius

    Also known as
    Cardinal Hosius
    Dates
    1504–1579
    Role
    Cardinal, president of Council of Trent, opponent of Luther

    Cardinal and president of the Council of Trent. Known as a distinguished opponent of Luther. Foresaw the apostasy of Andreas Dudecius from his pride alone.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P15 — Man of utmost integrity; foresaw that Andreas Dudecius would apostatize from pride; said the danger of apostasy was threatening him; when asked whence he foresaw it, replied: "From the man's pride alone"
      "So Cardinal Hosius, president of the Council of Trent"
