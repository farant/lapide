Charles Scribani

Also known as
Carolus Scribani
Dates
1561–1629
Role
Jesuit Provincial Superior of the Province of Flandro-Belgica

Jesuit Provincial Superior of the Province of Flandro-Belgica who granted printing permission for Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch, by authority of Father General Vitelleschi. Signed at Antwerp, 23 August 1616.
