Clergy

  • Augustinus Crampon (1826–1894) — Priest of the Diocese of Amiens; editor and annotator of Lapide's commentaries for the 1891 Vives edition
  • Charles Scribani (1561–1629) — Jesuit Provincial Superior of the Province of Flandro-Belgica
  • Cornelius a Lapide (1567–1637) — Jesuit priest, Scripture professor at Louvain and Rome, biblical commentator
  • Egbert Spitholdius (early 17th century) — Licenciate in Sacred Theology, Canon and Parish Priest of Antwerp, Censor of Books
  • Henry Francis Van der Burch (c. 1567–1644) — Archbishop and Duke of Cambrai, Prince of the Holy Roman Empire, Count of Cambrai; previously Bishop of Ghent and Dean of the Metropolitan Church of Mechlin
  • Jacobus Antonius (19th century) — Bishop of Amiens (1849–1873)
  • Mutius Vitelleschi (1563–1645) — Superior General of the Society of Jesus
