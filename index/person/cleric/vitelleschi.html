Mutius Vitelleschi

Also known as
Mutius Vitelleschi
Dates
1563–1645
Role
Superior General of the Society of Jesus

Superior General of the Society of Jesus who granted permission for Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch to be printed, after review by three Jesuit theologians. Signed at Rome, 9 January 1616.
