Augustinus Crampon

Also known as
Aug. Crampon
Dates
1826–1894
Role
Priest of the Diocese of Amiens; editor and annotator of Lapide's commentaries for the 1891 Vives edition

Priest of the Diocese of Amiens who edited and annotated Cornelius a Lapide's commentaries for the 1891 Vives edition in Paris. His annotations were approved by the Bishop of Amiens on 2 May 1852.
