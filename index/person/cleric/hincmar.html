Hincmar of Reims

Also known as
Hincmarus Remensis, Hincmar, Archbishop of Reims
Dates
c. 806–882
Role
Archbishop of Reims

Archbishop of Reims. Extolled the virtues of St. Theodoric and St. John, both bishops of Cambrai. Not canonized.
