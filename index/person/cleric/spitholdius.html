Egbert Spitholdius

Also known as
Egbertus Spitholdius
Dates
early 17th century
Role
Licenciate in Sacred Theology, Canon and Parish Priest of Antwerp, Censor of Books

Canon and Parish Priest of Antwerp who served as Censor of Books. Assessed Lapide's commentary as "learned and pious, and in every respect worthy of publication, so that it may instruct all who are eager for learning and advance them in piety." Signed 9 May 1615.
