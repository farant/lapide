    Egbert Spitholdius

    Also known as
    Egbertus Spitholdius
    Dates
    fl. early 17th century
    Role
    Licenciate in Sacred Theology, Canon and Parish Priest of Antwerp, Censor of Books

    Canon and Parish Priest of Antwerp who served as Censor of Books. Certified Lapide's commentary as learned, pious, and worthy of publication.

    Preliminaries

    • Permissions P3 — Gives censor's assessment certifying Lapide's commentary as learned, pious, and worthy of publication; dated 9 May 1615
