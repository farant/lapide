    Eusebius (Jerome's companion)

    Also known as
    Brother Eusebius
    Role
    Companion of Jerome; friend of Paulinus

    Companion of Jerome and friend of Paulinus. Reported Paulinus's character, contempt for the world, and love of Christ.

    References in Commentary

    • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P48 — Jerome mentions "your most loving brother Eusebius" who reported Paulinus's character, contempt for the world, and love of Christ
      "You have here your most loving brother Eusebius, who doubled for me the favor of your letter by reporting the uprightness of your character"
