Jacobus Antonius

Also known as
Jean-Pierre-Antoine-Marie-Joseph Boudinet
Dates
19th century
Role
Bishop of Amiens (1849–1873)

Bishop of Amiens who approved Crampon's annotations on Lapide's commentaries. Signed under his Latin baptismal name on 2 May 1852. Likely Jean-Pierre-Antoine-Marie-Joseph Boudinet, Bishop of Amiens from 1849 to 1873.
