    Philastrius

    Dates
    d. c. 397
    Role
    Bishop of Brescia

    Bishop of Brescia known for his catalogue of heresies. Held the erroneous view that stars move freely in the sky like birds.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 14 P3 — Cited for the erroneous view that stars move in the sky like birds in air
      "Likewise Philastrius errs when he says: It is a heresy to assert that the stars are fixed in the sky"
