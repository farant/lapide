    Abraham ibn Ezra

    Also known as
    Aben Ezra
    Dates
    c. 1089–1167
    Role
    Jewish biblical commentator

    Medieval Spanish-Jewish commentator, poet, and polymath. One of the most distinguished biblical exegetes of the Middle Ages.
