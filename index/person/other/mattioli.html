    Pietro Andrea Mattioli

    Also known as
    Mattioli
    Dates
    1501–1577
    Role
    Italian physician, naturalist

    Italian physician and naturalist, best known for his commentary on Dioscorides' De Materia Medica.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • On the Work of the Sixth Day — Cited in Dioscorides commentary for personally testing that salamanders are consumed by fire
      "Mattioli says he himself experienced this, having thrown many salamanders into fire, which were quickly consumed."
