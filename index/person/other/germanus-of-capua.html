    Germanus of Capua

    Also known as
    Germanus, Bishop of Capua
    Dates
    d. c. 540
    Role
    Bishop of Capua

    Bishop of Capua whose soul St. Benedict saw being carried to heaven by angels in a fiery sphere, as reported by Pope Gregory the Great.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter V: On the Dispositions Required for This Study — His soul seen by Benedict being carried to heaven by angels in a fiery sphere
