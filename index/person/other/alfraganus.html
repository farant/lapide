    Al-Farghani

    Also known as
    Alfraganus
    Dates
    c. 805–870
    Role
    Arab astronomer

    Arab astronomer whose Elements of Astronomy was influential in both the Islamic world and medieval Europe.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 16: And God Made Two Great Lights — Cited alongside Ptolemy for dividing stars into six classes of magnitude
