    Symmachus (translator)

    Also known as
    Symmachus
    Dates
    fl. late 2nd century
    Role
    Bible translator; Ebionite then Jew

    Fourth translator of the Hebrew Scriptures into Greek, under Emperor Severus. First an Ebionite, then a Jew.

    References in Commentary

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 2 P3 — Cited for translating: "the earth was idle and unformed"

    • Genesis 1 — Verse 6 P15 — Named among translators lacking "God saw that it was good" on the second day
