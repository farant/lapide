Symmachus (translator)

Also known as
Symmachus
Dates
fl. late 2nd century
Role
Bible translator; Ebionite then Jew

Fourth translator of the Hebrew Scriptures into Greek, under Emperor Severus. First an Ebionite, then a Jew.
