    Severus (correspondent of Paulinus)

    Also known as
    Severus
    Dates
    fl. 5th century
    Role
    Correspondent of Paulinus of Nola

    Correspondent of St. Paulinus of Nola. Paulinus wrote to him (letter 42) about the church at Nola and the verses inscribed therein.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Section One P8 — Paulinus attests in letter 42 to Severus about church at Nola
