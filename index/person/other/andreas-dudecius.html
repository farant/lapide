Andreas Dudecius

Also known as
Andreas Dudecius, Bishop of Tinnin
Dates
fl. 16th century
Role
Bishop of Tinnin, legate of Hungarian clergy at Council of Trent; later apostate

Bishop of Tinnin who served as legate of the Hungarian clergy at the Council of Trent. Admired for his eloquence, but Cardinal Hosius suspected him of pride and predicted his apostasy; he subsequently fled to Calvin's camp.
