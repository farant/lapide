Eleazar (High Priest)

Also known as
Eleazar the high priest
Dates
3rd century BC
Role
High Priest of the Jews during the Septuagint translation

High Priest of the Jews through whom Ptolemy Philadelphus chose six of the most learned men from each tribe -- seventy-two interpreters -- to translate the Hebrew Scriptures into Greek.
