    Onkelos

    Dates
    c. 1st–2nd century AD
    Role
    Author of the Aramaic Targum on the Pentateuch

    Jewish proselyte traditionally credited with authoring the authoritative Aramaic translation (Targum) of the Pentateuch.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 2: And the earth was without form and void — Cited for translating: "the earth was desolate and empty"
      "Onkelos, the earth was desolate and empty."
