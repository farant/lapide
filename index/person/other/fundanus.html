Fundanus

Also known as
Fundanus
Dates
fl. 4th century
Role
Formerly Bishop of Alutina

Formerly Bishop of Alutina who, out of fear of death, surrendered the sacred books during the persecution. When the magistrate consigned them to fire, a shower from a clear sky extinguished the flames, followed by hail.
