    St. Monica

    Dates
    c. 332–387
    Role
    Mother of Augustine

    Mother of St. Augustine of Hippo. Her conversation with Augustine about eternity is recorded in his Confessions.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 14: Let There Be Lights in the Firmament — Referenced as Augustine's conversation partner in Confessions Book IX
      "See St. Augustine's conversation on this matter with his mother Monica, book IX Confessions, ch. 10."
