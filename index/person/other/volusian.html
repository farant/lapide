    Volusian

    Also known as
    Rufius Antonius Agrypnius Volusianus
    Dates
    fl. early 5th century
    Role
    Roman senator, correspondent of Augustine

    Roman senator to whom St. Augustine wrote about the corrective, nourishing, and delightful power of Scripture, and about the profundity of Christian letters.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Chapter II: On the Object and Breadth of Sacred Scripture — Augustine writes to him about the corrective, nourishing, and delightful power of Scripture
      "St. Augustine, writing to Volusian"
    • Chapter IV: The Judgments and Examples of the Fathers — Augustine writes to him (Epistle 1) about the profundity of Christian letters
      "writing to Volusian, Epistle 1"
