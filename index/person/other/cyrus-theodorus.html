Cyrus Theodorus

Dates
4th century
Role
Poet

Poet who composed verses mocking the prefect Modestus after his humiliation by Basil the Great and his later dependence on Basil when ill.

References in Commentary

  • 01_Preliminares.html#dedicatory-letter ~p25 — "Cyrus Theodorus mock this Prefect... with these verses: 'You are Prefect over all men else, Modestus, / but under Basil the Great you take your place. / However much you crave to be in command, you submit; / an ant you are, though you may roar like a lion.'"
