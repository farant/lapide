    Theodore (physician)

    Also known as
    Theodore
    Dates
    fl. 6th century
    Role
    Physician rebuked by Gregory the Great for neglecting Scripture

    Physician rebuked by Pope Gregory the Great (Book IV, letter 84) for negligently reading Sacred Scripture. Gregory called Scripture a letter from Almighty God.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Section One — Rebuked by Gregory for negligently reading Sacred Scripture
      "rebukes Theodore, even though he was a physician, for negligently reading Sacred Scripture"
