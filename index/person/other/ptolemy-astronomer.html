    Also known as
    Ptolemy
    Dates
    c. 100–170 AD
    Role
    Greco-Egyptian astronomer and geographer

    Greco-Egyptian astronomer and geographer. Author of an astronomical canon used for chronological calculations in the sacred chronology.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Sacred Chronology — The canon of Ptolemy used for chronological calculations

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Verse 16: And God Made Two Great Lights — Cited alongside Alfraganus for dividing stars into six classes of magnitude
