    Archduke Albert of Austria

    Also known as
    His Most Serene Highness the Archduke, Most Serene Archduke
    Dates
    1559–1621
    Role
    Archduke of Austria, co-sovereign of the Spanish Netherlands (with Archduchess Isabella)

    Archduke of Austria and co-sovereign of the Spanish Netherlands. Called Van der Burch to the bishopric of Ghent and later nominated him as Archbishop of Cambrai.

    References in Commentary

    • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P15 — Called Van der Burch to the bishopric of Ghent; described as applying keen judgment in selecting prelates, conceding nothing to favor or blood, only to virtue
      "you were called to the bishopric of Ghent by our Most Serene Archduke, who in selecting prelates applies a keen and singular judgment"
    • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P18 — Nominated Van der Burch as Archbishop of Cambrai, transferring him from Ghent
      "His Most Serene Highness the Archduke was considering transferring you from the bishopric of Ghent and had nominated you as Archbishop of Cambrai"
