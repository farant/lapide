    Theodectes

    Also known as
    Theodectes
    Dates
    c. 375–c. 334 BC
    Role
    Greek tragic poet

    Greek tragic poet who was struck blind when he wished to transfer material from Jewish Scripture into a theatrical piece. He repented and recovered his sight.
