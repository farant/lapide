    Evodius

    Dates
    d. c. 424
    Role
    Bishop of Uzalis; correspondent of Augustine

    Bishop of Uzalis and correspondent of Augustine. Augustine's Epistle 99, on one thing being a figure of two contrary things, was addressed to Evodius.
