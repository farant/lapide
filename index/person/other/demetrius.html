Demetrius (Alexandrian librarian)

Also known as
Demetrius the prefect
Dates
fl. 3rd century BC
Role
Prefect of the Alexandrian library

Prefect of the Alexandrian library who arranged for the Septuagint to be deposited there, and who answered Ptolemy's question about why no historian had mentioned the Law of Moses.
