    Emmelia

    Dates
    d. c. 375
    Role
    Mother of Basil the Great

    Mother of Basil the Great. Illustrious for piety; followed her son into the wilderness.

    Preliminaries

    • TO THE MOST REVEREND AND MOST ILLUSTRIOUS LORD HENRY FRANCIS VAN DER BURCH, ARCHBISHOP AND DUKE OF CAMBRAI, PRINCE OF THE HOLY ROMAN EMPIRE, COUNT OF CAMBRAI. — Named as Basil's mother, illustrious for piety no less than birth; followed her son into the wilderness
      "Saint Basil too was descended from parents illustrious for piety no less than for birth — Basil and Emmelia — and his mother followed her son even when he withdrew into the wilderness."
