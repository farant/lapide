    Emmanuel

    Role
    Addressee of Lacordaire's letter

    Addressee of Lacordaire's letter on the worship of Christ in Sacred Scripture. The young man to whom the "Letters to a Young Man on the Christian Life" are addressed.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • ON THE WORSHIP OF JESUS CHRIST IN THE SCRIPTURES. — Lacordaire addresses him as "my dear Emmanuel"
      "So it is, my dear Emmanuel."
    • ON THE WORSHIP OF JESUS CHRIST IN THE SCRIPTURES. — Addressed directly
      "Whence comes, you will say to me, this power of the psalms and the prophecies?"
    • ON THE WORSHIP OF JESUS CHRIST IN THE SCRIPTURES. — Addressed at the close
      "Once Saint Paul has been studied and savored, my dear Emmanuel, the Scriptures are yours."
