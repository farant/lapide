Reginald of Piperno

Also known as
Reginald
Dates
d. c. 1290
Role
Dominican friar, socius of Thomas Aquinas

Dominican friar and close companion (socius) of St. Thomas Aquinas. Thomas at the point of death spoke to Reginald about the three things he had obtained from God: purity of mind, freedom from prelacies, and knowledge of his brother's state in glory.
