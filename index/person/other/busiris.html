    Busiris

    Dates
    mythological
    Role
    Mythological Egyptian king who slaughtered guests

    Mythological Egyptian king who used to slaughter and sacrifice his guests. Cited in Virgil's Georgics as "unsung Busiris" as an example of litotes.
