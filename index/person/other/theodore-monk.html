    Theodore (holy monk)

    Also known as
    Theodore
    Dates
    fl. 4th–5th century
    Role
    Holy monk, unlettered but skilled in divine Scripture

    Holy monk recorded by Cassian who was so unlettered that he did not even know the alphabet, yet so skilled in divine volumes that the most learned consulted him. He taught that effort must go to uprooting vices rather than perusing books.
