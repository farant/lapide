    Peter the Deacon

    Also known as
    Peter the Deacon
    Dates
    fl. 6th century
    Role
    Deacon, eyewitness to Gregory the Great's writing

    Deacon who attested as an eyewitness that the Holy Spirit sat upon Pope Gregory the Great in the form of a dove as he wrote.

    References in Commentary

    • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Chapter V P18 — Attests as eyewitness that the Holy Spirit sat upon Gregory in the form of a dove as he wrote
