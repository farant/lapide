Remigius of Auxerre

Also known as
Remigius Autissiodorensis
Dates
c. 841–908
Role
Benedictine monk, biblical commentator, teacher

Benedictine monk and biblical commentator. Listed first among the "most learned men" who used Jerome's Vulgate version over the preceding nine hundred years. Venerated locally but not formally canonized.
