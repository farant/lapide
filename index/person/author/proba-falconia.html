Proba Falconia

Also known as
the Latin Sappho
Dates
c. 322–c. 370
Role
Roman Christian poet, composed biblical centos from Virgil

Roman Christian poet who composed centos adapting Virgil's Aeneid to narrate the life of Christ. Called "the Latin Sappho."

Works

  • Cento Vergilianus
