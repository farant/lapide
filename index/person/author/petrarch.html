    Petrarch

    Also known as
    Francis Petrarch, Franciscus Petrarca
    Dates
    1304–1374
    Role
    Italian poet, scholar, father of Renaissance humanism

    Italian poet and scholar, often called the father of Renaissance humanism. Relates that King Robert of Sicily swore that letters were dearer to him than his kingdom.
