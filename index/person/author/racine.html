Racine

Also known as
Jean Racine
Dates
1639–1699
Role
French dramatist

French dramatist. Lacordaire lists "the laments of Racine's Phaedra" among the greatest achievements of secular poetry, but sets them below the Miserere, Lamentations, and Isaiah.
