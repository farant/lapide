Henry VIII

Also known as
Henricus VIII
Dates
1491–1547
Role
King of England

King of England. Tried to promote John Fisher, Bishop of Rochester, to a wealthier see, but Fisher refused to abandon his modest flock. The episode illustrates episcopal humility in contrast to royal power.
