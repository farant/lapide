Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor

Also known as
Emperor Henry IV
Dates
1050–1106
Role
Holy Roman Emperor

Holy Roman Emperor who drove St. Odo from his see at Cambrai because Odo refused to receive staff and ring from the Emperor.
