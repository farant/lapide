King Dagobert

Also known as
Dagobertus Rex Francorum
Dates
c. 603–639
Role
King of the Franks

King of the Franks who sought the counsel of St. Authbert, Bishop of Cambrai, coming to him frequently. Mentioned in the context of Authbert's influence over the Christianization of Hainault.
