    Diocletian

    Also known as
    Diocletian
    Dates
    c. 244–311
    Role
    Roman Emperor

    Roman Emperor who, together with Maximian, ordered by edict that the books of Moses and Scripture be handed over for burning. The faithful preferred martyrdom rather than surrender the sacred books.
