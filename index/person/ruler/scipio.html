    P. Cornelius Scipio

    Also known as
    Scipio
    Role
    Roman nobleman; grandfather of Scipio Africanus

    Roman nobleman, grandfather of Scipio Africanus. Got the cognomen Scipio because he guided and led his father in place of a staff (scipio), and transmitted the name to his posterity.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Got the cognomen Scipio because he guided his father in place of a staff
      "Scipio was the cognomen of the Cornelii, which P. Cornelius (grandfather of P. Cornelius Scipio Africanus, who defeated Hannibal) originated"
