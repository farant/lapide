    Darius

    Also known as
    Darius, father of Xerxes
    Dates
    550–486 BC
    Role
    King of Persia

    Persian king. Mentioned both as the Darius defeated by Alexander the Great and as the sixth ancient lawgiver listed by Diodorus who added to the Egyptian laws.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • II. The Richness of the Old Testament — Alexander defeated Darius
      "Alexander, who after defeating Darius"
    • Moses as the Most Ancient Theologian, Philosopher, Poet, and Historian — Diodorus lists Darius (father of Xerxes) as sixth lawgiver who added to Egyptian laws
      "The sixth is said to be Darius, the father of Xerxes"
