    Charlemagne

    Also known as
    Carolus Magnus, King and Emperor Charlemagne
    Dates
    c. 742–814
    Role
    King of the Franks, Holy Roman Emperor

    King and Emperor called "thrice-greatest -- in learning, piety, and military glory." Established schools of Sacred Letters at Paris and elsewhere, and near death corrected the four Gospels against Greek and Syriac manuscripts.
