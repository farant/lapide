    Empress Eudocia

    Also known as
    Eudocia, Aelia Eudocia
    Dates
    c. 401–460
    Role
    Byzantine Empress, poet

    Byzantine Empress and poet who composed centos adapting Homer's Iliad to narrate the life of Christ, paralleling Proba Falconia's use of Virgil.
