Archduke Albert of Austria

Also known as
Archduke Albert, Most Serene Archduke
Dates
1559–1621
Role
Co-sovereign of the Spanish Netherlands (with Archduchess Isabella)

Co-sovereign of the Spanish Netherlands who appointed Van der Burch to the bishopric of Ghent and later nominated him as Archbishop of Cambrai. Praised for applying "a keen and singular judgment" in selecting prelates, "conceding nothing to favor or to blood, but everything to virtue." Not named explicitly in the text, referred to as "our Most Serene Archduke."
