    Ninus

    Dates
    legendary
    Role
    Legendary founder of the Assyrian monarchy

    Legendary founder of the first Assyrian monarchy, together with Semiramis. Pagans reckon their chronology from his time, which coincided with the age of Abraham.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Sacred Chronology — Pagans reckon their times from Ninus and Semiramis, who founded the first Assyrian monarchy
      "the pagans reckon their times first from Ninus and Semiramis, who founded the first monarchy of the Assyrians"
