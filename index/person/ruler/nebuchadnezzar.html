    Nebuchadnezzar

    Dates
    c. 634–562 BC
    Role
    King of Babylon; destroyed Jerusalem

    King of Babylon who destroyed Jerusalem. Mentioned in the sacred chronology in connection with the Babylonian captivity.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Sacred Chronology — Babylonian captivity and destruction of Jerusalem by Nebuchadnezzar: year 3416

    • Sacred Chronology — Jerusalem was stormed by Nebuchadnezzar after Zedekiah
