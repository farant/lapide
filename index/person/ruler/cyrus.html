Cyrus the Great

Also known as
Cyrus
Dates
c. 600–530 BC
Role
King of Persia, conqueror of Babylon

King of Persia and conqueror of Babylon. Speaks to Babylon of the God who commanded him to rebuild the temple of Jerusalem.
