Duke of Alba

Also known as
Fernando Alvarez de Toledo, 3rd Duke of Alba
Dates
1507–1582
Role
Spanish military commander and governor of the Netherlands

Spanish military commander who rescued Mechlin in 1572. When heretics seized the city and Van der Burch's father was cast into prison and destined for death, the Duke of Alba arrived suddenly with his army and saved him.
