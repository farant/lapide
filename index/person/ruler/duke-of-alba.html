Duke of Alba

Also known as
Fernando Álvarez de Toledo, the Duke of Alba
Dates
1507–1582
Role
Spanish general and governor of the Netherlands

Spanish general and governor of the Netherlands. Suddenly arrived with his army in 1572 to rescue Van der Burch's father from heretics at Mechlin.
