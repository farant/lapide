Philip II of Spain

Also known as
Philippus II
Dates
1527–1598
Role
King of Spain and the Spanish Netherlands

King of Spain. Van der Burch's father was "most dear to the Catholic King Philip II, of glorious memory" on account of his steadfast loyalty amid the upheavals of the Low Countries.
