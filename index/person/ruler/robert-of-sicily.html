Robert of Sicily

Also known as
Robert, King of Sicily
Dates
1277–1343
Role
King of Naples and Sicily

King of Naples and Sicily who told Petrarch under oath that letters were dearer to him than his kingdom, and he would rather part with the crown than with letters.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Utility Iv P7 — Said to Petrarch under oath that letters were dearer than his kingdom
    "Robert, King of Sicily, was so delighted with letters, especially sacred ones"
