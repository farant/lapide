Emperor Henry IV

Also known as
Henricus IV Imperator
Dates
1050–1106
Role
Holy Roman Emperor

Holy Roman Emperor who drove Odo, Bishop of Cambrai, from his see for refusing to receive the staff and ring as an imperial gift. Odo spent the rest of his life in exile at Anchin.
