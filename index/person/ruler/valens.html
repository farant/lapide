Emperor Valens

Also known as
Valens Imperator
Dates
328–378
Role
Roman Emperor (364–378); Arian sympathizer

Roman Emperor and Arian sympathizer whose prefect Modestus confronted Basil the Great with threats of confiscation, exile, torture, and death. Basil's famous defiance -- dismissing each threat in turn -- broke the prefect, who reported to Valens: "We have been vanquished by the Bishop of this Church."
