    Alfonso V of Aragon

    Also known as
    Alfonso, King of Aragon
    Dates
    1396–1458
    Role
    King of Aragon and Naples

    King of Aragon and Naples who boasted that amid the business of his kingdom he had read the entire Bible with glosses and commentaries fourteen times.
