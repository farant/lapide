Belshazzar

Dates
d. 539 BC
Role
King of Babylon

King of Babylon. Isaiah describes the fall of the king of Babylon through the fall of Lucifer; some things in Isaiah 14 properly suit Belshazzar, such as "Your corpse has fallen."
