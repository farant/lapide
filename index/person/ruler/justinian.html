    Emperor Justinian

    Also known as
    Justinian, Emperor Justinian
    Dates
    c. 482–565
    Role
    Byzantine Emperor

    Byzantine Emperor in whose time schools of Sacred Scripture existed at Nisibis, as attested by Junilius Africanus.
