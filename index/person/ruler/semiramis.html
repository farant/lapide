    Semiramis

    Dates
    legendary
    Role
    Legendary co-founder of the Assyrian monarchy

    Legendary co-founder of the first Assyrian monarchy, together with Ninus. Pagans reckon their chronology from their time, which coincided with the age of Abraham.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Sacred Chronology — Together with Ninus, founded the first monarchy of the Assyrians
      "from Ninus and Semiramis, who founded the first monarchy of the Assyrians"
