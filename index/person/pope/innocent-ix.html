Pope Innocent IX

Also known as
Giovanni Antonio Facchinetti
Dates
1519–1591
Role
Pope (1591); two-month pontificate

Pope whose brief two-month pontificate, following Gregory XIV, was another transitional interval before Clement VIII finally brought the Vulgate correction to completion.
