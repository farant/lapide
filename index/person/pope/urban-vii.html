Pope Urban VII

Also known as
Giovanni Battista Castagna
Dates
1521–1590
Role
Pope (September 1590); shortest pontificate in history at twelve days

Pope whose twelve-day pontificate -- the shortest in papal history -- served as a transitional moment between Sixtus V and Gregory XIV during the Vulgate correction project.
