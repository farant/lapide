Pope Sixtus V

Also known as
Felice Peretti
Dates
1521–1590
Role
Pope (1585–1590); ordered the Sistine Vulgate printed but recalled it before completion

Pope who revived the Vulgate correction project with great zeal and ordered the completed work printed. Upon discovering that errors had crept in during printing, he decreed the entire edition be recalled, but died before accomplishing this.
