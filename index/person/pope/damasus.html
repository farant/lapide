    Pope Damasus I

    Also known as
    Blessed Damasus, Damasus
    Dates
    c. 305–384
    Role
    Pope (366–384)

    Pope who commissioned St. Jerome to translate the Old Testament from Hebrew into Latin, producing what became known as the Vulgate.
