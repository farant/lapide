Popes

  • Pope Clement VIII (1536–1605) — Pope (1592–1605); issued the Clementine Vulgate
  • Pope Gregory XIV (1535–1591) — Pope (1590–1591); undertook to complete the Vulgate correction
  • Pope Innocent IX (1519–1591) — Pope (1591); two-month pontificate
  • Pope Pius IV (1499–1565) — Pope (1559–1565); presided over the conclusion of the Council of Trent
  • Pope Pius V (1504–1572) — Pope (1566–1572); canonized 1712. Carried forward the Vulgate correction
  • Pope Sixtus V (1521–1590) — Pope (1585–1590); ordered the Sistine Vulgate printed but recalled it before completion
  • Pope Urban VII (1521–1590) — Pope (September 1590); shortest pontificate in history at twelve days
