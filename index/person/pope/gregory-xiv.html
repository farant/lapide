Pope Gregory XIV

Also known as
Nicolo Sfondrati
Dates
1535–1591
Role
Pope (1590–1591); undertook to complete the Vulgate correction

Pope who succeeded after Urban VII's twelve-day pontificate and undertook to complete the Vulgate correction that Sixtus V had begun. Died before the project was finished.
