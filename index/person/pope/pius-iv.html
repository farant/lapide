Pope Pius IV

Also known as
Giovanni Angelo Medici
Dates
1499–1565
Role
Pope (1559–1565); presided over the conclusion of the Council of Trent

Pope who presided over the conclusion of the Council of Trent. Entrusted the correction of the Latin Vulgate to select Cardinals and other men skilled in sacred letters and languages.
