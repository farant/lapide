### 1572
- **Slug**: `ad/16th-century/70s/1572`
- **Description**: Heretics seized Mechlin by surprise; Van der Burch's father was imprisoned, subjected to hardships, and suffered severe financial losses. Only the Duke of Alba's sudden arrival with his army prevented his death.
- **Occurrences**:
  - `#dedicatory-letter-p12` — The first sack of Mechlin by heretics during the Dutch Revolt
