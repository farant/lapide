# Years — 14_Commentaria_In_Pentateuchum_Mosis_Canones

This file contains all years/dates mentioned in the text. The Sacred Chronology section uses "years from the beginning of the world" (Anno Mundi) and "years before Christ" (BC). Both systems are recorded here. The Anno Mundi dates are internal to Lapide's chronology and do not have independent slug entries; only the BC dates and AD dates are given slugs.
