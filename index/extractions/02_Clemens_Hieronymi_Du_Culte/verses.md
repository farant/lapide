## Proverbs

### Proverbs 31:10
- **Slug**: `proverbs/31/10`
- **Description**: "Who shall find a valiant woman?" — the acrostic poem at the end of Proverbs, cited by Jerome as one of the alphabetic compositions in Hebrew Scripture.
- **Occurrences**:
  - `#helmeted-prologue-p2` — Jerome cites the alphabetic acrostic at the end of Proverbs as evidence for the twenty-two Hebrew letters
    text: "the Proverbs of Solomon at the end, from the place where he says, 'Who shall find a valiant woman?' are reckoned by the same alphabets or divisions."
- **Related people**: Solomon, Jerome
