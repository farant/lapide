Canaan

Description
The Promised Land; used as a figure for heavenly glory
Coordinates
31.5, 34.75

The Promised Land of the Old Testament. Used typologically in the dedicatory letter: Van der Burch will lead his people "not into the land of the Canaanites promised to the Jews, but into the land of the living and of those who triumph in heaven."
