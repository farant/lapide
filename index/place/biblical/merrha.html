Description
Biblical commercial city; its merchants listed alongside those of Teman in Baruch's passage as seekers of earthly prudence who have not known the way of wisdom

Biblical commercial city. Its merchants are listed alongside those of Teman in Baruch's passage as seekers of earthly prudence who have not known the way of wisdom.
