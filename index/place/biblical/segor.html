    Segor

    Also known as
    Bala, Zoar

    Small city near the Dead Sea, originally called Bala, to which Lot fled from the destruction of Sodom. Lapide uses it as an example of prolepsis (anticipation) in Scripture: Moses calls the city by the name Segor, which it received only later.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Moses calls Bala by the later name Segor (prolepsis)
      "he calls the city Bala by the name Segor, which however was not then but only later called Segor"
