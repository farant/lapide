    Paradise

    Also known as
    Garden of Eden
    Description
    The garden where God placed Adam and Eve; Lapide affirms it is to be understood historically

    The garden where God placed Adam and Eve after creation. Lapide affirms in Canon 1 that Moses' narrations about paradise, Adam, Eve, and the creation are to be taken historically and properly, against Origen's purely allegorical reading.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Things Moses narrates about paradise are historical, not allegorical
      "the things he narrates about paradise, Adam, Eve, and the creation of all things completed successively over the space of six days, etc., are to be taken historically and properly, as they sound."
