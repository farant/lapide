Midian

Also known as
Ethiopia (in some references)
Description
Region east of the Gulf of Aqaba; Moses fled there from Egypt and spent 40 years pasturing sheep and contemplating divine wisdom

Region east of the Gulf of Aqaba. Moses fled there from Egypt and spent 40 years pasturing sheep, speaking with God in the burning bush, and drawing divine wisdom by contemplation.

References in Commentary

  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses Three Periods P2 — Moses fled to Midian, pastured sheep, spoke with God in the burning bush, and drew divine wisdom by contemplation for 40 years
    "he fled to Midian; here, pasturing sheep, having spoken with God in the burning bush, he drew all divine wisdom by contemplation for a full 40 years"
  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — Moses And Christ P7 — St. Basil says Moses in Ethiopia (Midian) devoted 40 years to contemplation
    "in Ethiopia (in Midian) he devoted forty years to contemplation"
  • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Argumentum P5 — War of the Hebrews with the Midianites
    "the war of the Hebrews with the Midianites"
  • Commentaria In Pentateuchum Mosis Canones — Argumentum P9 — Moses wrote Genesis while living as an exile in Midian
    "Moses wrote Genesis while he was living as an exile in Midian"
