    One of the rivers of paradise mentioned in Genesis; Jerome identifies it with a very wide river that Apollonius crossed on his way to the Brahmans.

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Apollonius crossed the very wide river Phison to reach the Brahmans
      "having crossed the very wide river Phison, he reached the Brahmans"
