    Pontus Euxinus

    Also known as
    Black Sea, the Pontus
    Description
    Large body of water north of Asia Minor; fish prefer its sweeter waters for nurturing offspring
    Coordinates
    43, 35

    Large body of water north of Asia Minor. Fish prefer the Pontus to other sea-bays as more suitable for producing and nurturing offspring, because its water is sweeter, the sun lingering only briefly there.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)
