Egypt

Description
Land of Pharaoh and the Exodus; also mentioned as a center of early monasticism
Coordinates
26.8206, 30.8025

Land of Pharaoh and the Exodus, from which Moses led the Hebrews through the desert to the Promised Land. Also mentioned as a center of early Christian monasticism alongside Palestine and Mesopotamia.
