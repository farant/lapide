Antioch in Armenia

Description
Seat of an archbishopric where St. Macarius served as Archbishop
Coordinates
36.2, 36.15

City with an archbishopric in Armenia. St. Macarius served as Archbishop of Antioch in Armenia; his relics were later sent by Van der Burch to Mons to relieve the plague.
