    Region south of Judea inhabited by the descendants of Esau (Edomites). Lapide references the promise to Jacob of dominion over Esau, that is, the Edomites.

    Commentary on the Pentateuch of Moses

    • Canons Bearing a Torch Before the Pentateuch — Dominion over the Edomites promised to Jacob
      "to Jacob, that is, to the Jacobites, dominion over Esau, that is, the Edomites, is promised"
