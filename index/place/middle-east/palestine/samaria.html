    Capital of the northern kingdom of Israel; mentioned by Jerome in connection with the prophet Hosea (Ephraim, Samaria, Joseph, Jezreel) and Amos (the mountain of Samaria).

    Pope Clement VIII, Jerome's Prefaces, On Worship

    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Hosea frequently names Samaria alongside Ephraim, Joseph, and Jezreel
      "Hosea frequently names Ephraim, Samaria, Joseph, Jezreel"
    • II. JEROME TO PAULINUS. — Amos speaks to the fat cows on the mountain of Samaria
      "He speaks to the fat cows that are on the mountain of Samaria"
