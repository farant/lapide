Bethlehem

Description
Site of St. Jerome's monastic retreat; Lapide found its equivalent in Rome
Coordinates
31.7054, 35.2024

City in Palestine, site of St. Jerome's monastic retreat where he pursued his scriptural labors. Lapide invoked Jerome's Bethlehem as a metaphor for his own life of solitary study in Rome: "whose holy Bethlehem, so earnestly sought by him in Palestine, I have found here in Rome."
