Jerusalem

Description
Holy city; destination of St. Lietbert's pilgrimage with three thousand men
Coordinates
31.7683, 35.2137

The Holy City and destination of pilgrimage. St. Lietbert, Bishop of Cambrai, departed for Jerusalem drawing with him three thousand men who accompanied him on the journey.
