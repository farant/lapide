Pontus

Description
Region in northeastern Asia Minor; St. Basil journeyed through its cities and countryside
Coordinates
40.5, 36.5

Region in northeastern Asia Minor (modern northern Turkey). St. Basil journeyed through the cities and countryside of Pontus, rousing the people to the faith with his words.

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P22 — Basil journeyed through Pontus to rouse the people
    "journeying through the cities and countryside of Pontus, began to rouse with his words the sluggish minds of that people"
  • Proemium Et Encomium Sacrae Scripturae — The Septuagint P3 — Aquila of Pontus was the second interpreter of Sacred Scripture from Hebrew into Greek after the Seventy
    "The second interpreter of Sacred Scripture from Hebrew after the Seventy, according to Epiphanius, was Aquila of Pontus"
