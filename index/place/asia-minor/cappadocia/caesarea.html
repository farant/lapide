Caesarea in Cappadocia

Also known as
Caesarea Cappadociae
Description
Seat of St. Basil's bishopric in Cappadocia; through its single church he "gave light to the whole world"
Coordinates
38.7312, 35.4787

City in Cappadocia (Asia Minor) and seat of St. Basil the Great's bishopric. Gregory Nazianzen said of Basil that "through the single Church of Caesarea, gave light to the whole world." Basil was ordained priest there by Bishop Hermogenes, and later reluctantly became bishop himself after the death of Eusebius of Caesarea.
