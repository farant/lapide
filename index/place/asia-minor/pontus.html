Pontus

Description
Region in Asia Minor where St. Basil preached and transformed the province
Coordinates
40.5, 37

Region in northern Asia Minor (modern Turkey). St. Basil preached throughout its cities and countryside, transforming the whole province by rousing the people to build monasteries, devote themselves to psalms and prayers, and care for the poor.
