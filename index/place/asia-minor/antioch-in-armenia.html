    Antioch in Armenia

    Description
    City in Armenia; seat of St. Macarius as Archbishop
    Coordinates
    39.92, 43.05
    © Esri, Maxar, Earthstar Geographics

    City in Armenia, not to be confused with Antioch on the Orontes. St. Macarius served as Archbishop of Antioch in Armenia. His relics were later sent to Mons during a plague.
