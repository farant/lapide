    Lampsacus

    Description
    Ancient Greek city on the Hellespont in Asia Minor; birthplace of the philosopher Strato
    Coordinates

    Ancient Greek city on the Hellespont in Asia Minor. Birthplace of the philosopher Strato, who held the erroneous view that the world was ungenerated and had existed from eternity.

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • He Created — Strato of Lampsacus named as holding the error that the world was ungenerated and eternal
