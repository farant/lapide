Flanders

Description
County in the Low Countries; Van der Burch's grandfather was President of its Council
Coordinates
51.05, 3.73

County in the Low Countries (modern Belgium). Van der Burch's grandfather served as President of the Council of Flanders. Ghent is called "the jewel of Flanders."
