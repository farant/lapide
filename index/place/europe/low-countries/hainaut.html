    Hainaut

    Also known as
    County of Hainault
    Description
    Historical county in the Low Countries; under Van der Burch's spiritual care
    Coordinates

    Historical county in the Low Countries, under the spiritual care of the Archbishop of Cambrai. The region suffered plague and began to flourish in the Christian faith under Bishop Authbert. Saints Ghislain, Vincent Madelgarius, and Waldetrudis are associated with this region.
