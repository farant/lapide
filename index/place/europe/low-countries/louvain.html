Louvain

Also known as
Leuven
Description
University city in Brabant where Cornelius a Lapide taught Sacred Scripture for over twenty years
Coordinates
50.8798, 4.7005

University city in the Duchy of Brabant (modern Belgium). Cornelius a Lapide taught Sacred Scripture publicly at Louvain for more than twenty years before being summoned to Rome. Near the shrine of Our Lady of Aspromont, where Lapide narrowly escaped martyrdom in 1604.
