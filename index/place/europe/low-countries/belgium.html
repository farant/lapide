Belgium

Also known as
Low Countries, Southern Netherlands, Spanish Netherlands
Description
Region of the Low Countries / Southern Netherlands, used interchangeably with "Belgium" in the text
Coordinates
50.8503, 4.3517

In the text, "Belgium" refers to the Southern Netherlands (Spanish Netherlands), roughly corresponding to modern Belgium. Used interchangeably with "the Low Countries." The dedicatory letter calls Van der Burch "our Moses of the Low Countries" and declares "Belgium is the jewel of the world."
