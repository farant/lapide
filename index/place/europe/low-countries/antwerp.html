Antwerp

Also known as
Antwerpen
Description
Major port city in the Low Countries; center of printing and publishing for Lapide's commentaries
Coordinates
51.2194, 4.4025

Major port city in the Low Countries (modern Belgium) and center of printing and publishing. Multiple works of Cornelius a Lapide were first published at Antwerp by the Nutius and Moretus printing house. The Provincial Superior Scribani and censor Spitholdius issued their permissions there.
