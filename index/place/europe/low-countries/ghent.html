Ghent

Description
Major city in Flanders; seat of a diocese in the Low Countries, birthplace of Emperor Charles V
Coordinates
51.0543, 3.7174

Major city in the County of Flanders (modern Belgium), seat of a diocese. Van der Burch served as Bishop of Ghent before his elevation to the archbishopric of Cambrai. Described as "the jewel of Flanders and Belgium" and renowned as the birthplace of Emperor Charles V. Van der Burch's departure caused deep mourning among its people.
