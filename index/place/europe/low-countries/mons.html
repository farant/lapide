Mons

Description
City in the County of Hainaut; afflicted by plague and relieved through the relics of St. Macarius
Coordinates
50.4542, 3.9566

City in the County of Hainaut in the Low Countries (modern Belgium). Afflicted by a severe plague that carried off thousands, relieved when Van der Burch sent the relics of St. Macarius, Archbishop of Antioch. The people erected a silver reliquary in thanksgiving.
