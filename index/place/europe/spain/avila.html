    City in Castile, Spain. Seat of Bishop Alfonso Tostado, who composed extensive commentaries on the Decateuch and Old Testament historical books.

    • Saintly Examples of Scripture Study — Blessed Alfonso Tostado, Bishop of Avila, composed individual volumes on the Decateuch and Old Testament books
