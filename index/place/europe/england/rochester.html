Rochester

Description
Seat of a bishopric in England held by St. John Fisher, the martyred bishop
Coordinates
51.3884, 0.5068

City in England and seat of a bishopric. St. John Fisher was raised to the bishopric of Rochester for his learning and innocence of life. When Henry VIII offered him a grander see, Fisher refused to abandon his modest bride, declaring he would count himself blessed to render a proper account for even this small flock.
