    Amiens

    Description
    City in northern France; diocese of Augustin Crampon, annotator of Lapide's commentaries
    Coordinates
    49.8941, 2.2958
    © Esri, Maxar, Earthstar Geographics

    City in northern France, seat of the diocese of Amiens. Augustin Crampon, who annotated Lapide's commentaries, was a priest of this diocese. The Bishop of Amiens approved Crampon's annotations in 1852.

    Preliminaries

    • Title Page — Crampon described as a priest of the Diocese of Amiens
      "illustrated with notes by Augustinus Crampon, Priest of the Diocese of Amiens"
    • Permissions P4 — Crampon's annotations approved by the Bishop of Amiens
      "Annotations by which Aug. Crampon, priest of the diocese of Amiens, has illustrated and enriched Father Cornelius a Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch. Nothing hinders their printing. Given at Amiens, 2 May in the year 1852."
