Amiens

Description
Seat of a diocese in northern France; Crampon served as priest there
Coordinates
49.8941, 2.2958

City in northern France and seat of a diocese. Augustin Crampon, who annotated Lapide's commentaries, was a priest of the diocese of Amiens. Bishop Jacobus Antonius approved Crampon's annotations there.
