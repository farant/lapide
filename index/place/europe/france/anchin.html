Anchin

Description
Site of an abbey in Flanders where St. Odo of Cambrai spent his exile and died
Coordinates
50.3686, 3.0808

Site of an abbey in Flanders (modern northern France). St. Odo, Bishop of Cambrai, was driven from his see by Emperor Henry IV and spent the rest of his life in exile at Anchin, where he died.
