Anchin

Also known as

Description
Benedictine abbey near Douai in northern France; place of exile and death of St. Odo of Cambrai

Benedictine abbey near Douai in northern France. St. Odo, Bishop of Cambrai, spent the rest of his life in exile here and died at the abbey.
