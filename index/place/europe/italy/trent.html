Trent

Description
City in northern Italy; site of the Council of Trent (1545-1563)
Coordinates
46.0748, 11.1217

City in northern Italy, site of the ecumenical Council of Trent (1545-1563). The Council's decrees on canonical Scripture and the authenticity of the Vulgate edition are reproduced in the Preliminares and form the theological foundation for Lapide's commentary enterprise.
