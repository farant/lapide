Athens

Description
Center of learning in ancient Greece where Basil and Gregory Nazianzen studied
Coordinates
37.9838, 23.7275

Center of learning in ancient Greece. Saints Basil the Great and Gregory Nazianzen studied eloquence and philosophy there before devoting thirteen years to reading and meditating upon Moses and the Sacred Scriptures.
