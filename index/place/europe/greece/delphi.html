    Sacred city in central Greece, site of the oracle of Apollo. In the chronology of Moses, the tomb of Dionysius (Liber) is said to be at Delphi near the golden Apollo.

    Preface and Praise of Sacred Scripture

    • Praises of Moses from Scripture and the Fathers — The tomb of Dionysius (Liber) is said to be at Delphi near the golden Apollo
      "In the 290th year Perseus killed Dionysius, that is Liber, whose tomb they say is at Delphi near the golden Apollo"
