1616

Events

  • Mutius Vitelleschi, Jesuit Superior General, granted permission for Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch, 9 January
  • Charles Scribani, Provincial Superior, granted permission for printing at Antwerp, 23 August
  • First publication of Lapide's Commentaries on the Pentateuch of Moses at Antwerp
