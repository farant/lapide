    1592

    Events

    • Cornelius a Lapide entered the Society of Jesus, 8 July
    • Pope Clement VIII issued the bull Cum Sacrorum on the Vulgate, 9 November

    Preliminaries

    • THE LIFE OF CORNELIUS A LAPIDE. — Lapide's entry into the Jesuits
      "As a young man he entered the Society of Jesus on 8 July in the year of salvation 1592"
    • POPE CLEMENT VIII. FOR A PERPETUAL MEMORIAL OF THE MATTER. — Dating of the papal bull
      "Given at Rome, at St. Peter's, under the Fisherman's Ring, on the 9th day of November 1592, in the first year of Our Pontificate."
