303

Events

  • St. Lucian, a priest and martyr, undertook a new (seventh) edition of the Old Testament from Hebrew into Greek under Emperor Diocletian
  • Maximian and Diocletian ordered by edict that the books of Moses and other Sacred Scripture be surrendered for burning; many faithful resisted and obtained the laurel of martyrdom
