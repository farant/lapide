    1,656 years (Adam to the Flood)

    Events

    • The elapsed time from Adam (creation of the world) to the Flood, according to Lapide's chronology of Genesis

    Chapter I (The Six Days of Creation)

    • Introduction — First figure in the patriarchal chronology
      "From Adam to the flood there elapsed 1,656 years."
