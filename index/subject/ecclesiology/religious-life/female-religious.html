Female Religious Communities

Description
Convents of women religious and the bishop's duty to visit, reform, and direct them

Van der Burch's care for women's monasteries throughout the archdiocese of Mechlin and diocese of Ghent is noted: they have been "frequently visited, reformed, built up, and directed by you with holy ordinances." Basil also attests in his Epistle 63 that women in the East had "emulating the same pursuit, happily attained an equal rule of life" as monks.
