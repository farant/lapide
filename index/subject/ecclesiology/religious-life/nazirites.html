    Nazirites

    Description
    The Old Testament institution of Nazirites (Numbers 6) as a typological precursor to Christian religious life and monasticism

    The Old Testament institution of Nazirites (Numbers 6) as a typological precursor to Christian religious life and monasticism. Moses instituted the Nazirites and dictated their laws; Basil is called "the Moses of cenobitic monks" by analogy.
