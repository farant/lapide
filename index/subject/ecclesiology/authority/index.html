Authority

  • Excommunication — The penalty of major excommunication imposed automatically (ipso facto) for unauthorized printing or alteration of the Vulgate text
  • Imprimatur and Ecclesiastical Censorship — The system of ecclesiastical approval required before printing sacred books, including the role of censors, ordinaries, and religious superiors, as decreed by Trent and enforced by papal bulls
  • Lay Investiture Controversy — The conflict over whether secular rulers could confer ecclesiastical insignia (staff and ring) on bishops — a key issue of ecclesial independence
