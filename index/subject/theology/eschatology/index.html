    Eschatology

    • Hell and Damnation — The creation of hell on the first day after the fall of the angels, prepared in the center of the earth as a prison with fire and brimstone for the fallen angels
    • Purgatory and Prayers for the Dead — The doctrine of Purgatory and prayers for the dead, established from the books of Maccabees, which Reformers struck from the canon because they could not refute this teaching
