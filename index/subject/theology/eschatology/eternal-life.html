Eternal Life and Heavenly Glory

Description
The blessed eternity toward which all pastoral and scholarly labour is directed; union with God in heaven as the final end

Heavenly glory is presented as both the motive and the reward of all earthly labour. The dedicatory letter promises Van der Burch that he will lead his people "into the land of the living and of those who triumph in heaven," and closes with Daniel 12:3. In the biography, Lapide is consumed by contemplation of blessed eternity, roused to "contempt for human things and desire for heavenly things."
