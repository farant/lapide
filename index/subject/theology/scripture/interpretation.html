Authority of Interpretation

Description
The exclusive right of the Church to judge the true sense and interpretation of Scripture; private interpretation contrary to the Church's teaching or the unanimous consent of the Fathers is forbidden

Trent decreed that no one, relying on his own prudence, may interpret Scripture contrary to the sense held by holy mother Church or contrary to the unanimous consent of the Fathers. This assertion of interpretive authority undergirds the entire commentary tradition to which Lapide's work belongs.
