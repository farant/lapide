Resurrection of the Body

Description
The bodily resurrection prophesied in Job, who testifies that he will rise from the earth on the last day and see God in his flesh -- the clearest prophecy of bodily resurrection in the Old Testament

The bodily resurrection prophesied in Job, who testifies that he will rise from the earth on the last day and see God in his flesh -- the clearest and most cautious prophecy of bodily resurrection in the Old Testament.

References in Commentary

  • Clemens Hieronymi Du Culte — Jerome To Paulinus P15 — Jerome praises Job as the model of patience whose book prophesies the resurrection of bodies more clearly and cautiously than any other.
    "it so prophesies the resurrection of bodies that no one has written of it either more clearly or more cautiously."
