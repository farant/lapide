The Gospel as Source of Saving Truth

Description
The Gospel promised through the prophets, promulgated by Christ, and preached by the Apostles as the fountain of all saving truth and moral discipline

The opening declaration of the Trent Session IV decree identifies the Gospel as the single fountain from which all saving truth and moral discipline flow. This Gospel was first promised through the prophets, then promulgated by Christ Himself, and finally commanded to be preached to every creature by His Apostles.
