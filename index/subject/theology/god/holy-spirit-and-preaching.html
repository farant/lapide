    Holy Spirit and Preaching

    Description
    The Holy Spirit's role in inspiring and guiding preaching — symbolized by the dove whispering sermons to the saints

    The Holy Spirit's role in inspiring and guiding preaching — symbolized by the dove whispering sermons to the saints. While Basil was preaching, Ephrem saw a dove whispering the sermon into his ear — "a dove, which is the sign and hieroglyph of the Holy Spirit."

      "Saint Ephrem saw a dove whispering the sermon into his ear — a dove, I say, which is the sign and hieroglyph of the Holy Spirit"
