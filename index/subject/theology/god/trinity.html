The Holy Trinity

Description
The triune God -- "most holy Trinity and threefold Unity" -- as the ultimate end of all study and labour

In his personal devotional writings preserved in the biography, Lapide consecrates all his scholarly labours to the glory of the Trinity and gives himself over to the triune God to "receive, ponder, and celebrate His oracles and inspirations." The Trinity is the final destination toward which all commentary and learning point.
