    Pilgrimage

    Description
    The practice of religious pilgrimage to holy sites, undertaken as an act of piety

    The practice of religious pilgrimage to holy sites, undertaken as an act of piety. St. Lietbert of Cambrai departed for Jerusalem and "drew with him three thousand men, who accompanied him on pilgrimage."
