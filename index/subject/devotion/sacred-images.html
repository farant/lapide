Sacred Images

Description
The use and veneration of sacred images, including the miraculous image of Our Lady before which St. Basil prayed

Basil is recorded as praying before an image of Our Lady on which the celebrated martyr Mercury was also depicted. Through this image, Basil received a vision: "he saw the martyr at first dim and obscure, but not long afterwards holding a bloodied spear," foretelling the death of Julian the Apostate. The source is St. John Damascene's first Oration on Images, making this passage both a devotional exemplum and a doctrinal witness for the veneration of images.
