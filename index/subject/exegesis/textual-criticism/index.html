Textual Criticism

  • Editorial History of the Vulgate — The successive editorial efforts under Popes Pius IV through Clement VIII to restore the Vulgate to its original purity through comparison of ancient manuscripts, Hebrew and Greek sources, and patristic commentaries
  • The Old Latin (Italic) Version — The ancient Latin translation of the Bible predating Jerome, called "Italic" by Augustine, "Common and Vulgate" by Jerome, and "Old translation" by Gregory
