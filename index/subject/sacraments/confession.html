Confession (Sacrament)

Description
The sacrament of penance — hearing confessions as a priestly ministry

The sacrament of penance — hearing confessions as a priestly ministry. Lapide was summoned by Van der Burch as "a confessor for the more solemn feasts of the year"; Van der Burch himself devoted himself "to hearing confessions."

References in Commentary

  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P3 — Lapide was summoned by Van der Burch as "a confessor for the more solemn feasts of the year."
    "summoned by you from Mechlin to the Metropolitan Church over which you presided as Dean — as a confessor for the more solemn feasts of the year"
  • Preliminares — Dedicatory Letter P15 — Van der Burch devoted himself "to hearing confessions" as part of his priestly duties.
    "by devoting yourself to hearing confessions"
